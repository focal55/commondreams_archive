Common Dreams Action Alert - May 1, 1999

Today the people of Colorado - including hundreds of Columbine High school students - are rallying in Denver saying 'SHAME on the NRA'.

Folksinger Cheryl Wheeler is performing at the rally - singing her powerful new single, released by Rounder Records, ‘If It Were Up To Me’ . Wheeler wrote the song in the wake of past high school shootings.

Many radio stations across the country are playing the song in response to the Littleton tragedy. Colorado radio stations are so swamped with requests that they are playing the song every 2 hours.

Rounder Records has pledged a $5.00 donation to The Center to Prevent Handgun Violence every time the record is played on one of the following radio stations.

We're writing to ask that you call the radio station nearest  you and request that they play ‘If It Were Up To Me’ .

Please Make The Call and help The Center to Prevent Handgun Violence to reduce senseless firearms violence. 

Thanks,
Craig Brown
for Common Dreams NewsCenter
