Corporate Greed Infects Downtown Washington
Quarintine Prescribed to Protect People from IMF/World Bank & Corporate CEOs
“Globalization’s Discontents” to Announce Plans to Protest Final Meeting of IMF & World Bank
 

WASHINGTON - August 29 - Activists for corporate accountability and global justice will hold a press conference on Tuesday September 3rd to describe their plans and discuss the reasons to “quarantine” finance ministers and business executives gathering in Washington at the end of September.

"This will be the last time the IMF and World Bank hold their annual meetings in Washington," said Robert Weissman of the Mobilization for Global Justice. "We will quarantine these carriers of corporate greed to stop them before they improvish, pollute and destroy again."

Remedies for isolating these bodies and rendering them harmless will include non-violent direct actions, demonstrations, teach-ins, vigils, and debates before and during the annual meetings of the Boards of the International Monetary Fund (IMF) and World Bank.
