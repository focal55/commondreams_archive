Common Dreams Action Alert - May 1,1999
Help fight gun violence - request radio stations play 'If It Were Up To Me'

click here for more details
*****************************

To support the growing movement for gun control, Rounder Records and Cheryl Wheeler are giving away her poignant song, "If It Were Up to Me" as a free Liquid Audio digital download. We hope you enjoy this timely, powerful song. To listen to "If It Were Up to Me" , you need to download Liquid Music Plaver for free. After installing Liquid Audio, click on the Liquid Audio Souund sample or click here.

                

Since first releasing "If It Were Up to Me" to radio stations in January, Rounder Records has used Cheryl Wheeler's powerful song to raise money for and draw attention to the campaign against handgun violence.

Rounder Records pledged $5 to the Center to Prevent Handgun Violence each time AAA radio stations played the song. Rounder is also donating a portion of the proceeds from Cheryl's album, Sylvia Hotel, to the Center. Just last week, Cheryl Wheeler and Rounder presented an initial check for $1,200 to the Center to Prevent Handgun Violence in Washington, D.C.

Sadly, the mass shooting in Colorado has returned this tragic issue to the headlines once again. Many radio stations across the country are playing the song in response to the tragedy, including KACD in Los Angeles and KBCO in Boulder, Colorado.

KBCO received such a strong response from their listeners that they have been playing the song every two hours.
