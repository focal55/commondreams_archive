OCTOBER 16, 2001
9:33 AM
 CONTACT:  Commercial Alert
 Gary Ruskin 503.235.8012
Take part in the National Ad Slam Contest!
 
WASHINGTON - October 16 - Commercial Alert today launched the first annual National Ad Slam Contest. The Ad Slam is a nationwide contest to expel advertisers from schools. We will award a $5,000 prize to the school that makes the best and most creative effort to remove advertising and commercialism from school premises during the 2001-2 school year. We will award other prizes for creativity, media coverage, and teamwork. If you participate with thousands of other activists across the country, we will all win.

In recent years, corporate marketers have invaded the schools. Their ads waste valuable school time, corrupt textbooks and curricula with marketing, promote junk food and soda pop despite skyrocketing levels of childhood obesity, and peddle boorish, violent and anti-social entertainment.

"The Ad Slam is a civic contest to throw the hucksters out of school," said Gary Ruskin, executive director of Commercial Alert. "We're going to have plenty of fun as we rid the schools of Channel One, Coke, Pepsi and the other marketers."

Anyone in any school community can participate. Here is how you can get involved:

1) Send us an email to tell us that you want to participate. Our email is alert@essential.org.

2) Visit our website for instructions for teachers, parents, and students, at http://www.commercialalert.org/adslam/index.html. If you can't download the instructions, please email us at alert@essential.org or call 503.235.8012, and we will fax, mail or email them to you.

3) Send this email to your friends and family members, teachers and students.

4) Ask your favorite organizations to endorse the contest. To endorse the contest, send an email to alert@essential.org.

5) Make a contribution to the Ad Slam Contest prize fund. Visit this web page for details: http://www.commercialalert.org/jointoday.html. 6) Learn more about commercialism. Visit our website at http://www.commercialalert.org.

The National Ad Slam Contest is endorsed by the National Council of the Churches of Christ of the USA, Adbusters Media Foundation, Bioneers, Center for a New American Dream, Center for Media & Democracy, Center for Media Education, Center for Science in the Public Interest, Citizens' Campaign for Commercial-Free Schools, Citizens for Media Literacy, Commercialism in Education Research Unit, Consumers Union, Junkbusters, Motherhood Project of the Institute for American Values, Mothering Magazine, New Mexico Media Literacy Project, Obligation, Organic Consumers Association, Public Citizen, Rethinking Schools, Seeds of Simplicity and the TV-Turnoff Network.

Commercial Alert's mission is to keep the commercial culture within its proper sphere, and to prevent it from exploiting children and subverting the higher values of family, community, environmental integrity and democracy.

Commercial Alert's materials are distributed electronically via our mailing list <commercial-alert@lists.essential.org>. To subscribe, send the word "subscribe" to alert@essential.org, or go to http://lists.essential.org/mailman/listinfo/commercial-alert.
