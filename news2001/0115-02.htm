Unprecedented Coalition To Speak Out Nationwide Urging Senate Not To Confirm John Ashcroft As United States Attoreny General
 
WASHINGTON - January 15 - An unprecedented, broad-based coalition of organizations opposed to the confirmation of John Ashcroft as U.S. Attorney General John Ashcroft will hold nationwide press conferences. The coalition is urging Senate Judiciary Committee Members to withhold any commitment to vote for confirmation of Ashcroft until his record has been fully review and debated in the Senate. Coalition members will hold press conferences in Missouri, Illinois, Wisconsin, California, Georgia, Connecticut, Massachusetts, Rhode Island and Pennsylvania.

The coalition consists of a wide variety of groups including those representing civil rights, gun control advocates, labor, women's rights, and pro-choice organizations. Representatives from these groups will appear at the press conferences listed below.

For more information on John Ashcroft's record during his years in the Senate and Missouri and the full list of the coalition partners visit http://www.opposeashcroft.com.
