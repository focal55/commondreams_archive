 CONTACT:  Fair Taxes For All Coalition

Rallies & Ads Nationwide Urge Congress To Oppose Irresponsible & Unfair Tax Cuts
 
WASHINGTON - April 10 - Americans are joining forces across the country to send a strong message to their U.S. Senators and Representatives: The tax cut proposed by the Bush Administration is too big, too risky, and too irresponsible and it ignores the will of the people.

Nationwide Rallies

On Wednesday, April 11, members of the Fair Taxes For All coalition will hold rallies in 42 cities in 27 states to call for responsible and fair taxes that put the people's priorities such as Social Security and Medicare first.

Multi-State Ad Campaign Launched

Beginning on Wednesday, April 11, radio ads calling for Fair Taxes For All will begin airing in key states.

News Conference

What:
Leaders of Fair Taxes For All unveil the ads, lay out details of the nationwide actions, and release state-by-state analysis of the impact of the proposed tax and budget plans

When:
Wednesday, April 11, 10 am

Where:
National Press Club, Murrow Room

Who:
-- Gerald W. McEntee, American Federation of State, County & Municipal Employees
-- Ralph G. Neas, People For the American Way
-- Nancy Duff Campbell, National Women's Law Center
-- Hilary Shelton, NAACP
