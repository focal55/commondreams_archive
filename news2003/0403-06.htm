Rebuilding Homes, Israeli Committee Against House Demolitions Announce Rapid Response Rebuilding Program;
16 New Demolitions in Jerusalem This Morning -- Rebuilding Begins on Saturday
 
REDWOOD CITY, CA - April 3 - Rebuilding Homes, an organization which works with both Israeli and Palestinian citizens to rebuild Palestinian homes demolished by Israeli Defense Forces, today announced the launch of its Rapid Response Rebuilding Program in cooperation with the Israeli Committee Against House Demolitions (ICAHD). The announcement comes on the heels of 16 new demolitions in the Jerusalem area this morning, including the home of Salim Shawamreh, an ICAHD spokesperson.

"Though I've never been charged with any crime, this is the fourth time my home is demolished by the IDF," said Shawamreh. "Nevertheless, we will keep rebuilding to let the world know that our families have a right to live on the land we own. With help from all who care, we will start rebuilding as fast as they demolish. We refuse to be enemies."

With the 90 Palestinian homes demolished in the past 3 months, the Rapid Response Rebuilding Program is designed to bring world-wide attention to Israel's policy of demolishing Palestinian homes.

"Only a small percentage of demolitions are related to Israeli security-related issues," said Donna Baranski-Walker, Campaign Manager of Rebuilding Homes. "The practice is designed to break the Palestinian spirit and family structure. The Rapid Response Rebuilding Program asks people worldwide to support rebuilding to encourage Palestinians and Israelis who are working together, nonviolently and constructively, for a just peace. We are mobilizing now to begin rebuilding Mr. Shawamreh's home and clear debris from 5 other homesites this Saturday."

In each rebuilding effort, Rebuilding Homes, ICAHD, and Palestinian partner the Jerusalem Center for Social and Economic Rights, recruits Israelis to work hand-in-hand with Palestinians to foster solidarity between the two peoples. "Of course our goal is to rebuild homes," said

Fred Schlomka, ICAHD Operations Manager, "But we're mainly a political organization. Each rebuilding day brings both sides to work together, eat together, and plan next steps. With worldwide support, we'll stop the bulldozers and build momentum for a just peace."

About Rebuilding Homes Rebuilding Homes, founded in June 2002, is a 3-way Palestinian-Israeli-American partnership dedicated to educating the world about Palestinian house demolitions and bringing Israelis, Palestinians, and internationals together in constructive resistance against the Israeli Occupation. For more information please visit the Web site at www.rebuildinghomes.org
