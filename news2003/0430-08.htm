Public Interest Groups Join Sen. Cantwell in Urging Senate to Keep Priscilla Owen off Federal Appeals Court
 
WASHINGTON - April 30 - News Advisory:

1 p.m. Press Conference to Follow Cloture Vote Scheduled for Thursday Morning

Sen. Maria Cantwell will join leaders of Texas and national public interest groups in a press conference to follow the scheduled cloture vote on the U.S. appeals court nomination of Texas Supreme Court Justice Priscilla Owen, a right-wing judicial activist with a record hostile to civil rights, reproductive choice, and the interests of individual workers and consumers. Owen's record has not changed since her nomination was rejected by the Senate Judiciary Committee last year, and she must be stopped again. Senator Cantwell and the groups will discuss the need to remain vigilant throughout this filibuster.

WHAT: Press conference on Priscilla Owen nomination and cloture vote
