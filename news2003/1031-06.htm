Danny Glover and Rep. Barbara Lee to Lead Events for Kucinich
 
WASHINGTON - October 31 - Noted movie star, film producer, activist, United Nations Goodwill Ambassador, and Kucinich endorser Danny Glover has scheduled events on Nov. 2 and Nov. 23 to help promote Dennis Kucinich's presidential campaign.

Congresswoman Barbara Lee (D., Calif.), who co-chairs the Congressional Progressive Caucus, the largest caucus in Congress, with Congressman Kucinich, will lead a Town Hall Forum with Kucinich on Nov. 2.

November 2nd in Oakland, Calif.:

3:00 to 4:30 pm: Town Hall Forum with Kucinich and Lee. Local radio personality Ray Taliaferro will MC.

Taylor Memorial Methodist Church, 1188 12th Street.

5:00 to 8:00 pm: Kucinich Fundraising Dinner with Kucinich, Lee, and Glover.

Zazoo restaurant, 15 Embarcadero West, Jack London Square.

7:00 to 9:00 pm: Kucinich Celebration with Kucinich and supporters.

Java House, 3306 Lakeshore.

November 23rd in Los Angeles, Calif.:

1:00 p.m. Town Hall Meeting with Kucinich and Glover

Belvedere Junior High School Auditorium, 312 N Record Ave, Just South of Cesar Chavez in East Los Angeles.

Kucinich’s expanding grassroots campaign is opening a new office in East Los Angeles with a Halloween fundraising party tonight. For more information call 323-260-4895.

For Rep. Kucinich's Schedule: http://www.kucinich.us/schedule.htm.

Oakland Contact: Lora O’Connor, 415-215-9353, lora@kucinich.us
