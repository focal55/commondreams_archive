‘Take Back America’ Conference Taps Into Progressives’ Indignation
Over 1000 Activists Will Join Conference June 4-6;
Seven Democratic Presidential Candidates to Address Conference
 
WASHINGTON - June 2 - Activists from across the country who oppose President Bush’s tax cuts for millionaires, his weakening of environmental laws, and his undermining of rights for women, labor and minorities will gather in Washington for three days of meetings on a common strategy to counter conservatives with a positive program for good jobs and help for working families. Organized by the Campaign for America’s Future and the Institute for America’s Future, the three-day conference to ‘Take Back America,’ June 4-6, will address the core issues that effect most Americans and empower them to take control of the nation’s destiny again. It is organized with the help of many partner organizations.

Highlights of the conference will include a session on online organizing led by Wes Boyd of MoveOn.org, the launch of a new Apollo Project for Energy Independence and Jobs, the release of a poll conducted for the United Steelworkers of America in 9 key swing states from America’s heartland showing widespread, bi-partisan support for bold federal action to support new manufacturing jobs (June 5) and a ‘Millionaire March and Rally for Bush and Cheney’ (June 6) sponsored by USAction with more than 200 activists dressed in top hats and tiaras marching to the Vice Present’s residence to wholeheartedly thank the Administration for the latest tax cut.

What: Three Day National Progressive Conference

When: June 4 – 6, 2003

Where: Omni Shoreham Hotel 2500 Calvert St, NW, Washington, DC

Who: Confirmed: Sen. John Kerry, former Vermont Gov. Howard Dean, Sen. John Edwards, Robert Borosage, John Sweeney, Rep. Jan Schakowsky, Rev. Jesse Jackson, Ralph Neas, Robert Reich, Carl Pope, Sen. Jon Corzine and many more.

Dinner: June 4 awards dinner to honor Bill Moyers, Regena Thomas, Maria Elena Durazo.

For more information about the conference agenda and schedule please visit: http://www.ourfuture.org/projects/national_conference/index.cfm
