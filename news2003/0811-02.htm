Has This Administration Shredded the 1st Amendment?
 
WASHINGTON - August 10 - On Tuesday, August 11-Join Thom and guest Brett Bursey who was arrested for protesting outside “A Free Speech Zone”

Network Director of South Carolina Progressive Network, Brett Bursey faces federal charges for threatening the president with a "No War for Oil" sign at a Republican rally last October. Bursey was originally arrested for refusing to go to the "free speech zone" and charged with trespassing. Five months after the arrest, the state dropped the charges and the US Justice Department brought charges under the “threatening the president” statute. Bursey faces prison and fines if convicted.
