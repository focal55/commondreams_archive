1500 Israeli and Palestinian Leaders Support Effort to Allow People from the Region to Negotiate Concrete Plan to End Conflict
 
NEW YORK - May 12 - The son of the newly appointed Palestinian Prime Minister he Chief Islamic Justice of Palestine, Israeli Knesset and Cabinet Members, and Palestinian PLC and Cabinet Members are among the 1500 Israelis and Palestinians who are supporting a new grassroots effort to seize the agenda from violent extremists. They have joined OneVoice, a project that uses the Internet and other means to amplify the voice of Israeli and Palestinian moderates. People from both sides won't just be signing petitions, they will actually negotiate a concrete plan-the "People's Road Map"-to end the conflict.

Renewed international attention has magnified the need for moderates to take an active stand. On May 13, the heads of the World Jewish Congress and the American Sufi Muslim Association will take the lead by jointly signing a proclamation that acknowledges the right of both Israelis and Palestinians to live with freedom, security, dignity, respect and self-determination. OneVoice will then engage thousands to support the proclamation and join an interactive negotiation process to achieve consensus on ten critical issues. People will participate via the Internet, print ads, a confidential toll-free number, and other venues.

The OneVoice initiative has steadily gained the backing of a growing list of U.S. political leaders, businesspeople, spiritual leaders, and entertainers, including such diverse figures as Stuart Eizenstat (former Deputy Secretary of Treasury), James Zogby (president of the Arab American Institute), Edgar Bronfman (president of the World Jewish Congress), and actors Rhea Perlman, Danny Devito, Brad Pitt, Jennifer Aniston, and many, many others. In addition, the IBM International Foundation is backing the effort and developing the internet-based interactive negotiation platform.

Founders Daniel Lubetzky and Mohammad Darawshe are available for interview from New York. To schedule, please contact Jason Dring at 202-518-8047 or Jason(At)publicinterestpr.com

Daniel Lubetzky is the Founder of PeaceWorks Holdings LLC, a business corporation pursuing both peace and profits. In 2002 he founded the PeaceWorks Foundation to search for creative solutions to the Middle East crisis.

Mohammad Darawshe is Regional Director and Chairman of the OneVoice Steering Committee. He is a Palestinian Citizen of Israel. A highly respected expert in Arab-Israeli relations, Darawshe is a political strategist for the United Arab Party of the Knesset and a foreign policy consultant for governments working with the Middle East.

OneVoice is a global undertaking to amplify the voice of moderates, empower Palestinians and Israelis at the grass-roots level to seize back the agenda away from violent extremists, and to achieve broad-based consensus on core issues. OneVoice, conceived by the PeaceWorks Foundation, was developed by over two hundred Palestinian, Israeli and international community members.
