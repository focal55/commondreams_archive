Our Readers Asked...
How Do We Rank? 2,651

Over the years we have received thousands of e-mails from our readers asking: How many people read the Common Dreams website daily? Is readership increasing? Decreasing? How many hits monthly? How does Common Dreams compare to other progressive websites?
 

"Well...it's complicated", we would answer - because it was. But, now - thanks to alexa.com - we can share with you how Common Dreams ranks (as of July 1, 2003) compared, for perspective, to a sampling of other websites - some progressive, others obviously not. Alexa uses a combination of the number of 'users' and 'page views' over a 3-month period to calculate website popularity. We're not number 1 - but we're moving up.
