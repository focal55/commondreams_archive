Make Us Your Home Page...

...and get rid of that pre-loaded corporate website that your computer came programmed to open to automatically when you log on to the internet.

To see our site immediately every time you sign on to the Internet, you can easily make us your browser's home page. You can also bookmark the site or create a desktop shortcut. For instructions, click on the link below that matches your browser software.

Netscape 4.0 | Netscape 3.0 | Netscape 2.0 |
Internet Explorer 4.0 | Internet Explorer 5.0 | Internet Explorer 3.0 |
Macintosh Internet Explorer Users | Lynx | America Online 

Note: Our website is designed to work best with Netscape/Internet Explorer versions 4.0 or higher. If you have older browsers, you can upgrade for free at either Netscape or Internet Explorer.

If you have any problems, please e-mail our webmaster and we will try to give you a hand: webmaster(at)commondreams.org

Netscape 4.0

Make commondreams.org Your Home Page

  • Go to the "Edit" menu and choose "Preferences."
  • Select "Navigator" from the Category list on the left.

  • Under "Navigator starts with" check off the "Home Page."
  • Under "Home Page" type the following address in the location field:

    http://www.commondreams.org/

  • Click "OK."

Bookmark commondreams.org
For easy access to commondreams.org, add it to your bookmarks.

  • Go to the commondreams.org home page.
  • Go to the "Bookmarks" menu and choose "Add Bookmark."
  • To go to our home page at any time, click on the "Bookmarks" menu and choose "NewsCenter - News & Views for the Progressive Community"

Put commondreams.org on your Windows 95 Desktop

Note: This is ideal for those with permanent network connections, but may not make sense for those who use modems.

  • Click the following link with your right mouse button:

    commondreams.org

  • In the menu that appears, choose "Create Shortcut." In the "Description" box, type in "commondreams.org." Then click "OK."

This creates a shortcut icon on your desktop. When you need to access commondreams.org, double-click on the icon. If you are using a modem to connect to the Internet, you should first set up the connection. Then double-click on the commondreams.org icon instead of Netscape.

Internet Explorer 3.0

Make commondreams.org Your Home Page

  • Go to the commondreams.org front page.
  • Go to the "View" menu and choose "Options."
  • Click on the "Start and Search Pages" or "Navigation" tab.
  • Make sure the box under "Home Page" reads "Address."
  • Click on the "Use Current" button.
  • Click "Apply."
  • Click "OK."

Add commondreams.org to Your List of "Favorites"

  • Go to the commondreams.org home page.
  • Go to the "Favorites" menu and choose "Add to Favorites."

  • To go to our home page at any time, click on the "Favorites" menu and scroll down to "NewsCenter - News & Views for the Progressive Community"

Add Common Dreams Tools to Your List of "Quick Links"

Quick Links appear at the upper right-hand corner of the Internet Explorer screen. To see your current Quick Links, click on "Links" to the right of the "Address" box. If you like, you can turn these Quick Links into favorite commondreams.org contents. Here's how:

  • Go to your favorite sections, subsections or pages of commondreams.org. For example, go to US Dailies.
  • Go to the "View" menu and choose "Options."
  • Click on either the "Start and Search Pages" or "Navigation" tab.
  • Click on the box next to "Page" and select one of the five quick links (for example, "Quick Link #1").
  • In the "Name" box, type the name you would like to appear on the Quick Link button. Don't touch the "Address" box.
  • Click on the "Use Current" button.
  • Click "Apply."
  • Click "OK."

Put commondreams.org on your Windows 95 Desktop

Note: This is ideal for those with permanent network connections, but may not make sense for those who use modems.

  • First, set up your window so that you can see the Win 95 desktop behind it. The best way to do this is to click the middle button at the upper right-hand corner of your browser window.
  • Click on the following link and hold your finger down:

    commondreams.org

  • Drag your mouse pointer to the Windows 95 desktop and release. An icon will appear there.
  • To rename the link, click it with your right mouse button and choose "Rename." Type "commondreams.org" and hit your "Enter" key.

This creates a shortcut icon on your desktop. When you need to access commondreams.org, double-click on the icon. If you are using a modem to connect to the Internet, you should first set up the connection. Then double-click on the commondreams.org icon instead of the Internet Explorer icon.

Make commondreams.org Your Home Page

  • Go to the "View" menu and choose "Internet Options."
  • Click on the "General" tab.
  • Click on the "Use Current" button.
  • Click "Apply."
  • Click "OK."

Add commondreams.org to Your List of "Favorites"

  • Go to the commondreams.org home page.
  • Go to the "Favorites" menu (not the "Favorites Folder" on the button bar) and choose "Add to Favorites."

  • To go to our home page at any time, click on the "Favorites" menu and scroll down to "NewsCenter - News & Views for the Progressive Community"
  • Alternately, you may drag the icon in the "Address" box to the "Favorites Folder" on the "Button Bar."

Add Common Dreams Tools to Your "Links Bar"

The "Links Bar" by default appears at the upper right-hand corner of the Internet Explorer screen. To see your current Links, click on the "Links Bar," you may have to drag it left or right to see all links. If you like, you can add your favorite commondreams.org links to the "Links Bar." Here's how:

  • Go to your favorite sections, subsections or pages of commondreams.org. For example, go to US Dailies .
  • Notice the world icon in the "Address" box.
  • Simply drag it on to your "Links Bar."
  • When you want to visit this site again, simply locate it on the "Links Bar" and click.
  • To edit or rename the buttons on your "Link Bar" click on the "View" menu, choose "Explorer Bar" choose "Favorites" click on the "Links" folder, locate the web site underneath, use the right mouse button to click on the web site you want to change, choose "Rename," from the pop out menu and rename the site as you want it to appear on the links bar. Click on the "X" in the upper right hand corner of the "Explorer Bar" to close window.

Macintosh Internet Explorer Users

1. While in the Internet Explorer application, click on the "Edit" Menu at the top left of screen.

2. Choose the "Preferences" option from the "Edit" Menu. This will bring up the Preferences Box

3. Choose "Browser Display" on the left side of the Preferences Box.

4. Enter 'http://www.commondreams.org' into the "Address" field on the right middle of the Preferences Box.

5. Click "OK" to exit the Preferences Box. You're home page is now set to CommonDreams.org!

Lynx

Make commondreams.org Your Home Page

  • In your startup file (for example, in your .cshrc file), set the WWW_HOME environment variable to the location:

    http://www.commondreams.org

Bookmark commondreams.org

  • Go to the commondreams.org home page.
  • Press the "a" key to add a bookmark.
  • Press the "d" key to save commondreams.org as a bookmark.
  • To view your bookmarks, press the "v" key. You can choose the commondreams.org bookmark to go to our home page.

America Online

Note: If you plan to use AOL for your Internet connection, we strongly suggest that you upgrade to the latest version. To do this, click on AOL's "Go To" menu and choose "Keyword." Then type in "Upgrade."

Make commondreams.org Your Home Page

  • Go to the commondreams.org front page.
  • Click the following link with your right mouse button and choose "Copy URL to Clipboard."

    commondreams.org
    (If the "copy" option did not appear, drag your mouse over the following Web address and hit your "Control" and "C" keys together:

    http://www.commondreams.org/
  • Go to the "Members" menu and choose "Preferences."
  • Click on the "WWW" icon.
  • Click once on the "Home Page" box at the bottom of the window before you. Then hit your "Control" and "V" keys to paste in the URL. If the commondreams.org address does not appear in the box, simply type in http://www.commondreams.org/
  • Click "OK."

Add commondreams.org To Your "Favorite Places"

  • Go to the commondreams.org front page.
  • At the upper right-hand corner of your browser, you should see a blocky red icon that looks like a heart. Click it once.
  • When AOL asks you if you would like to add commondreams.org to your home page, click on the "Yes" button.

To return to commondreams.org at any time from AOL, click on the "Go To" menu and choose "Favorite Places." Then click twice on the icon labeled "http://www.commondreams.org." You can change the title of the link by clicking it once, followed by the "Modify" button. However, be sure not to change the information in the "Location" field.
