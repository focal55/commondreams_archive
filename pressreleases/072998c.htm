 Union Demands Government Investigation Of Wal-Mart "Buy American" Program As False And Deceptive
CHICAGO - July 29 -

News Advisory:

UNION DEMANDS GOVERNMENT INVESTIGATION OF WAL-MART "BUY AMERICAN" PROGRAM AS FALSE AND DECEPTIVE

WHEN: THURSDAY, JULY 30, 1998 AT NOON

WHERE: CHICAGO - NAVY PIER, FESTIVAL HALL, ROOM 207

WHO: UNION FOOD AND COMMERCIAL WORKERS INTERNATIONAL UNION (UFCW)
AND FOOD AND ALLIED SERVICE TRADES DEPT., AFL-CIO (FAST)

WHAT: The UFCW and FAST will issue a detailed report, which has been sent to every state attorney general and the Federal Trade Commission, documenting some of the false and deceptive aspects of Wal-Mart's much touted "Buy American" program.

The UFCW is holding its national convention this week at the Navy Pier in Chicago.
