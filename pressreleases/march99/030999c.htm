Press Briefing on East Timor
 
NEW YORK - March 9 - Press Briefing on East Timor

United Nations Correspondents Association

3rd floor, United Nations, New York City

Thursday, March 11

11 a.m.

with:

Jose Luis Guterres, Secretary of Foreign Affairs, National Council of Timorese (CNRT)

Joao Carrascalao, President, Union of Democratic Timorese

Mr. Guterres and Mr. Carrascalao will comment on the U.N. negotiations among Portugal, Indonesia and the Secretary-General now taking place concerning Indonesia-occupied East Timor.
