CONTACT: Handgun Control
Statement of Sarah and Jim Brady on the Defeat of Proposition B and the NTA in Missouri
 
WASHINGTON - April 7 - The following was released today by Handgun Control Inc.:

Statement of Sarah and Jim Brady on the mefeat of Proposition B and the NTA in Missouri

"Jim and I are so grateful to Missouri voters for defeating Proposition B and sending the National Rifle Association packing. The magnitude of this loss for the NRA, which has been on an electoral losing streak, cannot be overstated.

"Proposition B was the NRA's to lose and the people of the Show Me State showed them the door. The NRA was handed this stunning loss on the concealed handguns issue despite the fact that they spent at least $3.7 million -- five times what the opponents of Proposition B spent.

"The Missouri vote sends two important messages: first, the people in the heartland of America know in their hearts that guns don't and can't make our homes or communities safer. And, second, even with their millions, the NRA's extreme leadership is out of touch with the public -- especially law enforcement officials and even gunowners.

"Even the NRA's campaign manager declared that Proposition B would be the last great gun battle of the 20th Century. That may be true. But let's hope that in the next millennium, we don't fight these battles, but instead, we achieve common sense solutions that will prevent and reduce gun violence in our nation."
