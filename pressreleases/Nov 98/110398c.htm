Straight Talk on the '98 Election: A Progressive Analysis of Election Results Hosted by the Campaign for America's Future
 
WASHINGTON - November 3 - Join experts Celinda Lake, Guy Molyneux, Ruy Teixeira, Ellen Miller* and Robert Borosage for the progressive perspective on Tuesday's election.

What issues played crucial roles in the vote on Tuesday?  What did voters say about Social Security, education, and the economy?  What does turnout tell us about voters, the impact of the Presidential scandal and impeachment prospects, and the agenda of the two parties?  Did anyone get a mandate on Tuesday - and for what?  How effective was the money edge?  What did voters say when they spoke directly - on referenda and initiatives?

Join us to discuss these and other questions at a media seminar Thursday at the National Press Club, sponsored by the Campaign for America's Future.

For 1998, the Campaign for America's Future created Straight Talk: Common Sense for the New Majority a publication designed to help activists and political leaders reach out to a new progressive majority.

Celinda Lake of Lake Sosin Snell Perry & Associates and Guy Molyneux of Peter D.Hart Research Associates are two of the co-authors of Straight Talk. Ruy Teixeira is the Director of the Politics and Public Opinion Program at the Economic Policy Institute.   Ellen Miller* is the Executive Director of Public Campaign.  Robert Borosage is co-director of the Campaign for America's Future.
