Announcing the New Century Alliance for Social Security
 
WASHINGTON - December 1 - On December 3rd, we are introducing the New Century Alliance for Social Security. This alliance brings together over 160 citizen leaders and policy experts who have signed a Statement of Principles for Social Security's Future. The principles represent the voice of those who want to strengthen, not dismantle, Social Security.

Speakers include:

John Sweeney
AFL-CIO

Hugh Price
National Urban League
