On-Line Petition Started To Protest Human Rights Campaign's Endorsement Of Right-Winger Senator Al D'Amato
 
NEW YORK - October 21 - An Online Peition is started to show Human Rights Campaign (HRC) that Lesbians and Gays and other progressives are not happy with their endorsement of Al D'Amato.

On a weekly basis, the petition will be faxed to HRC so that they know NY's Gay and Lesbian Community is not fooled by Al D'Amato's supposed support of Lesbian and Gay issues. Why didn't Al D'Amato help us pass a Lesbian & Gay civil rights bill? He has power in Albany - where has he been?

To sign the petition, Point your browser to:

http://members.aol.com/fordhmlaw/Petition/
