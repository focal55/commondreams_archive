Center for Responsive Politics Schedules News Conference To Release Comprehensive Study of 1998 Political Fund-Raising
 
WASHINGTON - October 13 - The non-partisan Center for Responsive Politics will release its major, pre-election report on political fund-raising in the 1998 congressional
campaigns at a news conference on Monday, Oct. 19, 1998, at 10 a.m. in the First Amendment Room at the National Press Club.

With the 1998 congressional elections shaping up as the most expensive - and one of the least competitive - ever, the Center will analyze the patterns in political contributions and examine who's paying for this election.

A comprehensive look at the overall fund-raising trends as well as detailed profiles for every congressional race in the nation will be available. Highlights will include a breakdown of political donations by industry, sector, and interest group; listings of top donors; and an examination of the legislative agendas of major contributors.

Guided tours of the Center's Website at www.crp.org will be conducted before the news conference in the First Amendment Room. Information packets for individual states will be available.
