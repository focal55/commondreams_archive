Liberals Meet to Chart a Map for the Future
ADA's 2005 Conference and Convention, Thursday, June 23 - Friday, June 24, 2005
 
WASHINGTON - June 9 - ADA National President, Representative Jim McDermott (D-WA), and ADA National Director, Amy Isaacs, invite you to the 58th Annual Americans For Democratic Action Conference and Convention. The conference will feature major presentations and training workshops for grassroots activists at the Holiday Inn-Georgetown from Thursday, June 23 - Friday, June 24, 2005.

Participants include EMILY's List president Ellen R. Malcolm, strategist Donna Brazile, Pulitzer Prize-Winning Author Seymour Hersh, former Senator George McGovern, former Rep. David Bonior, and Democratic National Committee Vice Chair Rep. Mike Honda (D-CA).

ADA is one of the nation's leading independent liberal political organization, dedicated to individual liberty and building economic and social justice at home and abroad. Since 1947, we have led public opinion and coalitions by taking early, principled stands on a broad range of domestic, foreign, economic, military, social and environmental issues.
