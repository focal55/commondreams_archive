NARAL Pro-Choice America on FDA's Decision to Delay Action on Morning-After Pill
 

WASHINGTON -- January 24 -- Nancy Keenan, president of NARAL Pro-Choice America, the nation's leading advocate for personal privacy and a woman's right to choose, issued the following the statement after the Food and Drug Administration alerted the manufacturer of the morning-after pill that it would delay its decision on whether to permit the over-the-counter sale of the emergency contraceptive to women.

"I certainly hope FDA's decision isn't motivated by political considerations three days before President Bush's supporters stage their annual anti-choice protest in Washington," Keenan.
