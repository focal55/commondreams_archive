Oxfam to Publish Tsunami Shelter Report
 

WASHINGTON - December 13 - Oxfam International is publishing a report today (under strict international embargo until 8:01 p.m. EST) outlining the shelter situation in the countries worst affected by the tsunami one year after the disaster struck.

The report, "A place to stay, a place to live," will outline:

-- The scale of the shelter challenge the tsunami left in its wake

-- The progress that has been made so far

-- The obstacles to faster progress and some of the challenges faced

-- Recommendations for moving forward

The report is available now under embargo. Video material of the rebuilding effort from the worst affected countries will also be available.

Oxfam will be publishing another report on Dec. 20 looking at the impact of the tsunami on livelihoods and how livelihoods are beginning to recover.
