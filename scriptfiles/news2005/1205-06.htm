Sierra Club Press Conference at COP 11 Climate Conference, Dec 6
The real U.S. leaders on global warming solutions are in our cities, states, and businesses
 

MONTREAL - December 5 - With the world's attention focused on the Bush administration's irresponsible attempt to block progress at the United Nation's Climate Change Conference in Montreal, representatives from the environmental, labor, business, and state government communities will highlight the real U.S. leaders on global warming solutions: local government and business leaders who are taking real action and leading the way toward a safer and more secure future for us all.

Each speaker will give a brief presentation on innovative energy solutions that are re-energizing their communities. A question and answer period will follow the presentations.

WHAT: Press conference. Brief presentations/Question and answer period following

WHEN: Tuesday, December 6th, 2005 - 11:00am

WHERE:
Miramichi River Room,
Palais des Congrès de Montreal -
159 rue Saint-Amtoine Ouest, 9e etage,
Montreal, Quebec

SPEAKERS:
