Pentagon Auditors Conclude Halliburton Overcharged $100 Million
President Asked When and How U.S. Taxpayers and Iraqi People Will Be Repaid
 

WASHINGTON -- March 14 -- Today Reps. Waxman and Dingell sent a letter to President Bush revealing that Defense Department auditors have determined, in a report withheld from Congress, that Halliburton overcharged by more than $100 million under its no-bid Iraq oil contract.

The text of the letter and the executive summary of the audit are posted at: www.democrats.reform.house.gov/
