MoveOn.org Statement in Response to the Indictment of Lewis Libby
Statement by Eli Pariser, Executive Director of MoveOn.org Political Action
 

WASHINGTON – “Libby is getting off easy. The lies he and others in the Administration may have told the Grand Jury are nothing compared to the lies they told the American people to justify war in Iraq. Now 2000 American soldiers have died in a war most Americans regret. It’s not enough for Libby to step down. The President should fire all those involved.”
