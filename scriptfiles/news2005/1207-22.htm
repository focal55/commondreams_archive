More Tax Cuts Proposed Despite Deficits
 

WASHINGTON - December 7 - Economic Policy Institute economist Max Sawicky enumerates Congress’s flagrant tax breaks for the wealthy in today’s Snapshot. In the face of unsustainable budget deficits and draconian spending cuts, House Republicans make it clear where their priorities lie as they push for $112 billion in tax cuts aimed mostly at dividend income, capital gains, and business firms.
