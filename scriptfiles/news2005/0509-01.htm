Texans Detail Human Costs of Owen’s Radical Right Judicial Activism
 

WASHINGTON -- May 9 -- The Coalition for a Fair and Independent Judiciary, representing the country’s leading public interest and civil rights organizations, will detail the human toll of federal judicial nominee Priscilla Owen’s record of right wing judicial activism and indifference to victims of injustice at a press conference in the U.S. Capitol on Monday, May 9 at 3:00 p.m.

Texas citizens will talk about the impact on ordinary citizens of Owen’s controversial rulings during her tenure as a Justice on the Texas Supreme Court. Senator Charles Schumer (NY) and Senator Ted Kennedy (MA) will participate to talk about the importance of carefully assessing the record and qualifications of those seeking powerful, lifetime appointments to the federal bench, as well as the need for bipartisan cooperation and consultation as the Senate debates judicial nominations.

Under pressure from ultra-conservative groups, Senate Republican leadership is trying to eliminate the Senate filibuster of judicial nominations. Known as the “nuclear option,” this radical change in Senate rules would dismantle our system of checks and balances and overturn more than 200 years of Senate tradition. Priscilla Owen’s nomination is widely viewed as a potential trigger for implementing the nuclear option.
