Newly Released Pricing Studies Find Wal-Mart Charging Customers Wrong Price in Four States
Press Conference Call Nov. 21
 

WASHINGTON - November 21 - WakeUpWalMart.com will release two new studies documenting Wal-Mart's pricing errors in 4 states.

In addition, the National Consumers League and WakeUpWalMart.com will be jointly calling on Attorneys General in all 50 states to investigate Wal-Mart's pricing and scanner practices.

Connecticut Attorney General Richard Blumenthal will be on the call to discuss what Attorney Generals can do.

What: WakeUpWalMart.com Press Conference Call to Discuss Newly Released Studies on Wal-Mart's Pricing Errors
