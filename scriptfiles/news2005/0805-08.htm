Statement of Congressman Dennis J. Kucinich On The 14 Marines From Brook Park Battalion Killed In Iraq Wednesday

 

WASHINGTON - August 5 - Statement of Congressman Dennis J. Kucinich (D-OH) on the deaths of 14 Marines from a Brook Park, Ohio Battalion killed in Iraq on Wednesday:

“The horrors of war have once again struck home. Words cannot express the grief I feel. Twice in two days, our community has been rocked with the horrific news that heroes from our community have been killed while serving this nation.

“It is an absolutely tragic day for Brook Park, our state and our nation.

“The brave and courageous Marines killed today, like those killed yesterday from the same Battalion, represent the very best of the Marine Corps, our community and our nation. They will forever be remembered as heroes.

“Our community will always honor these heroes, and will be forever grateful for their service.

“My thoughts and prayers are with the families and loved ones of these heroes. May God bless them, and all those who continue to serve our nation.”
