David Rovics International Activist Singer/Songwriter/Blogger Will Perform Two New Songs At Camp Casey
 
CAMP CASEY, Texas - August 30 - On Tuesday, David Rovics, internationally acclaimed activist/singer/songwriter/blogger, will perform a, “Song for Cindy Sheehan” and “Every Day Is Mother's Day” at Camp Casey.

The MP3 studio recording of a “Song for Cindy Sheehan” is now at the top of several SoundClick Charts. The top tune is available to hear online and as a free download for portable music players like Apple’s iPod at: http://www.soundclick.com/davidrovics

“After reading Medea Benjamin's article, Every Day Is Mother's Day, at www.commondreams.org.,” David wrote in an email sent to fans. “ I wrote “Every Day Is Mother’s Day” on the plane to Houston yesterday. I'm going to finally get a chance to visit Camp Casey tomorrow (Tuesday)."

Camp Casey residents are encouraged to digitally record David’s performance and freely share it online.

David Rovics Bio

David Rovics has been called the musical voice of the progressive movement in the US. Amy Goodman has called him "the musical version of Democracy Now!" Since the mid-90's Rovics has spent most of his time on the road, playing hundreds of shows every year throughout North America, Europe, Latin America and the Middle East. He and his songs have been featured on national radio programs in the US, Canada, Britain, Ireland, Italy, Sweden, Denmark and elsewhere. He has shared the stage regularly with leading intellectuals (Noam Chomsky, Howard Zinn), activists (Medea Benjamin, Ralph Nader), politicians (Dennis Kucinich, George Galloway), musicians (Billy Bragg, the Indigo Girls), and celebrities (Martin Sheen, Susan Sarandon). He has performed at dozens of massive rallies throughout North America and Europe and at thousands of conferences, college campuses and folk clubs throughout the world. He makes all of his recordings available for free download on his website, www.davidrovics.com, and the downloads are in the many hundreds of thousands. More importantly, he's really good. He will make you laugh, he will make you cry, and he will make the revolution irresistable. http://members.aol.com/drovics/bio.htm
