Launch of TaketotheStreets.com;
Progressive Activities Listing Website
 

SANTA FE - August 8 - Two students have launched a new website called "take to the streets.com". Its sole purpose is to list progressive activities taking place nationwide, with emphasis on demonstrations and rallies. Instead of announcements of rallies being scattered over literally hundreds of websites, the need for such a list is compiled in one place for several reasons: Information has power only if it has focus; seeing such a list will have a positive psychological effect on large numbers of people to motivate open-minded and fair-minded people all over this country to take part, and take responsibility in helping America live up to its full promise.

There is no charge for any Progressive Organization to list.
