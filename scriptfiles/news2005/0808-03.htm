Sierra Club Presents the Chico Mendes Award to Forest Defender, Unfairly Imprisoned
 

MEXICO CITY- August 8 - Felipe Arreaga Sánchez, Celsa Valdovinos and Albertano Peñaloza Domínguez have been tireless environmental advocates for more than ten years. Now Felipe is unjustly imprisoned simply for defending Guerrero's forests. The Sierra Club together with Greenpeace, Amnesty International and the Center for Human Rights of the Mountain, Tlachinollan, will hold a ceremony recognizing the three activists' courageous work. The Sierra Club will honor them with their prestigious Chico Mendes Award.

WHAT: The presentation of the Chico Mendes Award to Felipe Arreaga Sánchez, Celsa Valdovinos and Albertano Peñaloza Domínguez

WHEN: Tuesday, August 9 at 11:00 a.m.

WHERE: Casa Universitaria del Libro, Calle de Orizaba #24, Col. Roma

México D.F. - Felipe Arreaga Sánchez, Celsa Valdovinos y Albertano
Peñaloza Domínguez han sido defensores incansables de la protección del
