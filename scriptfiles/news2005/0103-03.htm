Government Watchdog Groups to Urge House Not to Weaken Ethics Rules
 

WASHINGTON -- January 3 -- The Congressional Ethics Coalition will hold a press conference today to call upon the U.S. House of Representatives not to weaken the chamber's ethics rules or to seek retribution against members of the House ethics committee who voted last year to admonish Majority Leader Tom DeLay. The House is expected to act upon a proposed ethics package for the new session this week.

The coalition is a nonpartisan, ideologically diverse group of government watchdog groups, including the Center for Responsive Politics.
