Citizens to Call on Rep. Davis to Oppose Bush Privatization Plan at Rally in Fairfax, Va. Saturday
 

WASHINGTON -- February 25 -- News Advisory:

  • Citizens to Call on Rep. Davis to Oppose Bush Privatization Plan at Rally in Fairfax, Va. Saturday
  • District Residents Expected to Gather Before Rep. Davis' Town Hall and Demand He Signs Pledge, Protest Bush's Plan

In a show of political force, scores of Virginia residents of U.S. Rep. Tom Davis', R-Va., congressional district will gather with state and national organizations on Saturday to call on Rep. Davis to sign a pledge to oppose President Bush's scheme to privatize Social Security.

WHAT: Social Security Town Hall Event

WHEN: Saturday, Feb. 26, 10:30 a.m. EST

WHERE: City Hall, 10455 Armstrong Street, Fairfax, Va.

PARTICIPANTS: Scores of Virginia residents
