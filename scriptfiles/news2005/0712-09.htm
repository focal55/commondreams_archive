Lawmakers Call For Reform of Chemicals Policy to Protect Consumers
Most Chemicals Untested, Many Dangerous to Human Health
 

WASHINGTON - July 12 - Environmental health advocates will join with Senator Frank Lautenberg (NJ) and Rep. Henry Waxman (CA) in calling for reforms to protect children and all Americans from toxic chemicals found in common consumer products. These chemicals are linked to cancer, birth defects and other serious maladies and current law does not require manufacturers to disclose all of the chemicals found in their products. Their bill introduction comes as the European Union is debating its own chemicals policy, which the Bush Administration has actively opposed.
