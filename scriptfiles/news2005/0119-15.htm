Lamarche to Speak and Emcee at Several Inaugural Events Will Join Billionaires for Bush at the Recoronation Ball
 

WASHINGTON -- January 19 -- Pat LaMarche, of Yarmouth, Maine, the Green Party's Vice Presidential candidate, has a busy schedule for the second term inaugural of George W. Bush in Washington D.C. on Thursday, January 20.

Pat will join Green Party Presidential candidate, David Cobb, and others for a rally at 9 A.M. at Malcolm X Park, 16th St. and Euclid NW, sponsored by the D.C. Anti-War Network (DAWN), CODE PINK (www.codepink.org ), and other groups, and then speak at a rally at Dupont Circle called The Women's March and Funeral Procession in the counter-inaugural Women's March and New Orlean-Style Funeral Procession with members of NOW, The Raging Grannies, Ladies of Liberty, National Mourning Project, Theaters Against War and many, many more.

Ms LaMarche will speak and emcee the event which all groups will then march to at McPherson Square, 15th St. and I St. NW, where a major anti-Bush rally will take place with a Counter Inaugural stage set up with speakers and entertainment at 11 A.M.. (contact David Lytell 202-966-0040).

In the evening Pat will attend the Re-Coronation Ball from 8:30 P.M. until 2 A.M. sponsored by the Billionaires for Bush at the Platinum Night Club 915 F Street, NW, near Metro Center Dress to Oppress. Accessories will be available at the event (top hats, tiaras, and cigarette holders.) See www.billionairesforbush.com for tickets and tips on how to dress wealthy! Advanced tickets highly recommended.(suggestions for Pat's fake name can be sent to nallen@acadia.net ) Pat-Me-On-The Back LaMarche??

On Friday, January 21 at 2 P.M. she will join David Cobb to speak at the opening of the Progressive Democratic Summit, an event that will feature experienced speakers in the progressive movement, informational workshops, and other useful information for progressive activists and organizations.
