New “Harry & Louise”-Style Radio Ad Challenges Sen. Wyden to Preserve Estate Tax
United for a Fair Economy & Oregon Citizens Rally to Launch Multi-Pronged Ad Campaign to Prevent New Tax Breaks for Billionaires
 

PORTLAND, OR — The national advocacy organization United for a Fair Economy unveiled a new radio ad today at a press conference calling on Senator Wyden to preserve the estate tax, and in turn, $1 trillion in revenue over the next two decades, when the vote comes up this summer. The ad’s launch is an opening salvo in an aggressive TV and radio ad campaign to educate citizens about the potential of massive new tax breaks for billionaires.

Local Oregon leaders joined United for a Fair Economy in voicing their opposition to the White House’s plans to permanently repeal the estate tax.

“We know that the White House will be leaning hard on Senator Wyden and others like him,” said Michael Davoli of United for a Fair Economy. “We want to make sure he hears from his friends and neighbors at home, not just from the President and the wealthiest 1% of Americans.”

United for a Fair Economy today began airing a 60-second “Harry and Louise”-style radio ad in the Portland media market—one of six markets where the group is running ads, including: Helena and Great Falls, MT; Portland, ME; Fargo, ND; and Sioux Falls, SD. The group, along with other organizations who oppose the repeal of the estate tax, plans to run a series of TV ads during the summer.

The radio spot features a near-retirement age couple lamenting about the debt they and their children will be carrying if the President repeals the estate tax to benefit multi-millionaires.

“With income way down and the tremendous expense of the Iraq War, there will surely be less money for healthcare and education. What a debt our children and grandchildren will be carrying!” says husband, Frank, to his wife, Marge.

Today’s Wall Street Journal released a survey that shows that, while most millionaires favor changing the estate tax, most who have inherited wealth say that the rich should pay more taxes.

To listen to the ad, visit: www.FairEconomy.org. United for a Fair Economy (UFE) is a national, nonpartisan, non-profit organization raising awareness that concentrated wealth and power undermine the economy, corrupt democracy, deepen the racial divide, and tear communities apart.
