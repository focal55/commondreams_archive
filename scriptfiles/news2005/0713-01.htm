Experts: HHS Teen Health Website Inaccurate & Misleading
 

WASHINGTON - July 13 - Scientific experts say that www.4parents.gov, an HHS website that claims to "equip parents to talk with their teens about sex and relationships," is seriously flawed and likely to backfire, providing erroneous and incomplete information about sexually transmitted diseases, the effectiveness of contraceptives, and sexual orientation.

Rep. Waxman released these expert analyses and called on the US Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) to pull down the site and build a resource for parents backed by the best available scientific evidence.
