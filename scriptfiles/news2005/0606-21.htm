Equal Rights Groups Oppose Judicial Nomination of William Pryor
Telephone Press Conference Hosted by The Interfaith Alliance
 

In the next several days, the Senate plans to consider the nomination of William Pryor to a seat on the U. S. Court of Appeals for the 11th Circuit. Mr. Pryor repeatedly has exhibited that he is far outside of the mainstream of American jurisprudence. Many groups that work to maintain constitutionally guaranteed equal rights for all Americans are strongly opposed because his confirmation would be detrimental to religious liberty, human rights, women's rights, rights of the disabled, consumers, workers, and the environment.

WHAT: Telephone press conference. After brief statements from leaders of several advocacy groups, reporters will be invited to ask questions. An MP3 recording of the press conference will be available that afternoon at (www.interfaithalliance.org)
