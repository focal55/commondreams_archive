Will the Fed Continue Hammering Inflation?
 

WASHINGTON - December 13 - There's an old saying: when all you have is a hammer, every problem looks like a nail. The Economic Policy Institute is worried that the Federal Reserve will continue hammering at inflation with interest rate increases. These rate hikes will slow down potential wage growth by braking economic growth, yet, it's not wage growth that constitutes the main inflation problem. Today's Snapshot shows that labor costs are not driving price inflation in the current recovery, and, argues for the Fed to put down the hammer.
