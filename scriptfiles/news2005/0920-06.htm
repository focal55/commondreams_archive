Pelosi and Waxman to Hold News Conference to Discuss Creation of Anti-Fraud Commission

 

WASHINGTON - September 20 - House Democratic Leader Nancy Pelosi and Congressman Henry Waxman (D-CA) will hold a news conference today at 3:00 p.m. to announce the introduction of legislation to create an anti-fraud commission to prevent fraud and abuse in the recovery and rebuilding effort in the Gulf Coast region.

WHO: House Democratic Leader Nancy Pelosi Congressman Henry Waxman (D-CA), Ranking Member, Government Reform Committee

WHAT: News Conference on Legislation Creating Anti-Fraud Commission

WHEN: Tuesday, September 20 at 3:00 p.m.
