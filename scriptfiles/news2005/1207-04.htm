Rep. Gibbons Defends Public Land Giveaway Plan
 

WASHINGTON - December 7 - Environmental Working Group (EWG) President Ken Cook issued the following statement about Rep. Gibbons’ (R-NV) conference call today defending the proposal he wrote that would auction off, for as little as $1,000 an acre, taxpayer-owned lands in the U.S.:

“Mr. Gibbons has managed to put forth a proposal that has drawn criticism across the West and across party lines. His proposal to sell off millions of acres of prized public lands has offended just about everyone who uses these lands for any purpose. Mr. Gibbons’ legislation is sinking under the weight of unprecedented, bipartisan opposition.”

EWG’s state-by-state analysis of the proposal, championed by Rep. Gibbons and House Resources Committee Chairman Richard Pombo (R-CA), can be viewed here: http://www.ewg.org/reports/dirtcheap/index.php.
