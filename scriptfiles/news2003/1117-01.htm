New Proposal Provides Too Little Help For Poor Seniors, Too Much Help To Traditional Medicare
  

WASHINGTON - November 17 - Statement of Ron Pollack, Executive Director of Families USA, on Reported Medicare Prescription Drug Agreement:

"This proposal does too much to destroy Medicare and too little to help the seniors who can least afford their medicines. It still can, and should be, changed."
