Planned Parenthood Demands Comprehensive Global AIDS Bill
   WASHINGTON - May 1 - The following statement by Gloria Feldt, president of Planned Parenthood Federation of America and author of "Behind Every Choice is a Story," on a comprehensive global AIDS bill was released today:

President Bush is trying to have his cake and eat it, too. If the president is really committed to fighting the war on AIDS, then he needs to shutdown his cronies in Congress who are working to weaken the Global AIDS bill with anti-condom, abstinence-only measures.

While anti-choice members of Congress waste months playing deadly and unconscionable political games to appease their right-wing base, women's health and lives hang in the balance. Stopping the spread of HIV/AIDS is too important for politics as usual.

Congress must not divert precious U.S. funds to programs that fail to educate people about all the ways to prevent HIV transmission. Tying the hands of health care providers by imposing anti-condom, abstinence-only restrictions will cause increased suffering and more deaths.

How many lives will Congress and the Bush administration sacrifice to promote their ideology? Compassion demands the swift passage of a comprehensive Global AIDS bill.

Planned Parenthood Federation of America is the nation's largest and most trusted voluntary reproductive health care organization. Planned Parenthood affiliates serve nearly five million women, men and teenagers every year.
