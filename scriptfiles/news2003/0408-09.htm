Thousands to Hand Out 'Checks' to U.S. Congress at Children's Defense Fund Rally
  WASHINGTON - April 8 - Children's Defense Fund President Marian Wright Edelman, elected officials and millionaire Hollywood Moms will make statements Wednesday at a gathering before thousands of students hand out "$62,500 checks to a Millionaire from their college fund" to Senators and Reps. Thousands of other child advocates will meet with senators to stop the Bush Administration's plans to give massive tax cuts to the rich while eliminating, dismantling, cutting and freezing essential services for children. The Senate and House could vote as early as this week on a final budget resolution.

According to The Washington Post (Mar. 24, 2003), "hundreds of thousands" of Wednesday in Washington(tm) activists jammed the Capitol switchboard last month to stop the Bush Administration's budget and tax war on children. Based on a similar initiative used by the Civil Rights Movement of the 1960s, the Children's Defense Fund's Wednesdays in Washington(tm) efforts call on people to take action through personal visits, phone calls, letters, e-mail, faxes, prayers, and other activities to alert leaders about what can be done to truly Leave No Child Behind(r).
