Tim Robbins, Win Without War to Discuss the War, the Movement, and the Bush 'First-Strike Doctrine' at National Press Club
  

WASHINGTON - April 3 -

News Advisory:

Actor/Director Tim Robbins and Win Without War National Director, former Congressman Tom Andrews, will address the National Press Club on the range of Iraq-related issues including the war, the anti-war movement, post-war issues and the Bush "First-Strike Doctrine."

WHEN:
April 15, 2003
12:30 - 2 p.m.

WHERE:
National Press Club Ballroom

The luncheon will begin at 12:30 p.m. Remarks will begin just after 1 p.m., followed by a question-and-answer session.

Advance reservations may be made by calling 202-662-7501 or by email at pnelson@press.org.

The club is located at 14th and F Streets, one block from Metro Center, on the 13th floor of the National Press Building.

Reservations must be made by April 12 at 2 p.m.
