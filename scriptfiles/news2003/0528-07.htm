Call to Action Against Worst Assault on Poor Children in History
Edelman to Make Two Major Speeches in Boston
  WASHINGTON - May 28 - News Advisory:

Children's Defense Fund President Marian Wright Edelman will deliver two different major speeches in Boston this week on the Bush Administration's war on poor children. The Children's Defense Fund believes essential protections for children should be improved and expanded. The Bush Administration's plan dismantles, eliminates, cuts and freezes essential protections for millions of children to pay for massive new tax breaks for a few millionaires.

FRIDAY, MAY 30 -- SATURDAY, MAY 31

BOSTON, Mass.
FRIDAY, May 30
6:00 p.m. -- Mrs. Edelman delivers a major speech on Head Start to hundreds of low-income neighborhood residents and graduates at the Urban College of Boston's 10th anniversary commencement exercises.
Boston Swiss Hotel, One Avenue de Lafayette
