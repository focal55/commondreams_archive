The Baffler Magazine 2003 "Business and the American Mind" Tour
   NEW YORK - September 4 - Tom Frank, Dave Mulcahey, Chris Lehmann, Josh Glenn and other Baffler contributors will celebrate the publication of BOOB JUBILEE: The Cultural Politics of the New Economy (W.W. Norton & Company, September 2003, $15.95, paper) with events around the country.

A legendarily tenacious thorn in the side of modern business culture, The Baffler magazine draws on a long tradition of dissent in its battle against market forces. Gleefully assaulting the free market and the "two-fisted boodlers, upper-class twits, and hang-'em-high moralists" that make it tick, Baffler editors Thomas Frank and David Mulcahey along with contributors like Josh Glenn, Chris Lehmann, Ben Metcalf and a host of others, have restored a sense of outrage and urgency to the battle by speaking about American culture as though people cared.

Tom Frank is the author of 'Commodify Your Dissent' and 'The Conquest of Cool'. Dave Mulcahey is managing editor of The Baffler.
