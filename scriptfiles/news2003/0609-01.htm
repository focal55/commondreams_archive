Women's Rights Groups to Make Major Announcement

   WASHINGTON - June 9 - Feminist Majority, National Organization for Women, NARAL Pro Choice America and Planned Parenthood Federation of America will announce the most important event in 12 years to save reproductive freedom.

WHO:

-- Eleanor Smeal, president - Feminist Majority

-- Kim Gandy, president - National Organization for Women

-- Gloria Feldt, president - Planned Parenthood Federation of America

-- Kate Michelman, president - NARAL Pro Choice America

-- Irasema Garza, director of Women's Rights Department - AFSCME

-- C. DeLores Tucker, chair - National Congress of Black Women (invited)

-- Rev. Timothy McDonald - member of the Board of Directors, RCRC

-- Pastor of First Iconium Baptist Church, Atlanta, GA

-- Jennifer Heitel - GWU student activist

WHERE:

National Press Club, First Amendment Room, 529 14th Street NW, Washington, DC

WHEN:

June 10, 10 a.m.

WHY:

The White House and the majority of the US Senate and House are aligned in anti-choice lockstep. The federal judiciary is being packed with judges who are hostile to women's rights. State legislatures are chipping away at a woman's right to a safe and legal abortion. The U.S. Supreme Court hangs in the balance as rumors of impending resignations persist. Despite all this, poll after poll indicates that the majority of Americans are pro-choice. We must stand up and fight for our rights. This event will send a strong, clear message: Women WILL fight for their rights
