Mr. President Stop The Spin: Tell The Truth To The American People
   WASHINGTON - July 30 - Today's presidential news conference was nothing more than presidential spin in which the President once again failed to come forward and tell the truth to the American public concerning his Administration's misleading statements in the lead-up to the war against Iraq, stated Congressman Dennis J. Kucinich (D-OH).

Congressman Dennis J. Kucinich (D-OH), leader of the opposition to the war in Iraq in the House, issued the following statement today on President Bush's news conference:

"It is clear, in the lead-up to war against Iraq, the Administration from the top on down, orchestrated a campaign of misleading statements to persuade the Congress of the United States and American public to support a war against Iraq.

"At today's Presidential news conference, the President once again had an opportunity to be forthcoming and to dispel doubts about his Administration's pattern of misleading statements in the lead up to war. Unfortunately, the President failed to reestablish the credibility of his Administration. At this moment of crisis, while the lives America's sons and daughters are on the line in Iraq, the American public deserves the truth, not spin."
