"In the Spirit of Martin: The Living Legacy of Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr." is the first major exhibition of visual arts dedicated to celebrating this American hero and civil rights leader. Featuring 115 works by prominent and emerging artists, including folk artists, the exhibition is a visual testament to the enduring power of King's life and work.

"In the Spirit of Martin" was created and developed by Gary Chassman, Verve Editions, and organized for travel by the Smithsonian Institution Traveling Exhibition Service (SITES) in cooperation with The Martin Luther King, Jr. Center for Nonviolent Social Change. "In the Spirit of Martin" was made possible by PepsiCo Inc.
