MoveOn.org Voter Fund To Roll Out Ad Campaign To Focus On Bush Economy
 

WASHINGTON - November 19 -

First in Television Ad Series Notes Job Losses,
Makes Links Between Bush Failures and Those of Hoover

Senator Corzine to Comment on Bush Economic Performance
At National Press Club News Event Friday at 1 P.M.

In the aftermath of new government jobs data to be released on Friday, MoveOn.org Voter Fund will show a new TV spot it plans to begin airing in battleground states, stressing the failures and tilt toward rich people of George W. Bush’s edonomic program.

Sen. Jon Corzine (D-NJ), will speak at the news conference, along with Ack Exley, organizing director for the Voter Fund.

WHO: Senator Corzine and Zack Exley of the MoveOn.org Voter Fund

WHAT: News Conference and viewing of new TV spot

WHERE: National Press Club, Lisagor Room

WHEN: Friday, Nov. 21, 2003 at 1 p.m.

The MoveOn.org Voter Fund is a Section 527 political committee affiliated with MoveOn.org, the nation’s fastest-growing grassroots advocacy organization, with some 1.8 million members in the United States.
