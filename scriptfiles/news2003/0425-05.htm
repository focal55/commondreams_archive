Planned Parenthood Denounces Santorum's Attack on Birth Control; Statement of Gloria Feldt
   WASHINGTON - April 25 - Following is a statement of Gloria Feldt, president of Planned Parenthood Federation of America and author of "Behind Every Choice Is a Story" regarding Sen. Santorum:

"Sen. Rick Santorum's (R-Pa.) recent offensive comments were not limited to attacking lesbians and gays. He also declared that Americans have no constitutional right to privacy. And he said that the U.S. Supreme Court erred in Griswold, the decision that established the right to birth control. In Sen. Santorum's ideal world, women and men would once again face state bans and restrictions on contraception.

"The criticism of his hateful statements about lesbians and gays is certainly justified. But where is the outrage over the senator's equally hateful attempt to return us to the days when women did not have access to birth control? Sen. Santorum's disregard for women as moral decision makers is despicable. His desire to impose his personal beliefs upon others is just plain wrong.

"It is time for the Republican Party to walk the talk of 'compassionate conservatism.' Sen. Santorum's homophobic and sexist remarks warrant his immediate removal from leadership."

Planned Parenthood Federation of America is the nation's largest and most trusted voluntary reproductive health care organization. Planned Parenthood affiliates serve nearly five million women, men and teenagers every year.
