Thousands of Kucinich Supporters at MeetUps Tonight
   WASHINGTON - September 4 -Tonight as the Democratic presidential candidates debate in Albuquerque, thousands of supporters of one candidate, Congressman Dennis Kucinich, will be gathering in hundreds of meetings at restaurants, cafes, libraries and community centers across America. Kucinich supporters will not be isolated couch potatoes tonight, they'll be socializing and strategizing. The gatherings are organized through the MeetUp.com website.

In roughly two months, nearly 12,000 Kucinich supporters have signed up to be part of the Kucinich MeetUp gatherings, the second largest group of all political campaigns. Many have set their VCRs to tape tonight's debate so they can focus at their MeetUps on organizing grassroots Kucinich groups in their communities and on nearby colleges.
