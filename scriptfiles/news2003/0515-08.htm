World Health Leaders to Discuss Health and the Environment
  WASHINGTON - May 15 - News Advisory:

WHAT: World health leaders and nearly 2,000 conference participants from more than 60 nations gather for the 30th Global Health Council annual international conference. 2003 theme: Our Future on Common Ground: Health and the Environment.

WHEN: May 27 - 30

WHERE: Omni-Shoreham Hotel, 2500 Calvert St NW, Washington, DC 20008

WHY: The environment, in its broadest sense, is one of the major determinants of human health and well-being. The four-day conference will address the key issues affecting the physical, social and economic environmental factors that pose health risks in the world. Nearly 2,000 healthcare providers, community organizers, program managers, policy makers, researchers, and advocates from around the world will meet to explore concrete ways in which the health community can make use of a better understanding of environmental issues in daily practice, and directly influence those issues that have the greatest impact on health. For more information on the schedule of events at the conference, visit the Council's website at www.globalhealth.org.
