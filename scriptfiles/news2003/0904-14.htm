With Minimal Notice, Four Protests Planned to Counter Attorney General John Ashcroft’s Closed-door Meetings
   NORTHAMPTON, MA - September 4 - As Attorney General John Ashcroft continues his tour touting the Patriot Act to hand-picked audiences, activists in four cities quickly organized demonstrations to counter his rhetoric.

Since Mr. Ashcroft’s tour started on August 19, seven local resolutions representing approximately 2 million people supporting rights and liberties guaranteed by the U.S. Constitution have been passed. In all, 157 local resolutions and three statewide resolutions passed thus far protect the liberties of nearly 19 million people. For details, see the Bill of Rights Defense Committee’s website.
