PFAW Launches Petition Drive Demanding Independent Investigation of Bush Administration Leak of CIA Agent's Identity
   WASHINGTON - September 29 - People For the American Way is launching a petition drive demanding that "Attorney General John Ashcroft name an independent investigator to determine and prosecute the Bush administration sources of illegal national security disclosures last July revealing the identity of an undercover CIA agent."
