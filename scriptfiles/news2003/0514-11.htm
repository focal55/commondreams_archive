TomPaine.com Launches A New Feature 'Take on the News'
  WASHINGTON - May 14 - TomPaine.com today debuts a new feature "Take on the News" - a Web log that will provide ongoing daily commentary on the media, media makers and the politics and policy of Washington.

"Democracy requires an aggressive, independent, skeptical press dedicated to serving the public interest. Today's consolidated, profit-driven, power-cozy news media too often falls short. TomPaine.com's new Web log, Take on the News, tracks the lowpoints and highpoints," the Op Ad opens.

The Ad contains two examples of what's in store for readers: the first is a critique of National Journal columnist William Powers and the second turns a skeptical eye towards a report from Lynn Neary on NPR. Multiple other postings can be found at http://www.tompaine.com/blog.cfm.

To view the ad, visit http://www.tompaine.com/op_ads/.

TomPaine.com is a nonprofit, nonpartisan Internet journal. Since 1999, its online content and ads have been praised by Rolling Stone, Forbes.com, the Columbia Journalism Review, Chicago Tribune, PC Magazine and many others.
