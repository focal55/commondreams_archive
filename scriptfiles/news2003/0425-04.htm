Sen. Lautenberg, Former Gun Industry Exec Urge Opposition to Legal Immunity for Gun Industry
   WASHINGTON - April 25 - News Advisory:

WHAT: News conference featuring Sen. Frank Lautenberg (D-N.J.) and former gun industry executive Robert A. Ricker, who has blown the whistle on gun industry practices that aid criminal access to guns. Sen. Lautenberg and Ricker are joining activists with the Justice for Gun Victims campaign to urge opposition to S. 659 -- a federal bill that would take away the legal rights of gun violence victims.

The proposed sweeping special interest legislation would shield gun dealers and gun manufacturers from legal accountability -- even when they act irresponsibly. For more information, visit http://www.justiceforgunvictims.org.
