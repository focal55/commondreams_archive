Kate Clinton & Reno Together Again for the First Time in "Patriot Acts" to Benefit United for Peace and Justice & Democracy Now!'s Home Station WBAI
   WASHINGTON - June 3 - Kate Clinton & Reno have each reached icon status here in home town, Manhattan. Though they have never shared a stage together, the current political climate is ripe, overripe in fact, for this coming together of the two smartest and funniest women in comedy.

Performing, alone and together, at Washington Sq. Church, 135 West 4th St.

KATE CLINTON is one of America's brightest political comedians. This humorist/monologist's agenda includes political material as fresh as the daily news, savvy send-ups of modern family relationships and illuminating perspectives on life as a gay American;

"Quick witted, clear spoken . . . She has developed a bizarrely logical, seemingly free associating style of delivery (and) had this critic in tears from laughing so hard." Ben Brantley, New York Times

RENO is a stream of consciousness style solo comedic performer living in New York City. Reno's performance film of her off Broadway show, "Rebel Without a Pause: unrestrained reflections on September 11th" opened on May 5th to rave reviews;

"With body language that's part prizefighter, part baggy-pants clown, Reno is famous for her bluntness . . There are no sacred cows in Reno's cynical and absurdist take on terrorism and politics." Stephen Holden, New York Times
