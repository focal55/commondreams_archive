POSTPONED: Sept 18 rally in DC featuring Nader, Kucinich, etc.
   WASHINGTON - September 17 - Because of safety concerns related to Hurricane Isabel, the Democracy Rising Rally scheduled for Thursday night, September 18th at the Metropolitan AME Church, 1518 M St NW, featuring Ralph Nader, Dennis Kucinich, Patti Smith, Head-Roc, Noyeek the Grizzly Bear, and community leaders is POSTPONED until early October. The new concert/rally date will be sent out within the next 24 hours.

Please call 202-319-2422 or visit http://www.democracyrising.org for more information, tickets, and to volunteer. Once the new date is confirmed, please put it on all calendar and event listings. Tickets purchased for the September 18th event will be honored at our rally in early October.

The fight for DC Statehood, more funding for libraries, reopening DC General Hospital, affordable housing, and saying no to school vouchers will continue at our rally in early October.
