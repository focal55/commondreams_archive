International Conference on Environmental Rights and Human Rights
   AMSTERDAM - August 27 - WHAT: International Conference on Environmental Rights and Human Rights

WHEN: September 16-18th 2003

WHERE: hotel Caribe, Cartagena - Colombia

Friends of the Earth International (FOEI), Transnational Institute (TNI), Oilwatch and Friends of the Earth Colombia (CENSAT Agua Viva) are inviting you to attend a three- day conference on Environmental Rights and Human Rights in Cartagena, Colombia,

September 16-18, 2003.

International experts on environmental issues and human rights issues will speak about:

* the impact of war on environmental rights and human rights

* human right violations of environmental activists

* impunity for environmental crimes

* biodiversity, public property or merchandise?

* accountability for transnational corporations

* land rights and territories

* environmental justice

* ecological debt from northern countries to the south.

Among the invited speakers:
