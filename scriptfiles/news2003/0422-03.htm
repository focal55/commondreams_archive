Find Out Who's Trying to Turn Earth Day Into LuntzDay
National Environmental Trust's LuntzSpeak.Com to Have Real-Time Updates
   WASHINGTON - April 22 - Earth Day is like Saint Patrick's Day (minus the beer and fun) because everyone wears green! And to help you figure out who's green and who's just off-chartreuse, visit LuntzSpeak.com (http://www.luntzspeak.com/).

LuntzSpeak is the National Environmental Trust's new website designed to help distinguish between someone with a real environmental record, and someone who's just using LuntzSpeak, the all-spin creation of famed pollster Frank Luntz that has been widely reported in the media.

ON EARTH DAY, LUNTZSPEAK.COM WILL BE UPDATED HOURLY with all the latest and greatest examples of LuntzSpeak from Members of Congress and the Bush Administration. LuntzSpeak.com will be providing links to actual Earth Day statements, which will be analyzed in real-time to see if and how they are following the advice in the Luntz memo.

Since launching last week, LuntzSpeak.com has been visited thousands of times and been featured in several publications. The National Environmental Trust is running ads to publicize the site in The Hotline, Roll Call, and Variety.
