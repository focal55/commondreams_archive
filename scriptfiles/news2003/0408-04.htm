April 12 Procession and Service
   COLUMBIA, SC - April 8 - On Saturday, April 12, 2003, the Carolina Peace Resource Center will host a Procession and Service to honor all of the fallen in America’s unjust war against Iraq. Mourners will gather in Veteran’s Memorial Park for a brief presentation. The Procession – complete with mock coffins to symbolize both American and Iraqi victims - will begin promptly at 1:30 PM and proceed to the Statehouse for a Service to acknowledge each life that has been lost in the US invasion of Iraq. The service will last until 4:00 PM.

We mourn for each person who has needlessly perished. In addition to the young American soldiers lost in combat, an estimated 1200 innocent Iraqis have been killed to date, and many more will die as the Bush Administration persists with this illegal, unprovoked war against Iraq’s sick & starving population. We support our troops in that we want them to come home immediately, safe and alive.
