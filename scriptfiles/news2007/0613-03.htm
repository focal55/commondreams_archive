Press Conference to Discuss Renewed Efforts in the House of Representatives to Impeach Vice President Cheney
Congressman Dennis Kucinich - Congresswoman Maxine Waters - Congressman Al Wynn
 

WASHINGTON - JUNE 13 - TODAY - Press Conference

When: Wednesday, June 13, 2007

Where: Cannon Terrace; Corner of New Jersey Avenue and Independence Avenue

Time: 9:30 a.m.

Congresswoman Maxine Waters (D-CA) and Congressman Al Wynn will join Congressman Dennis Kucinich to discuss the effort in the House of Representatives right now to impeach the Vice President of the United States, Richard Cheney.

There are currently 8 cosponsors of the impeachment resolution: Maxine Waters (D-CA) Lynn Woolsey (D-CA), Barbara Lee (D-CA), Yvette Clark (D-NY), Jan Schakowsky (D-IL), William Lacy Clay (D-MO) and Al Wynn (D-MD).
