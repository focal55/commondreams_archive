150th British Soldier Killed In Iraq
 

UNITED KINGDON - JUNE 7 - Tony Blair leaves office with the blood of 150 British soldiers on his hands. The occupation in Iraq has been a disaster and every month the troops remain the situation gets worst. More Iraqis have been killed since the invasion in 2003 than the combined total of British military and civilian casualties in World War II.

Lindsey German, national convenor of Stop the War, says, "Gordon Brown has to reverse this policy which has made Britain hated around the world. The troops have to be pulled out from Iraq now."
