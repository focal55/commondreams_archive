Statement of U.S. Senator Russ Feingold
On Democrats Offering Binding Legislation to End the President’s Failed Iraq Policy
 

WASHINGTON, DC - March 8 - “Senator Reid has worked hard to rally the caucus in support of binding legislation to reject the President’s failed policies in Iraq and require redeployment of most U.S. troops from Iraq. While the legislation doesn’t go as far as I would like, it is a strong step toward ending our involvement in this misguided war. I will continue to push for Congress to use its power of the purse to end our involvement in this war.”
