CARE Ready to Respond to Earthquake and Possible Tsunami Near Indonesia
 

September 12 - CARE’s operations in Indonesia are closely monitoring the effects of this morning’s 7.9-magnitude earthquake just off the coast of southern Indonesia, which also triggered a tsunami warning.  With 40 years of experience in Indonesia, our emergency teams will be ready to provide relief aid as needed.  We’ll bring updates on the situation as more information becomes available.  Meanwhile, please help CARE be ready to respond to this quake and other disasters around the world wherever they occur by donating to our Emergency Response Fund.
