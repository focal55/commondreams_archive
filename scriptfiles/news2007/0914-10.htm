Goodbye Gonzales! CODEPINK Hosts Long-Awaited
Going Away Party for the Attorney General
 

WASHINGTON - September 14 - CODEPINK and others will be hosting a going away Gonzales party and happy hour on Friday outside of the Justice Department. Ever since the Attorney General called the Geneva Conventions quaint and sanctioned torture, CODEPINK has been calling for his resignation. Our persistent and colorful protests became a constant in the hearing rooms and at a 3-month-long vigil outside the Department of Justice. We will be there to wave goodbye to Gonzo and celebrate his long-awaited departure as he gives his farewell speech to Justice Department employees. Great photo opportunities.

WHEN: Friday, September 14th 2:30-5:30pm

WHERE: 9th and Constitution NW, the front entrance of the Justice Department

"Resignation couldn't happen to a more deserving Attorney General," says CODEPINKer Liz Hourican of Arizona, who organized the protests outside the Justice Department. "For us, today is a date of celebration."

CODEPINK women and men from around the country continue their political pilgrimage to Congressional hearings, member offices and hallways to demand an end to the illegal US military occupation of Iraq. Each day that Congress does not move to bring our troops home CODEPINK activists will be on Capitol Hill to hold our elected officials accountable for not representing the will of the majority of Americans. CODEPINK now rents a 5 bedroom house blocks from the Capitol and will continue its presence in Congress until the troops come home.
