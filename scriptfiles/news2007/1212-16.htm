Center for Media & Democracy Announces the 2007 Falsies Award Winners
 

WASHINGTON - December 12 - The Center for Media and Democracy (CMD) has released the winners of the 2007 Falsies awards . For the fourth year, the watchdog organization has compiled the most egregious examples of spin, propaganda, and the pollution of the information environment, and narrowed down the field to the worst of the worst.

To decide this year's winners, CMD polled the public and over 1,400 people weighed in on the nominees, in addition to adding their own Readers' Choice awards and examples of Win Against Spin.

A complete write-up on the 2007 Falsies awards is available at the CMD website.

The winners are:

Gold Falsies: Joint award to Congressional Democrats and Freedom's Watch
Silver Falsies: Global warming skeptics
Bronze Falsies: Infant formula industry, represented by the International Formula Council
Dishonorable Mentions: Merck and the push for mandatory HPV vaccination, America Supports You, and FEMA's fake news conference.

To find out how these groups merit such an un-coveted award, and to see the Win Against Spin and Readers' Choice awards, go to the complete CMD article for the specifics.
