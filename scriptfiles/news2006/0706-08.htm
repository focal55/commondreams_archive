Pulitzer Prize-Winning Journalist Seymour Hersh to Address 300- Plus Students on Bush Admin's Unbridled Use of Executive Power
 

WASHINGTON - July 6 - On Tuesday, July 11, Alliance for Justice will host a special event with Pulitzer Prize-winning investigative journalist and author Seymour Hersh at the National Education Association.

From astonishing revelations about prisoner abuse at Abu Ghraib to recent disclosures about widespread domestic surveillance and data mining by the National Security Agency, Americans are questioning to what extent the Bush administration will go-and at what cost to our constitutional system of checks and balances-to achieve its goals. Hersh's speech is entitled, By Executive Order: An Administration Unconstrained by Constitutional Checks and Balances.

WHO:
-- Seymour Hersh, Pulitzer Prize-Winning Investigative Journalist/Author
-- Nan Aron, President of Alliance for Justice
