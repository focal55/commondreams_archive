Stand Up to Bush at the UN on Sept. 19!

And once again, they are exploiting the tragedy of September 11, 2001, to scare people into backing more bloodshed and the whittling down of our rights. On Tuesday, September 19, in New York City, we have the opportunity to stand up and show them that it's not going to work.

If you live near New York City, please join us if you can on Tuesday, September 19, to protest while Bush is addressing the United Nations. We will gather at 9:00AM at a location to be announced and march beginning at 9:30AM. Click here to download a leaflet you can photocopy and distribute to help get the word out.
