Reps. Shays and Meehan Join Reform Groups in Calling Upon the House to Reject Sham Lobbying Reform Bill
H.R. 4975 Fails to Address Influence-Peddling Scandals, Should Be Sent Back to the House for Rewriting
 

WASHINGTON - May 2 -

WHAT: Press Conference in which Reps. Christopher Shays (R-Conn.) and Marty Meehan (D-Mass.) join government watchdog organizations to denounce the so-called “lobbying reform” bill (H.R. 4975) pending before the House. The bill fails to address the most egregious lobbying scandals and abuses of congressional ethics. It needs to be sent back to the House and rewritten into meaningful lobbying reform legislation.

WHEN: Tuesday, May 2, 4:30 p.m.

WHERE: Cannon Terrace, Cannon House Office Building
Entrance at Independence and New Jersey Avenues
Washington, D.C.
