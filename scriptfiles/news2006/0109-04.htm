Groups to Launch Ads That Link Abramoff to Powerful Members of Congress
TV, Radio & Billboard Ads to Run in Rep. DeLay & Rep. Ney's Districts
 

WASHINGTON - January 9 - On a conference call with reporters on Tuesday, the Public Campaign Action Fund and the Campaign for America's Future and will unveil a new set of television, radio and billboard ads that link Rep. Tom DeLay, R-Texas, and Rep. Bob Ney, R-Ohio, to one of the worst corruption scandals to ever hit Washington.

The advertisements, which begin airing on Wednesday, Jan. 11 in Texas and Ohio, will kickoff a joint yearlong effort by the two public interest groups to expose the most corrupt Congress in history. Everyday Americans are paying the price for this corruption as Congress does the bidding of well-heeled lobbyists instead of the business of the people.
