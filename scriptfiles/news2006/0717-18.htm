Leaders to Blast Medicare Prescription Drug Plan
 

WASHINGTON - July 17 - Rep. Jan Schakowsky (D-Ill.) and Rep. Marion Berry (D-Ark.) will join Campaign for America's Future Co-director Robert Borosage at a news conference on Tuesday to release a new report on the devastating effects of Medicare Part D. The report highlights consequences of a massive gap in Medicare's prescription drug coverage known as the "doughnut hole." Nearly 7 million beneficiaries with annual drug costs between $2,250 and $5,100 will fall into the "doughnut hole" on Sept. 22.

-- "Leaders Call for a Medicare Part D Plan That Works"

WHEN: Tuesday, July 18 at 11 a.m.
