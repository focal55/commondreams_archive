Amnesty International Experts Available to Comment on State Dept.'s Country Reports on Human Rights; Report to be Released March 8
 

WASHINGTON - March 7 - Amnesty International USA's Executive Director, Dr. William F. Schulz, and Acting Director of Government Relations, Eric Olson, will be available to the media to provide reaction to the release of the Department of State's Country Reports on Human Rights. The report is expected to be made public on Wednesday, March 8. In addition to Schulz and Olson, Amnesty International's country specialists and regional advocacy directors will be also be available to the press for country specific inquiries.

WHAT: Response to U.S. Department of State's Country Reports on Human Rights

WHO: Amnesty International USA's Dr. William F. Schulz, Eric Olson, country specialists and advocacy directors

WHEN: After the report's release on Wednesday, March 8

For further information or to schedule interviews, please contact the AIUSA press office at 202-544-0200 ext. 302.
