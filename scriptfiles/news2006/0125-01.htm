EWG Responds to EPA Announcement Regarding New Effort on PFOA
 

WASHINGTON - January 25 - Environmental Working Group (EWG) will hold a press briefing today to comment on an announcement the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) will make at 2 pm related to Teflon chemicals.

The Teflon chemical has been found in the blood of 95 percent of Americans, never breaks down in our bodies or in the environment, and causes cancer and birth defects in animal studies.
