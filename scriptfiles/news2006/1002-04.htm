On this teleconference, experts will:

-- outline new campaign fundraising data from hot states

-- share data on the rapid spread of television advertising in judicial elections

-- offer examples of inappropriate special interest group pressure

-- provide public opinion data on Americans¹ attitudes toward these developments

-- discuss solutions to keep America¹s courts fair and impartial
