Statement of Eli Pariser, Executive Director of MoveOn.org Political Action on Lamont - Lieberman Primary
 

WASHINGTON - August 8 - “It is a bad time to defend the status quo in America, and that’s why Democratic Senator Joe Lieberman and countless Republican incumbents running for re-election are having such a tough time. The fact that a challenger such as Ned Lamont could have come from out of nowhere to a dead heat with a powerful, three-term Senator is a testament to the strength of the issues-- the Iraq war, our failed energy policy, our failing health care system--that are driving this campaign.

People are tired of the Iraq War, sky-rocketing gas prices and out-of-control prescription drug costs. They want change. And that's not just true of Connecticut primary voters--it's true of a majority of Americans across the nation.

To move forward, America needs new leadership in Congress and the White House. The 3.2 million members of MoveOn are committed to that goal. And we see a blueprint for a broad Democratic victory this fall in Ned Lamont’s principled campaign.

Whatever happens this evening, the Connecticut primary has put politicians on notice: if they’re covering for the Bush administration’s disasterous decisions, they’re going to be in trouble.

What this challenge in Connecticut shows is that professional operatives in Washington DC no longer have a vice-grip on the political process. As Jefferson and our nation’s founders intended, the voters do.”
