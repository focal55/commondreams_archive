Democracy Rising to Distribute Thousands of Copies of Book in Front of White House
 

WASHINGTON - June 14 - Democracy Rising will have its troops in front of the White House beginning Thursday June 15, 2006 at 1 p.m. distributing free of charge thousands of copies of the book Cruel and Unusual: Bush/Cheney's New World Order (Norton, 2004) by Mark Crispin Miller.

"Hundreds of people come from all points of the globe to Washington, D.C. every day to see the White House," said Democracy Rising's executive director Kevin Zeese. "While they are outside the gates looking in at the building, we'll give them a little going away present. This book lets them have a peek as to what's going on inside the White House."

Zeese said that he learned earlier this year that the book was about to be pulped - so his group picked up a tractor trailer load for a fraction of the cover price.

"This book portrays President Bush as the lawless militarist that he is," Zeese said. "It will be our pleasure to distribute it to people from around the world wondering what is going on inside the White House."

Zeese said that volunteers will be distributing the book on the sidewalk in front of the White House to passers by.

"I figure that we can give away a couple of hundred a day," Zeese said. "We should get them distributed by the end of the summer."
