U.S. Military Deaths in Iraq Surpass 2500
Take Back America Conference Participants March in Silent Protest, Saying 'Not One More Injury - Not One More Death - Not One More Dime for War
 

NEW YORK - June 13 -
WHEN: FLAG DAY: Wednesday, June 14th at 3:00 PM

WHERE: Silent walk from Washington HILTON HOTEL (Connecticut Avenue and T Street NW) to the WHITE HOUSE (1600 Pennsylvania Ave NW)

VISUALS: Large solemn march with banners, signs and flag-draped coffins

In response to the death of the 2500th soldier in Iraq, participants in the Take Back America Conference (http://takebackamerica.org) will honor the keynote speaker Barack Obama’s closing statement—“Go Out and Fight for Change”—by taking to the streets to call for an end the unjust Iraq war. Hundreds are expected to gather outside the Hilton Hotel at Connecticut and T Streets carrying coffins, banners and signs. (Procession route: Connecticut Avenue South, East on K Street and South on 16 th Street through Lafayette Park to the White House)

The march will end with a rally at the White House. Speakers include Representative Lynn Woolsey (invited), Reverend Yearwood of the Hip Hop Caucus, and Colonel Ann Wright, who resigned her diplomatic post in protest of the Iraq war.

Endorsers: Gold Star Families for Peace, CODEPINK, After Downing Street Coalition, Progressive Democrats of America, United for Peace and Justice-DC, Democrats.com, Peace Action, National Congress of Black Women, National Organization of Women, among others.
