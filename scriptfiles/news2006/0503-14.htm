PFAW Voters Alliance Calls on RNC to Repudiate Hate-Fueled Congressional Campaign; Statement of PFAW Voters Alliance President
 

WASHINGTON - May 3 - The following is a statement by People For the American Way Voters Alliance President Ralph G. Neas, on North Carolina Republican congressional candidate Vernon Robinson:

"There is no room in mainstream politics for Vernon Robinson's bigotry and scapegoating. The Republican National Committee should immediately repudiate his tactics, demand that he pull his current radio ad ( http://vernonrobinson.com/media/miller_mariachi.mp3 ), and withhold all forms of support and urge Republicans to do the same.

"It may be tempting to dismiss Robinson as a fringe candidate, but he just successfully fended off two primary challengers and his prolific fundraising makes him a credible threat. In 2004 he raised nearly $3 million -- more than any other candidate in a crowded field -- in an unsuccessful bid for Congress.

"His disgusting and incendiary ads during that campaign secured him airtime on conservative talk radio and Fox News, enabling him to tap a narrow but lucrative niche of intolerant Americans. He has also received backing from the anti-immigration lobby, including Congressman Tom Tancredo's Team America PAC and Phyllis Schlafly's Eagle Forum PAC.

"Ken Mehlman and Republican leaders have a choice to make. Is their party open to all Americans or is it the party of scapegoating and bigotry?

"Despite nascent efforts by the RNC to reach out to minorities and gays and lesbians, the party continues to look the other way whenever it benefits from intolerance, which is quite often. If the RNC continues its silence on Robinson it will prove that the party is still trying to have things both ways."
