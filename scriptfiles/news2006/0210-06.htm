Raging Grannies Will Attempt to Enlist in Military on Valentine's Day
 

WASHINGTON - February 10 - What: Grandmothers, heartbroken and enraged over the enormous loss of life and the terrible injuries in Iraq, plan to enlist in the military. The old make wars and the young die in them; on Valentine's Day, grandmothers and others will demonstrate their love for this country and its young people by enlisting in the U.S. military in place of young people. The grandmothers vehemently oppose this war; their enlistment is not a show of support for military action in Iraq. But if someone must die, let it be the old, not the young. Too many have died. Too many have been maimed. Enough. Grannies say, "End the war now."

When: Feb. 14, Valentine's Day, 2 p.m.

Where: Armed Forces Recruiting Center, 8202 Georgia Ave., Silver Spring, MD

Who: PeaceAction Montgomery has organized this event, in conjunction with Code Pink and Raging Grannies in 12 cities across the U.S. PeaceAction Montgomery is a county-based chapter of Peace Action, the nation's oldest and largest grassroots peace organization. See http:/www.PeaceActionMC.org.
