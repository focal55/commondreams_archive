Amnesty Intl Expert Available to Comment on Expected Supreme Court Ruling on Hamdan v. Rumsfeld; Decision Expected by Month's End
 

WASHINGTON - June 27 - Amnesty International USA's Advocacy Director for Domestic Human Rights and International Justice Jumana Musa will be available to the media to comment on the Hamdan v. Rumsfeld case. The U.S. Supreme Court is expected to issue a decision before the end of June.

Musa has been Amnesty International's legal observer at the military commission hearings in Guantanamo Bay for the last two years and has observed the proceedings firsthand on four occasions.

Details:

WHAT: Provide comment on U.S. Supreme Court's decision on Hamdan v. Rumsfeld

WHO: Jumana Musa, advocacy director for Domestic Human Rights and International Justice, Amnesty International

WHEN: Prior to and immediately after the decision
