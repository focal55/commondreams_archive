Briefing on the Medicare Drug Benefit
 

WASHINGTON - January 17 - On Friday, January 20, Rep. Henry A. Waxman, Ranking Democrat of the House Government Reform Committee, will hold a briefing to examine how the new Medicare drug benefit is working. At the briefing, Rep. Waxman will release new information on problems with the Medicare program. Medicare beneficiaries, doctors, and pharmacists, as well as state health officials are scheduled to appear.

WHAT: House Government Reform Committee Democratic briefing on the Medicare drug benefit

WHEN: 10:00 a.m., Friday, January 20, 2006

WHERE: 2154 Rayburn House Office Building, Washington, D.C.

To see previous reports on the Medicare drug benefit, visit www.democrats.reform.house.gov
