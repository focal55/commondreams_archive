Amnesty International Legal Expert Available to Comment on Hamdan v. Rumsfeld Case; Supreme Court to Hear Oral Arguments Tomorrow
 

WASHINGTON - March 27 - Amnesty International USA's Advocacy Director for Domestic Human Rights and International Justice, Jumana Musa, will be available to the media to comment on the Hamdan v. Rumsfeld case. The U.S. Supreme Court will hear oral arguments on Tuesday, March 28.

Musa has been Amnesty International's legal observer at the military commission hearings in Guantanamo Bay for the last two years and has observed the proceedings firsthand on four occasions.

Details:

WHAT: Provide comment on U.S. Supreme Court case Hamdan v. Rumsfeld

WHO: Jumana Musa, Advocacy Director for Domestic Human Rights and International Justice, Amnesty International

WHEN: Immediately

Musa will be at the Supreme Court plaza after arguments and can be reached at 202-669-0293.
