Lawyers' Committee for Civil Rights Holds Annual Awards Reception to Honor Outstanding Lawyers, Law Firms, and Clients
 

NEW YORK - December 11 - The Lawyers' Committee for Civil Rights Under Law (“Lawyers' Committee”) will hold its 2006 Annual Awards Reception to honor the outstanding work and tireless dedication of the lawyers, law firms, and clients who work with the Lawyers' Committee on critical civil rights matters.

The 2006 Annual Awards Reception will honor:

Sidney S. Rosdeitcher, Esq
Paul, Weiss, Rifkind, Wharton & Garrison LLP
Receiving: Whitney North Seymour Award

Schulte Roth & Zabel LLP
New York, NY
Receiving: Robert F. Mullen Pro Bono Award

Jerome E. Hyman, Esq
Cleary Gottlieb Steen & Hamilton LLP
Receiving: Segal-Tweed Founders Award

Reilly Morse, Esq
Mississippi Center for Justice
Receiving: Edwin D. Wolf Award

Derrick C. Evans, Rose Johnson & James Perry
North Gulfport, Mississippi & New Orleans, Louisiana
Receiving: Frank R. Parker Client Award

WHEN:
Monday, December 11, 2006
5:30 p.m. - 8:00 p.m.

WHERE:
Weil Gotshal & Manges LLP
767 Fifth Avenue
New York, New York


The Lawyers' Committee is a nonpartisan, nonprofit civil rights legal organization, formed in 1963 at the request of President John F. Kennedy to provide legal services to address racial discrimination.

For more information on the Lawyers' Committee, visit us at www.lawyerscommittee.org
