What's Pushing Inflation?
 

WASHINGTON - August 2 - Rising prices are often blamed on rising wages, but that clearly can’t be the case in this period of sluggish wage growth.

In today’s Economic Policy Institute snapshot, economist Josh Bivens analyzes the contribution of various factors in today’s rising prices and finds that the one that bears most of the blame is soaring corporate profits.
