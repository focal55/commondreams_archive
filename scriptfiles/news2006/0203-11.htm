Expert Reveals U.S. Defense Plans for the 21st Century
Rumsfeld and the Pentagon’s future plans reminiscent of old wine
 

WASHINGTON - February 3 - On Tuesday, Feb. 21, the latest defense plans by Defense Secretary Donald Rumsfeld will be evaluated at an exclusive presentation by Winslow Wheeler, director of the Straus Military Reform Project at the World Security Institute’s Center for Defense Information. The presentation entitled, “The New Defense Budget and Rumsfeld’s New Plan: Old wine, bigger bottle,” will feature Wheeler’s expert analysis on the Pentagon’s Quadrennial Defense Review and plans to reshape U.S. defenses for the 21st Century.

With 31 years of experience working in Congress on national security issues, Wheeler is a veteran in the field. He is the acclaimed author of “Wastrels of Defense,” a monograph about Congress and national security and a nationally-recognized commentator on defense issues.
