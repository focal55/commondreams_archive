MoveOn.org Political Action Statement on New Democratic Position on Iraq
 

WASHINGTON - August 1 - The following is a statement by Tom Matzzie, Washington director of MoveOn.org Political Action, on the new Democratic position on Iraq:

The unity of Democrats on Iraq is good news for America. The public is looking for real leadership on Iraq and now they have it.

We applaud the Democratic calls for redeployment -- a big step towards what MoveOn members want: a responsible and timely end to the U.S. occupation of Iraq.

The Democratic call for redeployment also offers voters a real contrast with the Republican war without end.

In the coming sprint to the election, MoveOn members will work to hold Republicans accountable for the disaster in Iraq in their home states and districts.

Only with a change of leadership in Washington can we get a change of course in Iraq that will bring all our troops home and help Iraqis live in peace.
