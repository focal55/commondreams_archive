Live Audio Feed of Briefing About Passage of Landmark Massachusetts Health Coverage Expansion Available
 

WASHINGTON - April 14 - Massachusetts recently enacted landmark legislation designed to cover almost all of the 550,000 state residents who are currently uninsured. This historic legislation makes Massachusetts the first state to achieve near-universal health coverage.

The National Press Club forum on Tuesday, April 18, will bring together some of the key actors and analysts of the Massachusetts legislation.

Some of the issues to be discussed include:

  • What might be the national impact of this historic legislation?
  • What were the political dynamics that brought about this legislation?
  • Are the expected results of this legislation realistic?
  • How do the different components of the proposal fit together?
