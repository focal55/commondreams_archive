National Poor Peoples’ Organization to Announce Plans For Massive March During RNC
Poor People’s Economic Human Rights Campaign Plans to Bring Thousands to Minneapolis to protest “the War at Home”
Group Vows to March without a Permit Citing Right to Protest
 

MINNEAPOLIS, MN - February 11 -

What: A press conference will be held by members of PPEHRC to announce their plans for the March for Our Lives at the RNC in September. PPEHRC has organized the poor & homeless marches during the past 2 Democratic and Republican presidential conventions. The March is designed to highlight the lack of serious attention to poverty in America in the Presidential campaign. At the news conference, they will address why they have a right to March without a permit and about efforts to disrupt their marches in the past.

Who: Cheri Honkala, National Organizer PPEHRC; Ann Patterson, Minnesota PPEHRC

When: Tuesday, February 19th, 10:00 A.M

Where: Conference Room J, Sabathani Community Center
310-East 38th Street Mpls, MN
