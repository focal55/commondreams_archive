Prior to Pakistan Elections, Amnesty International Urges President Musharraf to Ensure Process is Free and Fair
 

WASHINGTON, DC - February 15 - In anticipation of Pakistan's general elections on Monday, February 18, Amnesty International calls on President Pervez Musharraf to ensure the process is free and fair. Important to the integrity of these elections are the reinstatement of an independent judiciary and the just treatment of opposition parties and candidates.

"Human rights violations and harassment of opposition candidates are widespread. U.S. Secretary of State Condoleezza Rice must wield her influence on President Musharraf to ensure that such abuses are halted," commented T. Kumar, Amnesty International USA advocacy director for Asia and the Pacific.

T. Kumar is an expert on Pakistan and is available to provide analysis and comments regarding the geographical and political implications of Pakistan's elections.
