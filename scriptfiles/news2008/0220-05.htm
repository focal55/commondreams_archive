A Look at DOE's FY 2009 Budget Request
 

WASHINGTON DC - February 20 - On Feb. 4, 2008, the Department of Energy (DOE), under Secretary of Energy Samuel Bodman, presented its Fiscal Year 2009 (FY 09) budget request of $25 billion, an increase of $1.13 billion over the FY 08 appropriation level. The National Nuclear Security Administration (NNSA) is requesting $9.1 billion, a decrease of 0.4 percent from the FY 08 appropriation. CDI Research Assistant Elliott Becker provides an overview of the DOE's budget request for FY 09.
