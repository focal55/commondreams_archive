The Abortion Ban and Reproductive Rights: A Briefing by Nicaraguan Human Rights Defenders
 

NEW YORK - February 14 - Since November 2006, Nicaraguan women’s lives and human rights have been compromised by a complete ban on all abortions, even those deemed necessary by doctors due to life-threatening complications. The Autonomous Women’s Movement of Nicaragua is working to protect women’s reproductive rights in Nicaragua by bringing attention to this issue at the international level.

WHAT: Come hear two human rights defenders discuss the state of reproductive rights in Nicaragua, in a briefing co-sponsored by MADRE (www.madre.org) and Human Rights Watch (www.hrw.org).
