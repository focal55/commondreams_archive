CREW Releases Most Complete List of House Members' PACS
 

WASHINGTON, DC - April 23 - Today, Citizens for Responsibility and Ethics in Washington (CREW) released the most complete list of House members’ Political Action Committees (PAC) available to date. CREW research found that out of 432 House members: 231 have PACs, 133 House members do not have PACs, 68 House members’ offices refused to disclose an affiliation with a PAC, 112 Democrats have PACs, and 119 Republicans have PACs.

Legislation passed last year requires lobbyists to disclose their contributions to any entity "established, financed, maintained or controlled" by a member of Congress, but members of Congress do not have to identify their affiliation with PACs.

The methodology and list are available here.

Citizens for Responsibility and Ethics in Washington (CREW) is a non-profit legal watchdog group dedicated to holding public officials accountable for their actions.
