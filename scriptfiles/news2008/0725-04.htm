Statement by Eric Burns On Michael Savage
 
WASHINGTON - July 25 - Media Matters for America Chief of Staff Eric Burns released the following statement today responding to Talk Radio Network's defense of Michael Savage's controversial comments on autism.

** For Audio/Transcript of the remarks, CLICK HERE **

"In 1722 words, Talk Radio Network could not explain how Michael Savage was taken out of context.

"They provided no context to his comments that autism is 'a fraud, a racket' or calling children with autism 'brat[s].' Nor is there context for his comments acknowledging previously calling autism 'a phony disease.'

"No amount of finger-pointing can mask what Michael Savage said. His words speak for themselves."
