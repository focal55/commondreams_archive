From Gaza
 

WASHINGTON, DC - March 3 - AFP is reporting today: "Israel vowed on Monday to keep hitting Gaza even as troops pulled out of the ... territory after clashes that killed at least 120 Palestinians."

Secretary of State Condoleezza Rice will travel to Egypt, Israel, the Palestinian territories and Brussels from March 3 to 7.

The following individuals are in Gaza, which is seven hours ahead of U.S. ET; phone lines to Gaza are intermittent.


Dr. MONA EL-FARRA
A physician and community activist in northern Gaza, El-Farra writes a blog titled "From Gaza, with Love."


AMJAD SHAWA
Shawa is Palestinian NGO Network coordinator for Gaza. The network released a statement: "One of the people killed by an Israeli air strike was one of the PNGO staff in Gaza. Amjad Almrety was 26 years old."
