US Military Assistance to Governments and Government-Supported Armed Groups Using Child Soldiers 2002-2008
 

WASHINGTON, DC - April 2 - The State Department's 2007 Human Rights report revealed eight countries where children have been recruited and/or used as child soldiers by government or government supported armed groups. CDI’s research has revealed that of these eight countries, the United States has provided six with military assistance since 2002.
