Actor and Activist Lynn Redgrave Co-Hosts Screenings of Fox Searchlight's Kinsey with Planned Parenthood Leaders
 

WASHINGTON -- December 15 -- News Advisory:

Lynn Redgrave will join Planned Parenthood Federation of America (PPFA) President Gloria Feldt and Planned Parenthood New York City (PPNYC) President and Chief Executive Officer Joan Malin for screenings of Kinsey tonight at 6 p.m. and 8:30 p.m at Tribeca Cinemas. A reception will be held from 7:30 to 9:30 p.m., and there will be Q&A sessions following each screening.

Released nationwide on November 12 to resounding critical acclaim, Kinsey illuminates the life and work of famed sex researcher Alfred Kinsey, whose studies of human sexuality electrified American society in the late 1940s and '50s. The movie was recently nominated for three Golden Globe Awards.

"Kinsey is a powerful reminder of the importance of healthy sexuality and the many impediments to attaining it," said PPFA President Gloria Feldt. "The film illustrates the revolutionary nature of applying scientific research to human sexuality, including the study of sexual norms. Though Kinsey himself was as complex and imperfect as any of the rest of us, his groundbreaking research has helped generations to experience pleasure without shame and moved our society toward a place where sexuality can be understood and embraced as a fundamental aspect of our humanity."
