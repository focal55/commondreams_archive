Vice President's Chief of Staff Received Early Briefing on Halliburton Contracts
 

WASHINGTON - June 13 - Rep. Waxman discloses that a senior Defense Department official briefed I. Lewis “Scooter” Libby, the Vice President’s chief of staff, and other senior Administration officials in October 2002 about the Department’s proposal to award lucrative Iraq contracts to Halliburton.- Letter to the Vice President
