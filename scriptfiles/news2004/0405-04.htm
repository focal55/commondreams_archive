Advisory: Wednesday Briefing on Sierra Club's Earth Month Activities
 

WASHINGTON - April 5 - Throughout “Earth Month,” the Sierra Club will be highlighting its efforts to stop the host of Bush administration policies that pose an unprecedented threat to the health and safety of America’s communities. Through general public education, legislative, legal work and TV, radio and print advertising, the Sierra Club is using the Earth Day celebration, traditionally a time of heightened awareness of conservation issues, to draw attention to threats to the environment and the solutions.

From allowing mercury to accumulate in the waters we drink and in the fish we eat, to cutting down our last wild forests and pushing a polluting Energy Bill crafted in secret, Americans deserve to know the immediate and long-term implications of the Bush administration’s destructive environmental policy agenda.
