As Iraq Veers Out of Control, New Ad Calls for Change in Leadership
 

WASHINGTON - September 16 - A new 30-second television spot produced by MoveOn PAC says that it will take a new President to get us out of the quagmire in Iraq.

The $230,000 dollar TV ad buy begins Thursday in Des Moines, Iowa, Marquette and Green Bay, Wisconsin, Wilkes Barre, Pennsylvania, and Dayton, Ohio and nationally on CNN.

"It is clear that President Bush gave no thought to what he was getting us into and has no plan to get us out," said Eli Pariser, Executive Director of MoveOn PAC. "Our troops in Iraq deserve a President with a real plan who has real credibility with the international community."

The ad features an American soldier sinking into desert sand while struggling to hold his rifle above his head. The voice in the ad claims that, "George Bush misled us into war with Iraq, sending poorly equipped soldiers into battle. He said "Mission Accomplished", yet almost every day more soldiers die."

The ad asserts that the quagmire sent ill-prepared soldiers into battle, and cost the American people $150 billion that could have been used for health care and education at home.

According to the Pentagon, 874 American soldiers have been killed and 6,703 injured since the President declared "Mission Accomplished" in May, 2003.
