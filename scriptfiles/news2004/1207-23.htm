United Artists for Justice in Ukraine Announce Benefit Concert in Support of Free and Fair Elections in Ukraine
 

WASHINGTON -- December 7 -- News Advisory: United Artists for Justice in Ukraine, is pleased to announce a benefit concert to be held Wednesday, December 8 at 8 pm at the Ukrainian National Home, 140 Second Avenue at 9th Street in New York City.

This event is sponsored by the United Artists for Justice in Ukraine, a group assembled to develop awareness of the current political situation in Ukraine. All proceeds collected will be donated to guarantee free and fair voting in the re-run elections, by providing assistance to opposition groups and organizations who are coordinating international election observers.

Scheduled to perform are Olya Chodoba-Fryz; Slavko Halatyn; IKA; Lubko Konrad; Oles & Adya Kuzyszyn; Stefko Stawnychy from Sicksided & Burned; and the Prolisok Choir of CYM Yonkers and NYC.

Formed as a direct response to the mass demonstrations in Ukraine demanding justice and freedom and organized by students and youth -- the United Artists for Justice in Ukraine is comprised of Ukrainian-American musicians who assembled to show support for and promote awareness of the opposition demonstrators through the medium of song and music.

For additional information and how to donate, please contact Natalia Labenskyj at natalia@nyc.com.
