Members Demand Information on Medicare Cost Estimates
 

WASHINGTON - March 2 - In response to reports that the true cost of the Medicare prescription drug legislation was concealed, 19 members of the House Government Reform Committee today demanded the release of the cost estimates prepared by the Administration. Invoking their rights to the information under the "Seven-Member Rule," the members demanded information that was previously requested by Reps. Rangel, Dingell, and Waxman in a letter to HHS Secretary Thompson last month.

The letter is available at: www.house.gov/reform/min.
