UNITY '04 GOTV ACTIVITIES: Starting Friday morning with Tom Joyner's "March to Vote Rally," Unity '04 volunteers hit the streets in full force determined to turnout a record number of Black voters in this election. Volunteers armed with flyers, buttons, and even church fans are urging people to vote early or on Nov 2, take identification and voter registration cards to the polls, and call the voter intimidation hotline 1 866 OUR VOTE for legal assistance if they experience problems at the polls.

In addition to the traditional canvassing and rides to the polls, the coalition has incorporated innovative strategies to get voters to the polls in 14 states including an Internet campaign, robo-calls from Tom Joyner, and a historic voting rights play. They have a mobile video truck broadcasting public service announcements from celebrities driving through Illinois, Louisiana, and Georgia, and, on Sunday, Oct. 31 clergy in Black churches across the country focused the faith community on voter protection during "Protect Our Vote Sunday." For updates go to http://www.unity04.net or http://www.bigvote.org.
