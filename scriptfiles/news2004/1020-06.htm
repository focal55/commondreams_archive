Early Childhood Education Crucial for Future
 

WASHINGTON, D.C. - October 20, 2004 - Moving Ideas, the leading source for progressive public policy on the net, has released an analysis of the importance of early education and childhood development that properly prepares children for school. The analysis provides resources relating to The American Prospect's new special report, "STARTING YOUNG: The Case for Investment in America's Kids."

"Research shows that if we reach our children early, it will pay off down the road both socially and fiscally," Moving Ideas Director Melanie Alston-Akers asserts. "It's surprising that we haven't yet given this area the resources and funding it needs."

The analysis includes an introduction by Senator Edward Kennedy (D-MA), articles by leading experts in early childhood development, links to leading research and analysis in the field including the Economic Policy Institute's new report, "Exceptional Returns: Economic, Fiscal, and Social Benefits of Investment in Early Childhood Development," and a list of organizations working on this issue.
