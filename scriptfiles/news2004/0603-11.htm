June 8 Congressional Renewable Energy & Energy Efficiency EXPO
 

WASHINGTON - June 2 - WHEN: Tuesday - June 8, 200, 410:00 am - 5:00 pmNews Briefing: 11:30 am ***

WHERE:

Caucus Room -Room 345; Cannon House Office Building U.S. House of Representatives, 1st Street & Independence Avenue, S.E.; Washington, D.C. 20515

WHAT:

The Seventh Annual Congressional Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency Caucus will bring together over 50 companies from across the United States representing more than two dozen renewable energy and energy efficiency technologies (see list below). Previous EXPO's have drawn nearly 3,000 visitors including members of Congress, congressional staff, Executive Branch officials, members of the media, and interested members of the general public.

Free ice cream and soft drinks will also be available to all attendees.

WHO:

The EXPO is co-sponsored by the members of the Sustainable Energy Coalition * and the House and Senate Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency Caucuses. **

* The Sustainable Energy Coalition is a coalition of over 90 national and state business, environmental, consumer, and energy policy organizations which collectively represent several thousand companies, community groups, and municipal utilities. Founded in 1992, the Sustainable Energy Coalition works to promote increased use of renewable energy and energy efficiency technologies.

** The House and Senate Renewable Energy & Energy Efficiency Caucuses are non-legislative, information-sharing organizations which include 224 and 33 members of the U.S. House of Representatives and U.S. Senate respectively.

*** The 11:30 news briefing will feature statements by four or more members of Congress with an introduction by Scott Sklar, Chair of the Steering Committee of the Sustainable Energy Coalition. Further details on speakers will be forthcoming.

The EXPO is free-of-charge and open to the public; no reservations or RSVPs are required.
