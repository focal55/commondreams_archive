Alliance for Justice President Nan Aron to Discuss 'John Ashcroft is the Intrusive Spy-der-man' Campaign; Radio Availability
 

WASHINGTON - July 14 - Alliance for Justice President Nan Aron will be available to discuss the online initiative, "Spy-der-man," starring U.S. Attorney General John Ashcroft in a flash animation as the intrusive super-villain invading people's privacy, stealing library and medical records, and accessing e-mail. Armed with the far-reaching USA Patriot Act, John "Spy-der-man" Ashcroft abuses his power and authority as our nation's top cop. The campaign calls for Ashcroft to resign.

This initiative is the second phase of Alliance for Justice's newly created Allies for Justice, a campaign strategically calling attention to critical issues threatening our justice system. The "Ashcroft is Spy-der-man" initiative is supported by a humorous flash animation, a Web site featuring substantive material on the USA Patriot Act, political cartoons, a petition drive and on-the- ground organizing. To view the flash and petition go to http://www.ashcroftresign.com.

WHO: Nan Aron, president of the Alliance for Justice
