Kate Michelman to Speak at National Press Club Luncheon Monday, Jan, 12
 

WASHINGTON - January 8 -Event: National Press Club Luncheon with NARAL Pro-Choice America President Kate Michelman

When: Monday, Jan. 12 at 12:30 (Speech begins at about 1p.m.)

Where: National Press Club (ballroom) 13th Floor, The National Press Building, 14th & F Streets NW , Washington, D.C.

Details:

Michelman is stepping down from her post as President of NARAL Pro-Choice America and will make remarks looking back on the changes and developments she has seen during the past 20 years in Washington. There will be Q&A.
