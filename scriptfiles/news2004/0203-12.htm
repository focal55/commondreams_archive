Fathers of Daughters Take Stand Against Boy Bashing
 

WASHINGTON - February 3 - Why are tee-shirts with messages that promote violence-this time against boys-a new fashion trend? Bullying, aggression and assaults harm kids enough without messages of hate, intolerance and violence getting more promotion.

That's the issue behind the grassroots advocacy campaign launched today by the pro-girl national nonprofit Dads and Daughters (DADs).

David and Goliath, a Clearwater, Florida-based clothing manufacturer sells tee-shirts with slogans like "Boys Are Stupid. Throw Rocks at Them!"

"These anti-boy messages do not promote girl power," Dads and Daughters' President Joe Kelly says. "They are nothing more than a cynical manipulation of faux 'girl power' designed to generate corporate profit."

Dads and Daughters urges anyone who cares about children to visit http://dads.e-actionmax.com/showalert.asp?preview=1&aaid=504 and send a letter to the manufacturers and distributors of David and Goliath merchandise, saying you want them to put our children's well-being ahead of their bottom line.

Dads and Daughters (www.dadsanddaughters.org) is the national advocacy nonprofit for fathers and daughters. Dads and Daughters inspires fathers to actively and deeply engage in their daughters' lives, and galvanizes fathers and other to take social action that transforms the pervasive cultural messages that devalue girls and women.
