Planned Parenthood Action Fund Makes Final Election Day Push with Rally to Increase Voter Participation Among Women
Planned Parenthood Action Fund President Gloria Feldt, Massachusetts Planned Parenthood Advocacy Fund President Dianne Luby and Activist Barbara Lee Featured as Part of Election Day Mobilization Effort
 
BOSTON, MA -- October 29 -- On Tuesday, November 2, Planned Parenthood Action Fund (PPAF) President Gloria Feldt will join Dianne Luby, President Massachusetts Planned Parenthood Advocacy Fund and Cambridge based philanthropic-activist Barbara Lee, for a pre-canvass rally to kick off Planned Parenthood’s efforts to Get-Out-The-Vote on Election Day.

The PPAF program aims to increase voter turnout among the more than 22 million single women who did not cast ballots in 2000. PPAF activists will hit the streets of New Hampshire on Tuesday, November 2 as part of a massive nationwide mobilization effort to ensure women in New Hampshire and across the country vote in record numbers on Election Day.

WHO: Gloria Feldt, President, Planned Parenthood Action Fund
