Rev. Jackson, Rep. McCarthy, Law Enforcement Conclude Coast to Coast Push for President to Renew Assault Weapons Law; Moms to Hold Event in Washington DC on Mother’s Day to Urge Renewal
 

WASHINGTON - April 27 - Moms to hold event in Washington DC on Mother's Day to urge renewal, 138 days until assault weapons ban expires

Tomorrow's press conference concludes a nationwide effort to urge support for a Mother's Day event in Washington and raise awareness about the expiration of the assault weapons ban. Over the last few weeks throughout the country, Million Mom Marchers, police chiefs and mayors have held over 40 press conferences.

On May 9th, mothers and others from around the country will converge in Washington to urge the President and Congress to renew the ban. This kicks off a national "Halt the Assault Tour" that will travel throughout the country and back in time for this summer's political conventions.
