Punkvoter.com Honors Gloria Steinem and Unveils New Print Ad Campaign
 

WASHINGTON - September 30 - Life long women's rights advocate Gloria Steinem joins Punkvoter.com tour members, Young Voter Alliance and Florida Alliance of Planned Parenthood Affiliates as they sound off about how critical the Choice issue is and unveil Punkvoter.com's newest print ad campaign.

WHAT:

Press conference with Gloria Steinem and members of punk band Anti-Flag

WHEN:

Sunday Oct. 3, at 2 p.m

WHERE:

University of South Florida

Marshall Center in the Student Lounge

4202 E. Fowler Avenue

Tampa, Florida 33620

Punkvoter.com is a voter education organization that is made up of over 200 of the nation's largest punk rock bands, 30 independent record labels, and tens of thousands of punk voters! Punkvoter.com works with many local organizations like the Young Voter Alliance and Planned Parenthood to hold educational events with guest speakers as our bands tour the country.

Anti-Flag will be playing a concert at 6pm for Punkvoter.com at The Masquerade, 1503 E. 7th Avenue, Ybor City
