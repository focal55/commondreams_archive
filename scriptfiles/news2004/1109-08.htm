Post-Election Analysis
 

WASHINGTON -- November 9 -- Please join Demos and Brennan Center for Justice for a real time analysis and discussion about key challenges to our democracy revealed in our recent election, including widespread and systemic problems with our balloting process, continued disfranchisement of eligible voters, and opportunities for reform.

Panelists include:

Ethel Klein of EDK Associates,
Jon Greenbaum of the Lawyers Committee for Civil Rights Under Law
Veronica De La Garza of the Youth Vote Coalition

Those who are not able to participate in person on the 10th may listen in by phone and submit questions via email during the event.

For more information on participating by phone, or to arrange an interview with a panelist or Demos staff-member, please email press@demos-usa.org with the subject line "Making Democracy Work."
