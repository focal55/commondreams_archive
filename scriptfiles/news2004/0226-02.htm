MoveOn.org Voter Fund & Allies to Call for Justice Dept. Investigation of Administration’s Illegal Use of Tax Payer $$ For Bush “Re-Election Ads”
 

WASHINGTON - February 25 - Under intense pressure from the White House, CBS corporate has buckled and is re-airing the Bush administration’s ad touting its controversial Medicare bill recently signed into law by the President.

CBS corporate has just rejected MoveOn.org Voter Fund’s ad on the same subject. In response, MoveOn.org Voter Fund is calling on the Justice Department to investigate the White House’s illegal use of tax money for Bush “re-election ads.”
