"Inequality Matters" Conference at NYU, 6/3-5
 

WASHINGTON - May 25 - Inequality Matters conference.

Where: Kimmel Center, New York University When: June 3 - 5, 2004

Sponsors: Demos, Inequality.org and United for a Fair Economy.

The Goal: to move the critical national problem of an ever-widening income and opportunity gap onto the front burner of American politics and public discourse -- within this election season and beyond.

Speakers include Bill Moyers and Barbara Ehrenreich, kicking off the event at 6:30pm on June 3rd, plus William Greider, Robert Franklin, Chellie Pingree, John Podesta, Meizhu Lui, Kevin Phillips, and many others.

Space is limited, advance registration is $45. For more information: 212/419-8647, www.inequality.org, conference@inequality.org.
