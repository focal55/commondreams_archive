WASHINGTON -- December 14 -- News Advisory:

-- Sinclair Broadcast Group Abuses Public Airwaves; Airs Nightly Conservative 'The Point' Without Counterpoint

-- Media Matters for America Leads Launch of www.SinclairAction.com

WHAT: Media Matters for America, supported by a group of public interest organizations will host a press teleconference to announce the launch of a nationwide campaign aimed at drawing attention to the conservative slant in Sinclair Broadcasting's nightly commentary titled "The Point." The vehicle for the initiative is a new website http://www.SinclairAction.com.
