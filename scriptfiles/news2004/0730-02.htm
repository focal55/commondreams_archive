New Report Highlights Need for Investigation of Iraq Funds Mismanagement
 

WASHINGTON - July 29 - A new report from the Inspector General of the Coalition Provisional Authority finds multiple problems in how the United States managed the Iraqi oil proceeds deposited into the Development Fund for Iraq, leading Rep. Waxman to renew his call for a congressional investigation.
