Defenders of Wildlife Launches Wildlife Adoption Campaign
 

WASHINGTON -- November 15 -- Defenders of Wildlife today launched a holiday season campaign to help save threatened wildlife that enables family and friends to send a wildlife gift “adoption” by visiting www.WildlifeAdoption.org.

“Defenders of Wildlife can help these species through this program, which enables people to educate others and share a love of wildlife with a special friend or family member,” said Defenders of Wildlife Vice President for Membership Steven DelVecchio.

Defenders is offering ten different animals for “adoption,” including a wolf, panther, polar bear, snowy owl, tiger, elephant, black bear, whale, sea otter, and dolphin. Adoptions are available for a tax-deductible donation of $25 and come with a plush animal toy and a certificate of adoption suitable for framing.

Over the next few weeks, the www.WildLifeAdoption.org campaign will reach out to millions of online holiday shoppers to protect these magnificent creatures and the places they call home for future generations. Wildlife adoptions are wonderful holiday gifts for family and friends, children and grandchildren, animal lovers, dog lovers, cat lovers.

Defenders of Wildlife is dedicated to the protection of all native wild animals and plants in their natural communities. Defenders focuses its programs on what scientists consider two of the most serious environmental threats to the planet: the accelerating rate of extinction of species and the associated loss of biological diversity, and habitat alteration and destruction. Long known for our leadership on endangered species issues, Defenders of Wildlife also advocates new approaches to wildlife conservation that will help keep species from becoming endangered. Programs encourage protection of entire ecosystems and interconnected habitats while protecting predators that serve as indicator species for ecosystem health.
