Progressives to Outline Plan to Take Back America at Democratic Convention; Tuesday, July 27 - Thursday, July 29
 

WASHINGTON - July 20 - Campaign for America's Future Co-directors Robert Borosage and Roger Hickey today announced that the organization will bring together progressive leaders organizing an unprecedented amount of political activity outside the formal campaigns this election year in Boston, Mass. during the Democratic National Convention. The Campaign for America's Future last month rallied more than 2,500 progressive activists together at its Take Back America conference in Washington, D.C.
