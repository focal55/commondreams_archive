Green Party Announces Logo Contest
 

WASHINGTON - April 7 - The Green Party of the United States is holding a logo contest to choose a Green Party logo in time for the party's 2004 National Nominating Convention. Titled 'Forward! 2004', the convention will take place in Milwaukee, Wisconsin from June 23 to 28.

The Green Party is inviting design professionals to enter the 'LET'S loGO CONTEST!' The deadline for submissions is April 25; the winner will be announced on May 5.

The winning logo will be featured on the party's website, merchandise, letterhead, brochures, and other marketing materials. The winner of the contest will be featured with a link to his or her website from the Green Party web site. The first place winner will also receive a Green Party tee shirt and a one-year subscription to Green Pages.

"The Green Party is the only political party with a clear vision for the future. We hope the logo contest will inspire creative people to design a simple yet artful logo that embodies our forward-thinking values and vision," said Kara Mullen, national fundraiser for the Green Party, who initiated the logo contest.

For other details about the contest, visit the website of the Green Party of the United States at http://www.gp.org
