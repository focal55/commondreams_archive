Academy Award Winner Errol Morris to Create Ads Starring MoveOn Members: “Fog of War” Director Takes Aim at Bush with Ads Starring “Ordinary Americans”
 

WASHINGTON - July 2 - Today, MoveOn PAC issued a casting call to its 2.3 million members to star in a new series of political ads to be produced by Academy Award-winning filmmaker Errol Morris, aimed at defeating George Bush in November.

The ads, featuring real Americans instead of actors, are expected to premier around the time of the Democratic National Convention this summer.

“I have been struck by the eloquence of ordinary citizens—about their stories, about their hopes for change. And I thought: who better to talk to the American people than other American people—in their own words?” said Errol Morris, director of the Academy Award-winning documentary The Fog of War.

MoveOn members can register on-line at: http://www.moveonpac.org/morrissurvey.html. Errol will select a dozen people from those who respond for on-camera interviews.

“We know that our members have powerful stories to share about the failures of Bush’s policies. We are delighted to have the opportunity to work with such an esteemed filmmaker to bring those stories into households across America,” said Eli Pariser, executive director of MoveOn PAC.
