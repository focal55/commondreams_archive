Statewide Protests of Cooper Execution Tomorrow: Schwarzenegger Denies Clemency
 

SAN FRANCISCO - February 2 - Civil rights, human rights, religious and entertainment leaders plan to protest the upcoming execution of Kevin Cooper tomorrow, after Gov. Arnold Schwarzenegger unilaterally denied clemency without a formal hearing, as is the custom in California. Full page advertisements in tomorrow’s New York Times and San Jose Mercury News call on the Governor to reconsider his unorthodox decision, which failed to consider the wishes of four jurors from Cooper’s death penalty trial who want the execution halted while previously-ignored evidence in the case is examined.

The ad, to run in the western edition of the NY Times and San Jose Mercury News, can be viewed at www.deathpenalty.org.
