Reps. Kucinich and LaTourette To Hold Press Conference Tomorrow With Country Music Legend Willie Nelson To Announce A Resolution On Family Farming
 

WASHINGTON - October 5 -

Press Conference TOMORROW, Wednesday, October 6th , at Noon on the Cannon Terrace

Congressman Dennis J. Kucinich (D-OH) and Congressman Steven LaTourette (R-OH) will be joined by country music legend Willie Nelson at a Wednesday press conference to announce a resolution to call attention to the plight of family farmers.

The press conference will take place tomorrow, October 6th at noon on the Cannon Terrace. The resolution will call on Congress to once again concentrate its attention on family farmers and their importance to our nation's economy.

Who: Reps. Kucinich and LaTourette, and music legend Willie Nelson
What: Press Conference
When: TOMORROW--Wednesday, October 6th at 12:00pm
Where: Cannon Terrace
