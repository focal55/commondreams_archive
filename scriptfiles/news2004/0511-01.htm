Chairman Davis Urged to Open Abu Ghraib Abuse Investigation
 

WASHINGTON - May 11 - Calling the decision by the Republican leadership to defer to the Administration’s internal investigations “an abdication of Congress’ constitutional oversight responsibility,” all 19 members of the Government Reform Committee minority urge Chairman Davis to reconsider his position on investigating the role of private contractors in the Abu Ghraib abuses.- Letter to Chairman Davis
