Campaign Finance Reform Leaders Hold Press Conference To Kickoff Fight For Shays-Meehan Bill Tomorrow
   WASHINGTON - July 9 - Leaders of the coalition supporting the fight for campaign finance reform will join Representatives Chris Shays (R-CT) and Marty Meehan (D-MA) and Senators John McCain (R-AZ) and Russ Feingold (D-WI) at a news conference to kickoff the fight to pass the Shays-Meehan campaign finance reform bill.

Who: Representative Chris Shays (R-CT)
Representative Marty Meehan (D-MA)
Senator John McCain (R-AZ)
Senator Russ Feingold (D-WI)
Common Cause President Scott Harshbarger
Democracy 21 President Fred Wertheimer
Public Citizen President Joan Claybrook
League of Women Voters President Carolyn Jefferson-Jenkins
Campaign for Tobacco-Free Kids President Matt Myers

Where: Senate Swamp (across from Senate steps)

When: 11:00 a.m. on Tuesday, July 10, 2001

For further information, please contact the Common Cause Press Office at 202/736-5770.
