Downturn Hits Jobs, Says Economic Policy Institute
 

WASHINGTON - September 7 - News Advisory:

Government figures released this morning spell out bad news for Americans as unemployment figures for the month hit a four-year high.

How bad are today's new numbers, what are the signs for the future, and what should our government's response be?
