Redefining Progress's Market Solutions To Climate Change Conference Postponed
   WASHINGTON - September 12 - News Advisory:

Redefining Progress has postponed the "Market Solutions to Climate Change" conference scheduled for Thursday, Sept. 13, 2001, from 8:30 a.m.-4 p.m. at the Marriott at Metro Center, 775 12th Street N.W., Washington, D.C.

More information about the conference will be announced in the future.

Our thoughts and sympathies are with everyone who has been affected by yesterday's tragic terrorist attacks.
