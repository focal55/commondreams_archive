America Supports Access To Contraception, President Bush Does Not
NARAL Poll Shows Support For Contraceptive Insurance Coverage
  WASHINGTON - June 20 - News Advisory:

An overwhelming majority of Americans support contraceptive equity for American women, according to a new NARAL poll to be released 11 a.m., Thursday, June 21 in Room HC-9 of the U.S. Capitol. NARAL President Kate Michelman will present the poll's findings and will be joined by Rep. Nita Lowey (D-N.Y.) and members of the House Pro-choice Task Force.
