Sierra Club Reaction to Senator Jeffords' Announcement
Statement of Sierra Club Executive Director, Carl Pope
   WASHINGTON - May 24 - Senator Jim Jeffords is a champion for the environment, regardless of what party label he wears. For years, he has fought ardently for clean air and water, battling against polluters, standing up to defend America's beautiful landscapes, and staring down those who put profits ahead of our families' health. The Sierra Club has long admired Senator Jeffords' courage and leadership on environmental issues.

It is expected that Senator Jeffords's decision to become an Independent will result in his chairing of the Environment and Public Works Committee and Senator Daschle becoming Majority Leader.

With Senator Jeffords heading the Senate's environment committee, the safeguards protecting America's air, water and lands will be in good hands. Senator Tom Daschle (D-SD) becoming Majority Leader in the Senate will be a positive step for the environment, both because of Senator Daschle's dedication to clean air and water, and because Senator Trent Lott (R-MS) has ignored environmental protection as a priority. With an Administration hostile to the environment, Senator Daschle's leadership will be crucial to blocking attacks to our clean air, clean water, and public lands.

Senator Jeffords has long been a leader on a wide variety of environmental issues. A few of his environmental achievements include:

  • Public Lands: Senator Jeffords was one of two Republican cosponsors of the Arctic Refuge Wilderness Act to protect the coastal plain of the Arctic National Wildlife Refuge from dangerous oil drilling. Additionally he has voted to protect our unique and beautiful national monuments.
  • Renewable Energy: Senator Jeffords has fought for funding for renewable energy programs that help reduce our dependence on more polluting energy sources.
  • Clean Air: In March, Senator Jeffords, along with Senator Joseph Lieberman (D-CT), introduced the "Clean Power Act," which, if passed, will dramatically cut power plant emissions of nitrogen oxide, sulfur dioxide, mercury, and carbon dioxide by 2007.
  • Sprawl: Senator Jeffords set up the Senate Smart Growth Task Force, which includes 24 Senators committed to curbing suburban sprawl. He has introduced the Post Office Community Partnership Act and is a cosponsor of the High Speed Rail Investment Act, both of which will help reduce suburban sprawl. He supported the Dairy Compact which helps preserve family farms and protects open space.
  • Family Planning: Senator Jeffords has been a leader on efforts to eliminate the Global Gag rule which bans US funding for organizations in other countries that use their own money to perform or educate about abortion.
