Congressional Leaders Join the Children's Defense Fund to Unveil a New Initiative for Children and Families
  WASHINGTON - May 18 - Senator Chris Dodd, Ranking Member of the Senate Health, Education, Labor and Pensions Committee Subcommittee on Children and Families, and George Miller, Ranking Member of the House Education and the Workforce Committee, will be joined by Marian Wright Edelman, Founder and President of the Children's Defense Fund, at a press conference at 12:00 p.m., Wednesday, May 23rd, on the Senate side of the Capitol to announce a major new legislative initiative for children, The Act to Leave No Child Behind.

Who: Senator Chris Dodd (D-CT) Representative George Miller (D-CA) Marian Wright Edelman, Founder and President of the Children's Defense Fund

What: A new agenda for children—The Act to Leave No Child Behind

Where: The Senate Swamp Site East Plaza, U.S. Capitol

When: Wednesday, May 23, 2001 Noon
