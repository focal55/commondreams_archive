Doctors Warn That Global Warming Will Have Severe Impact On Health In Massachusetts
PSR Releases 'Death by Degrees' Report at Press Briefings in Boston and Northampton
   WASHINGTON - February 27 - News Advisory:
 

EVENTS: Physicians for Social Responsibility (PSR) speakers will present key findings from Death by Degrees: The Emerging Health Threats of Climate Change in Massachusetts, a new report that alerts Massachusetts residents to the health effects of climate change. Press briefings will take place in Boston and Northampton

DATE:
March 1, 2001

TIME/PLACE:
9:30 a.m. -- Northampton -- Cooley Dickenson Hospital -- Grand Staircase

2 p.m. -- Boston -- State House (Beacon and Park Streets) -- Grand Staircase

SPEAKERS:

-- Ira Helfand, M.D. -- emergency room physician at Cooley Dickenson Hospital and former president of PSR.
-- Robert K. Musil, PhD is Executive Director and CEO of Physicians for Social Responsibility
-- Rep. Douglas Peterson -- Democrat representing Ward 3, Essex County
-- Richard Clapp, DSc -- Associate Professor of Epidemiology, Boston University
-- Cindi Luppi -- Massachusetts Climate Action Network/Clean Water Network

VISUALS:
A 15-foot "Death by Degrees" thermometer will illustrate the impact of global warming

Death by Degrees will be posted on-line on March 1. See http://www.psr.org/massachusetts

Interviews and Embargoed Press Release Available on Request.
