EPI Economists Available To Discuss New Income And Poverty Trends
   WASHINGTON - September 24 - News Advisory:

The U.S. Census Bureau is releasing its annual data on national income and poverty trends tomorrow, Tuesday, Sept. 25. Lawrence Mishel, the vice president of the Economic Policy Institute, and Jared Bernstein, a senior economist at EPI, will be available throughout the day to comment on the new findings.

Lawrence Mishel is the principal author of The State of Working America, EPI's biennial flagship report. Mishel, who joined EPI in 1986, has a Ph.D. from the University of Wisconsin.

Jared Bernstein, a labor economist and co-author of The State of Working America, joined the Economic Policy Institute in 1992 and has a Ph.D. from Columbia University.

The Economic Policy Institute is a non-profit, non-partisan economic think tank founded in 1986. The Institute is located on the web at http://www.epinet.org.
