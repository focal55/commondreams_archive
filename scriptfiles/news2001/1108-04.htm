ACORN to Release Study Documenting Concentration of Higher Cost Lending to Minority and Low-Income Borrowers
   WASHINGTON - November 8 - At 12 noon Eastern time on Wednesday, Nov. 14., ACORN will release a report on the disproportionate volume of subprime lending to minority and low-income borrowers. ACORN's report presents national data and that for 60 metropolitan areas, and it looks at both refinance and purchase loans. The analysis is based on data released by the Federal Financial Institutions Examination Council (FFIEC) about the lending activity of more than 7,800 institutions during the year 2000.

The report will be available to reporters prior to the embargo date. Following that date, it will be available at http://www.acorn.org. For more information, please contact David Swanson at (202) 547-2500 or acornnews@acorn.org.
