Ralph Nader to Speak at The National Press Club June 5
   WASHINGTON - June 1 - News Advisory:

What:
National Press Club luncheon

Who:
Ralph Nader, Consumer Advocate and Green Party Presidential Candidate, will discuss "Conceit and Consent: The Two Party Duopoly"

When:
-- 12:30 p.m., Tuesday, June 5
-- Remarks will begin just after 1 p.m., followed by a question-and-answer session

Where:
National Press Club, 13th floor of National Press Building,
14th and F Streets, NW
Washington, DC

Nader's documented criticism of government and industry has had widespread effect on public awareness and bureaucratic power. Since 1966, he has been responsible for eight major federal consumer protection laws including the motor vehicle safety laws, Safe Drinking Water Act; the launching of federal regulatory agencies such as the Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA), Environment Protection Agency (EPA), and Consumer Product Safety Administration; the recall of millions of defective motor vehicles; and access to government through the Freedom of Information Act of 1974.
