Statement on Homestead Air Base Victory
   WASHINGTON - January 16 - Statement of Carl Pope, Executive Director of the Sierra Club On Homestead Air Base Victory:

"The Sierra Club applauds the Clinton Administration for making the right decision on the Homestead Air Base. For six years Sierra Club members in South Florida have worked to ensure that our national parks are protected from this commercial airport. By firmly rejecting a commercial airport plan next to Everglades and Biscayne National Parks, the US Department of Interior is standing up for all national parks and saying that developments that threaten our precious wildlands are unacceptable.

"The legacy President Clinton leaves today will protect our nation's wild lands forever. With this landmark decision, President Clinton will be remembered as a protector of the Everglades and of our nation's wildlands. When you step into Everglades or Biscayne Bay National Parks, you will hear the impact of today's decision -- peace and solitude rather than the roar of jets screaming overhead.

"The people of South Florida are the real winners today. The President heard their demand that no airport can be built on or near the property. Requiring a mixed-use development is the right solution for South Florida and for our nation. It will provide needed jobs for the region without harming our environment and national parks.

"Unfortunately, the fight is not yet over. Private land use speculators who hope to take private gain from the ruin of our national parks may sue. We hope that they accept and support this decision, and we will fight for as long as it takes to protect what belongs to the American people: our national parks."
