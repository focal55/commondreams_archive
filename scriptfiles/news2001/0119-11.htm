PFAW Reviews Ashcroft's Distortions And Evasions At Senate Confirmation Hearing
   WASHINGTON - January 19 - News Advisory:

Report Available by 6 p.m. at www.opposeAshcroft.org

An analysis of Senator John Ashcroft's testimony before the U.S. Senate Judiciary Committee this week reveals repeated efforts to mislead the Committee about his record as a U.S. Senator and as Missouri's Attorney General and Governor.

The report can be downloaded as a pdf file from People For the American Way's special Ashcroft nomination site, www.opposeAshcroft.org. Also available at this site are PFAW's comprehensive reports on Ashcroft's public record and actions in Missouri and Washington, DC.
