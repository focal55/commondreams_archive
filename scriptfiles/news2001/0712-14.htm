Statement of Common Cause President Scott Harshbarger on the Defeat of the Rigged Rule for the Reform Debate
   WASHINGTON - July 12 - This was a victory tonight for reform leaders in Congress and a victory for the principle of fairness.

The defeat of the unfair rule proposed by the Republican leadership in the House shows that there is a solid bipartisan majority in support of Representatives Chris Shays and Marty Meehan and their effort to clean up the campaign finance mess.

The House Republican leadership should have learned the lesson by now that they can't resort to obscure parliamentary tactics to thwart the will of the majority.

Speaker Hastert said he would bring up campaign finance reform after the July 4 recess under a fair rule. He didn't, and a majority of the Members of the House recognized that.

Now the leadership must make good on its promise to bring up campaign finance reform under fair procedures. We congratulate the courageous 19 members of the Republican caucus and all the Democrats who said 'no' to the unfair rule and 'yes' to campaign finance reform.
