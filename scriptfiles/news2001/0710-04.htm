Gloria Steinem Hosts An Evening of Whoopi in Washington DC
   WASHINGTON - July 10 - Voters For Choice prepares for an outrageous night of comedy and politics on July 11, 2001 at the Warner Theatre. The proceeds from the evening will go towards the election of local and national pro-choice candidates in 2002. The r.s.v.p. list is a Who’s Who in Washington: Senator Hillary Clinton, Senator Barbara Boxer, Senator Lincoln Chafee, Representative Maxine Waters, Representative Mel Watt, Representative Barbara Lee, Representative Stephanie Tubbs Jones, Representative Tammy Baldwin, Representative Ellen Tauscher, Representative Jan Schakowsky, Representative Louise Slaughter, and former Gore advisor—Donna Brazile.

Ms. Steinem will be available for comments immediately before the show, and at the post-show reception with Whoopi Goldberg.

In 1984, with board members from Ms. and Planned Parenthood, Gloria Steinem created Voters For Choice. Over the years, VFC has become the largest independent pro-choice political action committee in the United States. VFC has raised millions of dollars for pro-choice candidates and helped send hundreds of supporters of choice to Congress and state offices.

Credentialed press should reserve their passes prior to the show.

For more information on Voters For Choice visit www.voters4choice.org, or call (202) 944-5080. Reporters on deadline may call Voter’s For Choice Executive Director, Maureen Britell directly at (202) 276-5882.
