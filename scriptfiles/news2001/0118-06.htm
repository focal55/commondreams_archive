Major Coalitions Call For 'National Day of Resistance' and Voters' Bill of Rights
   WASHINGTON - January 18 - On Friday, January 19th, six coalitions are coming together to declare January 20th a National Day of Resistance. Coalition representatives will denounce the Bush Administration as illegitimate, having taken power through a procedural coup d’etat, and call for broad-based electoral reform through adoption of a Voters’ Bill ofRights.

Introduced by the Pro-Democracy Campaign coalition, the Voters’ Bill of Rights includes such measures as strict enforcement of the 1965 Voting Rights Act, abolition of the Electoral College and clean elections with full public funding for public elections.

All participating coalitions are committed to non-violence.
