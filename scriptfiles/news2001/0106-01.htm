Democrats.com Statement on Presidential Vote Certification
  WASHINGTON - January 6 - The following statement was issued by Bob Fertik and David Lytel, Co-founder of Democrats.com:

"On behalf of the members of Democrats.com, we wish to express our profound gratitude and admiration for the Congressional Black Caucus and the individual Members of Congress who stood today before Congress, the American people, and the world to object to the certification of Florida's 25 stolen Electors.

Today, George W. Bush completed the penultimate step in his theft of the American Presidency. Bush's theft will be completed on January 20 when he takes the oath of office, to uphold the Constitution of the United States of America - which he has already broken by orchestrating a concerted effort to prevent the counting of every vote cast in Florida. Even worse, this oath will be administered by Chief Justice William Rehnquist, who was Bush's principal accomplice in the theft of the Presidency when he joined with four fellow Republican Justices in blocking the manual recount specified under Florida law.

We are extremely proud of the fourteen Members of Congress who rose in protest today: Corrine Brown, Eva Clayton, Elijah Cummings, Peter Deutsch, Bob Filner, Jesse Jackson Jr., Eddie Bernice Johnson, Barbara Lee, Sheila Jackson Lee, Carrie Meek, Cynthia McKinney, Patsy Mink, Maxine Waters, and above all Alcee Hastings. As a result of their efforts, history will show that members of the Democratic Party were not willing to accept the ultimate crime against democracy - the theft of the Presidency.

We look forward to working with the Congressional Black Caucus and other Democrats of conscience in opposing the appointments and programs of the illegitimate Bush administration. We look forward to joining with progressive allies in voicing our outrage at the Inauguration on January 20 and beyond. And we look forward to future elections, when we will do everything in our power to make sure that every voter casts a vote, every vote is properly counted, and the Republicans who stole this election are permanently removed from office.

To show our appreciation to the "Florida Fourteen," we are sending cards and flowers to their offices and we invite our fellow Democrats to join in this effort by visiting our Web site at http://trustthepeople.com."
