Seattle Coalition Goes to Quebec
No Hiding Place in Quebec for Representatives at Summit of the Americas
April 20-21 Trade Meetings
DC Press Conference TOMORROW--TUESDAY, APRIL 17 at 10:00 AM
   WASHINGTON - April 16 - WHAT: Press Conference with U.S. Civil Society Representatives

Tens of thousands of protesters are expected to confront Hemispheric leaders attending the Summit of the Americas in Quebec City, Canada April 20th and 21st. Civil society representatives from the United States, including union leaders, students, environmentalists, globalization and human rights activists will descend on Quebec City for the summit to show their opposition to the proposed Free Trade Area of the Americas (FTAA). The FTAA, a NAFTA expansion agreement, would include every country in the Western Hemisphere except Cuba.
