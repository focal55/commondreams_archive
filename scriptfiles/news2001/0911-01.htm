Cancellation of Press Conference Today
  

WASHINGTON - September 11 - The March and Rally for Global Justice coalition - out of respect for the working people and their families affected by the World Trade Center tragedy - is canceling our press conference scheduled for today, Tuesday, September 11 at 11:30 a.m. at the United Methodist Church building in Washington, D.C.

The press conference will be rescheduled for a later date. For more information, please contact Lane Windham (AFL-CIO) (202) 637-5018 or Celia Alario (Mobilization for Global Justice) (202) 969-1593.
