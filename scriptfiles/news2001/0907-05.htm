Citizens Trade Campaign Unveils National Fast Track Radio Ad Campaign, Escalates Grassroots Mobilization
Conference Sept. 10
   WASHINGTON - September 7 - News Advisory:

What:
Press Conference To Unveil Fast Track Radio Ad Campaign And To Highlight Coalition's National Grassroots Mobilization Against Fast Track

Scripts and tapes of radio ads will be available. Local newspaper coverage of some of the coalition's grassroots activities from the August recess will be available.

Citizens Trade Campaign:
The Citizens Trade Campaign is a coalition of environmental, labor, family farm, consumer and religious organizations promoting social and environmental justice in trade policy.

When:
Monday, Sept. 10, 2001, 11 a.m.

Where:
U.S. Capitol HC-9

Who:
Citizens Trade Campaign executive board members, including
-- Brent Blackwelder, president, Friends of the Earth
-- Bill Richardson, president, National Family Farm Coalition
-- Jennifer Esposito, legislative representative, Teamsters
-- Lori Wallach, director, Public Citizen's Global Trade Watch

For more information, contact: Mark Helm, 202-873-7400, x102
