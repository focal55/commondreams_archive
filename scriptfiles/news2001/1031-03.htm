Afghanistan: US Should Stop Using Cluster Bombs
In-Depth Analysis Cites Hazard to Civilians
   NEW YORK - October 31 - The United States should immediately stop using cluster bombs in Afghanistan because they pose an unacceptable risk to civilians, Human Rights Watch said in a backgrounder released today.

The backgrounder details U.S. use of cluster bombs in Afghanistan so far, as well as the history of the weapon's use in Kosovo and the Persian Gulf War.
