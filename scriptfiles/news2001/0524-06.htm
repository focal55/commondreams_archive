Statement By Congressman Bernie Sanders on Jim Jeffords Becoming An Independent
   WASHINGTON - May 24 - "I recognize that Jim Jeffords' decision today has been a very difficult one for him personally. I believe, however, that what he has done is in the best interest of the people of Vermont and of this nation. It is increasingly apparent that the right wing leadership of the Republican Party does not share the concern of most Vermonters regarding such issues as fair taxation, education, the environment, womens' rights, family-based agriculture and other important national priorities.

As Vermont's Independent Congressman for the last 11 years, I am confident that Jim Jeffords will -- in his new role as Vermont's Independent Senator -- be a strong voice in the Senate on the issues he has championed throughout this career."
