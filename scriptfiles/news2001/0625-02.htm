Bush Energy Plan is Dirty and Dangerous
Groups to Call for a Smarter, Cleaner Energy Plan at DC Public Hearing
  

WASHINGTON - June 25 -

EVENT: As the Department of Energy holds a public hearing on the Bush Administration’s National Energy Policy in Washington DC, U.S. PIRG and other leading environmental groups will present an analysis of the economic and environmental impacts of the Bush Energy Plan. The groups will call on the President to help bring clean, renewable energy alternatives and improved energy efficiency to the American public. Several of the groups will also provide testimony at the public hearing, along with expert research (which will also be available to media.)

WHO:
