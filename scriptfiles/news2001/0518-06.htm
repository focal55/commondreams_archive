Environmental Group Blasts Bush's Irresponsible Energy Policy
   WASHINGTON - May 18 - Friends of the Earth (FoE) sharply criticized the Bush Administration today for creating an environmentally harmful energy plan that favors the profit margins of the oil, mining, gas and nuclear industries over the health of the American public.

"The Bush-Cheney energy plan's attempt to link clean energy with dirty energy is a fundamentally flawed strategy that cannot solve our nation's needs," said FoE president Brent Blackwelder. "But such a plan should not surprise the American public given the fact that oilmen lead the administration, a former auto industry representative runs the White House, and a coal industry lobbyist has just been nominated to manage the precious resources of our public lands."

Blackwelder added that "The plan's attempt to tie conservation and energy efficiency measures to increased fossil fuel production is like linking cancer research to profits gained from generating more tobacco sales."
