Green Party Press Conference on Filing for National Party Status
Meeting with media in Santa Monica will follow the national Green meeting in Santa Barbara
  

WASHINGTON - July 18 -

WHEN: Monday, July 30, 2001, at 11 a.m.

WHERE:
The office of the Los Angeles County Green Party
2809 Pico Boulevard
Santa Monica, CA 90409
Office phone: 310-449-1882; office fax: 310-449-1810.

PRESS CONFERENCE CONTACTS:
Anne Goeke, Former Co-chair, cell phone 717-468-1880, ajgoeke@igc.org
Dean Myerson, National Organizer, 303-956-0827, greens@deanmyerson.org

The press conference will address the Greens' emergence as the third largest political force in the United States since the 2000 election, how the new "Green Party of the United States" will continue to grow and challenge the two-party corporate-dominated political system that has alienated "We the People," and Green plans for the 2002 and 2004 elections.

The press conference follows the national convention of the Association of State Green Parties (ASGP), to be held in Santa Barbara, at which delegates from all over the United States are expected to vote in favor of filing papers with the Federal Election Commission for national committee status, establishing the Greens as a national party.
