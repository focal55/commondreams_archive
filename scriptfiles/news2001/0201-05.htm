Women's Leader Expresses Disappointment, Pledges Vigilance After Ashcroft Confirmation
Statement by Judith L. Lichtman, President
National Partnership for Women & Families
  WASHINGTON - February 1 - We thank every Senator who did the right thing by voting "no" today. But the Senate has let the nation down by confirming a bitter opponent of women's and civil rights to oversee the Department of Justice.

There are no winners today. President Bush forced us into a divisive battle that made a mockery of his promise to unite the nation. The Senate committed a grave disservice by giving America an Attorney General whose record is shameful and whose motives are suspect.

But we gather here today, stronger than ever, to issue a warning. An historic coalition came together with record speed to oppose this nominee. That same coalition will apply all its energy and skills to ensuring that no nominee will take away our freedom to choose, roll back equal protections, or deny our civil rights.

We will be vigilant watchdogs who refuse to let the Ashcroft Justice Department, the Bush Administration, or the Senate take America backward. We will fight for a nation that promotes fairness, equality and justice.
