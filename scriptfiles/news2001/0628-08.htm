Statement By National Environmental Trust President On President Bush's Energy Speech
   WASHINGTON - June 28 - Following is a statement by Philip E. Clapp, president, National Environmental Trust (NET) on President Bush's energy speech:

The real energy vampires are the oil companies and the utilities that have been gouging consumers. If the President wants to be a vampire-slayer, he ought to be taking them on instead of championing their agenda. The President's commitment to energy conservation only goes as far as the day's staged media event. Does he really think Americans are going to be taken in by these sort of shenanigans?
