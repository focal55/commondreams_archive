Sanders Addresses National Progressive Conference on Eve of Inauguration
   WASHINGTON - January 19 - Congressman Bernie Sanders (I-VT) will address an array of national progressive leaders at the Progressive Challenge conference in Washington, D.C.On the eve of the Presidential Inauguration, Sanders has been invited to the conference to discuss how progressives must forge ahead with their agenda despite the fact that the Republicans now control both Houses of Congress and the White House. Sanders will be joined by, among others, Senator Paul Wellstone (D-MN) and Reps. Barbara Lee (D-CA), Dennis Kucinich (D-OH) and Jan Schakowsky (D-IL).
