Campaign Finance Reform Leaders Discuss Discharge Petition in House of Representatives
   WASHINGTON - September 5 - Common Cause President Scott Harshbarger will join Senators John McCain (R-AZ), Russ Feingold (D-WI), Representatives Christopher Shays (R-CT), Marty Meehan (D-MA), Democracy 21 President Fred Wertheimer, Public Citizen President Joan Claybrook, League of Women Voters President Carolyn Jefferson-Jenkins, and other reform leaders in Congress at a news conference tomorrow, September 6, at 3:00 p.m. in the House Triangle.

The Members of Congress and the reform advocates will discuss the effort underway in the House of Representatives to collect 13 more signatures on a discharge petition which would force the Shays-Meehan campaign finance reform legislation to the House floor.

For more information, contact the Common Cause Press Office at 202/736-5770.
