LCV To Release 'Bush and the Environment: A Citizen's Guide to the First 100 Days'
   WASHINGTON - April 23 - News Advisory:

At a news conference in Washington, D.C. on Tuesday, April 24, Deb Callahan, president of the League of Conservation Voters (LCV), will release LCV's assessment of the Bush administration's environmental record during its first 100 days in office. Reporters outside of the Washington metro area can listen to the news conference live via conference call by dialing 1-888-754-4430.

WHAT: News conference to release "Bush and the Environment: A Citizen's Guide to the First 100 Days"
WHO: Deb Callahan, president of the League of Conservation Voters
WHERE: National Press Club, Zenger Room, 13th Floor
529 14th Street, Washington, D.C.
DIAL-IN: Call 1-888-754-4430
