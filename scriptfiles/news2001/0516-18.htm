Taxpayers for Common Sense Expert Available to Discuss Bush-Cheney Energy Plan
   WASHINGTON - May 16 - News Advisory:

Cena Swisher, an energy expert with Taxpayers for Common Sense, a national budget watchdog organization is available for interviews on the Bush-Cheney energy plan this evening and on Thursday. Swisher is a leading expert on subsidies and spending programs for the oil, gas, coal and nuclear industries.

TCS will also be conducting a rapid overall assessment of the subsidies and giveaways in the energy plan. The analysis will be circulated as soon as it is finished on Thursday. For a copy of the assessment, please call Keith Ashdown at 202-546-8500 ext. 110.

Taxpayers for Common Sense is a non-partisan voice for American taxpayers. TCS is dedicated to cutting wasteful spending and subsidies in order to achieve a responsible and efficient government that lives within its means
