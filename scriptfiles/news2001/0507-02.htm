VPC Joins Hispanic Congressional Caucus Members To Release New Hispanics And Firearms Violence Report
   WASHINGTON - May 7 - The Violence Policy Center (VPC) will be joined by U.S. Reps. Lucille Roybal-Allard (D-CA) and Grace Napolitano (D-CA) to release Hispanics and Firearms Violence at a news conference tomorrow, Tuesday, May 8, 2001 at 10:30 AM, in the House Triangle, U.S. Capitol. Representatives from both LULAC (League of United Latin American Citizens) and ASPIRA will also participate in the event.

The report is a comprehensive overview of the effects of firearms violence on Hispanic communities on both national and regional levels. Hispanics and Firearms Violence details homicide, suicide, unintentional injuries, and firearms ownership.
