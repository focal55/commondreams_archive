Statement of NARAL President Kate Michelman on Senator Jim Jeffords
 
"Looking ahead, I can see more and more instances where I will disagree with the President on very fundamental issues. The issues of choice, the direction of the judiciary . . ."
 — Senator Jim Jeffords, 5/24/01


WASHINGTON, DC — "Senator Jim Jeffords has always been a principled supporter of a woman's right to choose. He has consistently placed his personal convictions above party politics. His action today exemplifies the commitment he has always shown to his core beliefs and the people he serves.

"President Bush and anti-choice lawmakers in Congress are out of step with the American public on reproductive choice. The President's anti-choice actions — from John Ashcroft's nomination, to the global gag rule on international family planning, to his conservative judicial nominees — have energized America's pro-choice majority and alarmed moderate members of his own party.

"The leadership of Senator Jeffords' party deserted him, but Senator Jeffords refused to desert his conscience, or the women of America.

"Regardless of his party affiliation, NARAL has always been proud to count Senator Jeffords among our friends on Capitol Hill. We stand ready to work with him to protect and defend the right to choose from future assaults by the Bush Administration."
