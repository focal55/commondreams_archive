The Fourth Annual Congressional Progressive Caucus’ Alternative State of the Union Event
   WASHINGTON - February 26 - The Fourth Annual Congressional Progressive Caucus’ Alternative State of the Union Event

Tuesday, 27 February 2001
1:30pm - 2:30pm
Room 2203 Rayburn House Office Building, Capitol Hill

Washington- On February 27, 2001, President Bush will deliver his first State of the Union Address. Please Join the Leadership and Members of the Congressional Progressive Caucus as they discuss the State of our Union as it applies to real American interests. The Progressive Caucus will address issues of equality and justice as they relate to the following issues, and demonstrate how the Bush Administration strategy fails to address the needs of the average American.

Economy
Education
Electoral Reform
Tax Plan
Health Care
Energy and Environment
Budget and Military
Food Safety.
