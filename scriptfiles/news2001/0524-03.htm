Planned Parenthood President Lauds Jeffords' Principled Stand
  WASHINGTON - May 24 - The following is a statement by Gloria Feldt, president, Planned Parenthood Federation of America, Planned Parenthood Action Fund:

On behalf of Planned Parenthood, and all pro-choice Americans, I want to extend my heartfelt congratulations to Sen. James M. Jeffords for his principled decision today, based on his conscience and his deeply held beliefs on a range of issues, to declare himself an independent. When it comes to the future of the U.S. Supreme Court and a woman's right to choose, Sen. Jeffords will go down in history as the man who saved America. We have always counted the senator among our true friends, and we look forward to continuing to work with him to protect the rights, the health, and the lives of women everywhere.

Planned Parenthood Federation of America is the world's largest and most trusted voluntary family planning organization. It comprises 127 affiliates that operate 875 health centers nationwide, which serve nearly five million Americans annually. For more information on Planned Parenthood, visit www.plannedparenthood.org.
