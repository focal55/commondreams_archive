Breaking News: Appellate Victory in NOW v. Scheidler
  

WASHINGTON - October 2 - "At a time when the country is still reeling from large-scale international terrorism, we have won another victory in the long fight against domestic terrorism at abortion clinics," said National Organization for Women (NOW) President Kim Gandy. "The Seventh Circuit Court of Appeals' decision in NOW v. Scheidler, upholds our nationwide injunction that increases women's safe access to reproductive health services and protects clinics and health care workers from violence and intimidation."

NOW v. Scheidler is a class action lawsuit under RICO, the Racketeer-Influenced and Corrupt Organizations Act, fifteen years in the making, which resulted in the first-ever nationwide injunction against violence and threats of violence at abortion clinics.

Full details on the Court's decision and NOW's response will follow shortly.
