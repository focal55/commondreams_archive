Planned Parenthood of New York City Views Ashcroft Confirmation as Retrograde Step for Reproductive and Civil Rights
   NEW YORK CITY - February 1 - The following is a statement by Joan Malin, CEO of Planned Parenthood of New York City:

"Planned Parenthood of New York City (PPNYC) is dismayed that the United States Senate has confirmed John Ashcroft as United States Attorney General.

"John Ashcroft has built a career on being one of the fiercest opponents of reproductive rights. His confirmation will prove to be disastrous for women's health and lives. He has labeled Roe v. Wade a 'miserable failure,' and stated that if he could enact one law, it would be to outlaw all abortions.

"As U.S. Attorney General, Ashcroft will be directly responsible for the enforcement of federal legislation protecting women's reproductive rights. We do not believe that Ashcroft will come full circle to enforce laws that make abortion safe and accessible to all women, including federal clinic access laws that protect a woman's right to choose without fear of violence.

"This election demonstrated that a clear majority of Americans support a woman's right to choose -- and Ashcroft's ultra-conservative positions clearly do not reflect the will of the American people.

"We applaud Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton and Senator Charles Schumer of New York for opposing Ashcroft's confirmation. We thank them for recognizing that Ashcroft's views are out of step with the majority of Americans and that his public record demonstrates that he is unfit to be the principal enforcer of federal civil rights laws, including those that impact reproductive freedom.

"PPNYC will remain vigilant to emerging issues affecting women's reproductive freedom and access to safe, effective and confidential care. We will continue to work with city, state and federal agencies to secure protection for a woman's right to choose."

PPNYC's mission is to protect women's reproductive rights and maintain their access to safe, effective and confidential care. PPNYC pursues these goals through our state-of-the-art clinical services, education and professional training, and advocacy. PPNYC provides birth control; emergency contraception; medical and surgical abortion services; pregnancy testing and options counseling; GYN care; colposcopy; STD and HIV testing, counseling and treatment; prenatal care; social service counseling, and help in obtaining Medicaid or Child Health Plus. PPNYC has centers in the Bronx, Brooklyn, and Manhattan. To make an appointment, call PPNYC at 212-965-7000.
