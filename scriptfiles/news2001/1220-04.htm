Campaign Finance Reform Discharge Petition Up to 214
  WASHINGTON - December 20 - Representatives Greg Ganske (R-IA) and Peter Visclosky (D-IN) signed the discharge petition today to schedule a vote on Shays-Meehan. Representative Eddie Bernice Johnson (D-TX) signed earlier in the day.

218 signatures are needed to schedule a vote on the bill.

"The three signatures gained today signals a huge surge of momentum for the campaign finance reform fight," said Common Cause President Scott Harshbarger. "The stage is being set for this important pro-democracy bill to be voted on soon after Congress returns."

"Americans can thank Representatives Ganske, Visclosky and Johnson for living up to the highest ideals of this country," said Harshbarger.
