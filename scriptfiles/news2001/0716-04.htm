Press Event for July 17 Postponed for Sierra Club and Labor Unions
  WASHINGTON - July 16 - Due to steelworker negotiations, the July 17 press conference planned for the Sierra Club, United Steelworkers of America, Service Employees International Union and the Union of Concerned Scientists has been postponed and will be rescheduled when all participants are available after the August Congressional recess.

This event had been planned for July 17 at the National Press Club.
