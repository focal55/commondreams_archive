Are Money, Mass Media and Elite Interests Undermining Democracy Worldwide?
Robert Kuttner and Leading Government Experts Debate These Issues During Reuters Forum at Columbia University
  

NEW YORK - April 17 -

WHAT: Topic: Rogue Democracies: Do Elite Interests Control Access to Real Political Power?

Debate: How much does money in politics limit meaningful democracy? Is this peculiarly American disease being exported to other democracies? Is money the equivalent of free speech? Can we constrain the political use of money without constraining democracy? Is mass media crowding out political democracy as politics becomes more of a vehicle for entertainment and less a matter of direct deliberation? Why are the advanced democracies pursuing essentially similar economic policies?
