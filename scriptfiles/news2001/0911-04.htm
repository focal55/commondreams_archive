Democracy Now! in Exile/KPFA Ongoing Coverage of Violence Online
   WASHINGTON - September 11 -In conjunction with KPFA, Democracy Now! in Exile will be providing ongoing coverage of events related to the violence in the US today, from DCTV, just blocks from where the World Trade Towers stood. Along with broadcasts over KPFA in Berkeley and KFCF in Fresno, online audio and other media can be found at the following sites:


http://www.kpfa.org

http://www.kfcf.org

http://www.wbix.org

http://www.democracynow.org

http://www.fsrn.org


Additional reports can be found at

http://www.nyc.indymedia.org

http://www.dc.indymedia.org

http://workingforchange.com click on Laura Flanders' Manhattan weblog


Community radio stations with KU satellite receivers can access the KPFA broadcast.
