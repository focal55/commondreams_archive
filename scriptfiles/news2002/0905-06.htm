Planned Parenthood Applauds Senate Judiciary Committee Rejection of Justice Priscilla Owen to the Fifth Circuit Court of Appeal
   NEW YORK - September 5 - The following is a statement by Gloria Feldt, president, Planned Parenthood Federation of America:

"Planned Parenthood is pleased with the Judiciary Committee's decision to reject the nomination of Justice Priscilla Owen to the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Fifth Circuit. As a member of the Texas Supreme Court, Justice Owen repeatedly demonstrated an activist agenda and a disturbing history of eroding reproductive rights.

"Federal appellate judges exercise enormous power in deciding cases that affect the lives of millions of women and their families. For that reason, individuals appointed to these courts must demonstrate a commitment to protecting those fundamental rights.

"Planned Parenthood hopes the Bush Administration now will nominate candidates who will affirm women's reproductive rights."

Planned Parenthood Federation of America is the nation's largest and most trusted voluntary reproductive health care organization. Planned Parenthood affiliates serve nearly five million men, women and teenagers every year.
