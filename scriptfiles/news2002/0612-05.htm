D.C. Medical Marijuana Drive Kicks Off June 13
MPP to Release New Zogby Poll Results
   WASHINGTON - June 12 - The signature-gathering drive for the Medical Marijuana Initiative of 2002 will begin Thursday, June 13, at 11:00 a.m. D.C. Councilmember Jim Graham and multiple sclerosis patient Candida Fraze, plaintiff in MPP's successful lawsuit to overturn the Barr Amendment, will join MPP Executive Director Robert Kampia for a press conference and petition-signing in front of the Henry Jay Daly Building (headquarters of the DMV and Metropolitan Police Department), located at 300 Indiana Ave. NW.

At the press conference, Kampia will release results of a new national Zogby poll, the results of which are expected to have a dramatic impact on the ultimate fate of the initiative.

WHAT: Press conference and signature-gathering; release of new national Zogby poll on medical marijuana

WHO: Candida Fraze, D.C. Councilmember Jim Graham,
MPP Executive Director Robert Kampia

WHERE: In front of the Henry Jay Daly Building (300 Indiana Ave. NW (entrance near the intersection of 4th and D Streets)

WHEN: June 13, 2002, 11:00 a.m.
