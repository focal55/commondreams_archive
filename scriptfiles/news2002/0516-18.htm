Planned Parenthood: 9th Circuit Decision Against ACLA Is A Victory For Health Care Providers Throughout the U.S.
   WASHINGTON - May 16 - Following is a statement by Gloria Feldt, president, Planned Parenthood Federation of America:

"Our Constitution has never protected violence, nor has it protected speech used to place someone in fear of violence. Planned Parenthood is pleased that the appellate court upheld those principles and recognized that the wanted posters and website were nothing other than an attempt to intimidate doctors and clinicians into abandoning women. This is a victory for women and health care providers throughout the nation. Planned Parenthood will continue to do everything within the law to protect our staff, our clinics, and our patients. This 6-5 decision emphasizes the importance of our lower federal courts and one vote on a court of review."
