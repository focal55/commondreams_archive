Civil, Constitutional, and Women's Rights Groups Launch Campaign to Defeat Michael McConnell's Nomination to Federal Appeals Court
   WASHINGTON - September 16 - News Advisory:

Leaders of national civil, constitutional, and women's rights organizations will launch a campaign to defeat the nomination of law professor Michael McConnell to the U.S. Court of Appeals for the 10th Circuit at 11 a.m. on Tuesday, Sept. 17. Some of the groups will be releasing reports analyzing McConnell's legal philosophy and his potential impact on civil rights, reproductive choice, and religious liberty. The Senate Judiciary Committee is scheduled to hold a confirmation hearing on McConnell this Wednesday, Sept. 18.
