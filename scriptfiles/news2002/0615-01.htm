Statement of Consumer Advocate Ralph Nader on the Arthur Andersen Verdict
   WASHINGTON - June 15 - The following statement was released today by Ralph Nader:

The jury verdict in the Arthur Andersen case should help galvanize the resolve of federal and state government officials to launch a major crackdown on corporate crime, fraud and abuse that has cost millions of Americans trillions of dollars.

Visit http://www.citizenworks.org for more information on The Campaign for Corporate Reform
