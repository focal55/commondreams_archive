Sanders To Hold Town Meeting on
"Corporate Control of the Media"
 

WASHINGTON - April 25 - Congressman Bernie Sanders (I-VT) will be holding a Town Meeting on "Corporate Control of the Media" at two Vermont locations featuring two of the country's leading experts on the issue.

Robert McChesney, author of "Rich Media, Poor Democracy" and John Nichols, co-author of "It's the Media, Stupid," will join the Congressman at a Town Meeting on Sunday, April 28th, 7:30 p.m. at the Unitarian Church on Main Street in Montpelier and on Monday, April 29th, 7:30 p.m. at Billings Hall CC Theater at the University of Vermont in Burlington.

Congressman Sanders is holding the Town Meetings to discuss the growing corporate control over the media and the increasing degree to which a small number of huge corporations decide what the American people see, hear and read.  The meetings are co-sponsored by the Department of Sociology at UVM and the Department of Journalism at St. Michael's College.
