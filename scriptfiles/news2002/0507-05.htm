House Members to Denounce Clean Water Act Rollback at Press Conference
   WASHINGTON - May 7 - A bipartisan group of House members, led by Reps. Christopher Shays (R-Conn.) and Frank Pallone (D-N.J.), will join environmental groups at a press conference tomorrow to criticize the Bush administration's decision to weaken a critical Clean Water Act regulation. Reps. Shays and Pallone will also announce legislative action to reverse EPA's decision, which will allow polluters to dispose of mining waste and other industrial waste directly into waterways across the nation.

What:
Bipartisan group of House members hold press conference opposing EPA rollback of Clean Water Act. Legislative action to reverse EPA's decision will also be announced by Reps. Shays and Pallone.

Who:
Reps. Christopher Shays (R-Conn.), Frank Pallone (D-N.J.) and others.

When:
Wednesday, May 8, 2 p.m.

Where:
2203 Rayburn House Office Building
