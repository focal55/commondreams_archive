Military Ban On Abortions Overseas Threatens Health Of Our Servicewomen
Rep. Loretta Sanchez to Offer an Amendment Ending Ban
  

WASHINGTON - May 9 - Following is a statement by Gloria Feldt, president, Planned Parenthood Federation of America, regarding the military ban on abortions overseas:

"This ban threatens the health and reproductive freedom of servicewomen who are courageously protecting our freedoms while serving our nation overseas. This injustice must end. Women stationed overseas depend on the high quality of medical care available at our military hospitals. This prohibition may force a woman to seek inferior medical care at a facility off base, or she may be denied care altogether.

"The law now not only denies women proper health care, it also unjustly prevents these brave women from exercising their legal right to choose while they are honorably serving their country to protect freedom on foreign soil."
