Field Guide To Enron Scandal
   WASHINGTON - January 11 - Common Cause has prepared a short summary of Enron campaign finance data, including total Enron PAC and soft money contributions since 1992 (by cycle by party); total Enron contributions to the Bush-Cheney Inaugural Committee and to Attorney General Ashcroft's various campaign committees, and top recipients of Enron PAC donations among current Members of Congress.

The summary is available at:
http://www.commoncause.org/publications/jan02/011102.htm
