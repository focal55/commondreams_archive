NARAL To Unveil TV Ad Comparing Rendell And Casey On Choice
   WASHINGTON - April 30 - News Advisory:

EDITOR'S NOTE: Substantive information in this advisory is embargoed until May 1 at 10 a.m.

NARAL President Kate Michelman will unveil NARAL's first TV ad comparing the records of Ed Rendell and Bob Casey, Jr. on the issue of a woman's right to choose. The launch will take place on Wednesday, May 1, 2002 at 10 a.m. at the Hawthorne Suites at the address below.

WHO: NARAL President Kate Michelman

WHAT: NARAL Rendell/Casey TV Ad Launch

WHERE: Hawthorne Suites At the Convention Center Room: Franklin A 1100 Vine Street Philadelphia, Pa. 19107

WHEN: 10 a.m., Wednesday, May 1

Only Journalists With Valid Press Credentials Will Be Admitted.
