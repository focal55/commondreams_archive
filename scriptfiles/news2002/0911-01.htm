National Church Leaders to Speak Against War with Iraq; Press Conference To Be Held Sept. 12
  

WASHINGTON - September 11 -

What:
Press Conference -- U.S. Christian church leaders urge President Bush NOT to initiate pre-emptive military action against Iraq. Church leaders release letter to President Bush in which they "oppose on moral grounds the United States taking further military action against Iraq now" and will respond to President Bush's speech to the United Nations General Assembly.

Who:
Rev. Dr. Robert Edgar, General Secretary of the National Council of the Churches of Christ in the USA and former Member of Congress from Pennsylvania, and other national church leaders.

When:
Thursday, Sept. 12, at 2 p.m., EDT.

Where:
Cannon Terrace, northwest corner of the Cannon House Office Building Washington, D.C.
