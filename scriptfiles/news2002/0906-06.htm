The Rainbow Ark Foundation's Global Wisdom Council Begins It's Global Trek in LA on 9-9-02
 

OJAI, CA - September 6 - Can we heal our Ancient Collective Broken Heart?


An Invitation to help build an Ark for our times;


World peace begins in the healed heart of each one of us.

Building an Ark for the Twenty-first Century
The Rainbow Ark Foundation ,the creators and producers of the Global Wisdom Council Trek have been invited to collaborate with the 2002 International Trek of the Tall Ships, in order to begin to create a highly visible, global ambassadorial journey of televised Global Wisdom Councils. The Trek began in May of this year in Okinawa, Japan and proceeded to Korea, then on to Vancouver, BC; Seattle, San Francisco, Los Angeles, culminating its journey in San Diego on September 11,2002. The Global Wisdom Council will convene for a council at sea, on the Europa (the symbolic Rainbow Ark) as she ports in Los Angeles, September 9th with the tall ships. This council will be focused on the theme of September 11th, One Year Later.

Just as The Rainbow Ark Foundation's October 11, 2001, New York Global Wisdom Council on board the Europa, then ported in Manhattan, focused on the issue of the media's responsibility in creating a culture of peace, the Global Wisdom Council will continue to gather peacemakers, and media makers as well as wise visionaries from around the world to contemplate the following questions:

  • What values do we bring on board the Ark for the Twenty-first Century in order to build a sustainable world that supports everyone?
  • What values do we let go of?
  • What is our responsibility in healing our own duality of the us against them beliefs that still predominates our way of being in the world ?
  • Can we finally heal our ancient collective broken heart?
  • What is the media's (our collective voice) responsibility in creating a culture of peace?

Global Wisdom Council Trek:
The Rainbow Ark's Global Wisdom Council intends to continue on to Europe in 2003 - England, the Netherlands, and beyond, continuing to facilitate and televise Global Wisdom Councils. This working council will not only reveal and share solutions, but will help implement these solutions through it's growing grassroots global networks. In this way Global Wisdom Council will help shift the power of television to a higher octave...to educate, uplift, heal and share our collective wisdom and resources.

This voyaging Global Wisdom Council is designed to galvanize eco-spiritually based citizens to build links and bridges between people and organizations that are already working to create a better world, so that we can synergize a greater global collaborative effort in helping to create a culture of lasting peace - and healing of the Earth.

Background on The Global Wisdom Council:

Creating inner and outer peace;
The Rainbow Ark Foundation's first Global Wisdom Council was held in the summer of 1998 at Zaca Lake, CA. Peter Russell, author of The Global Brain, Mark Dubois, co-producer of the International Earth Day Events, David Dellinger, one of the original Chicago Seven and dear friend of Martin Luther King, Lavina White, Haider elder from the Queen Charlotte Islands were some of the many visionaries in attendance.

These cross cultural, cross disciplinary, cross generational global Councils are meant to create the space not only to acknowledge our differences, but to learn how to respect them in new and creative ways that will inspire transformative action, helping to heal the fragmentation of our cultures, our generations, and our relationship to the Earth and ourselves. This work is a seed that is intended to help rebuild community in the world; a new global community that seeks unity while honoring diversity. This council seeks to answer the current state of the world challenges from the inner wisdom of the heart.

As a result of the events of September 11, 2001, we took the Rainbow Ark Foundation's vision to the next level by creating the beginnings of a voyaging Global Wisdom Council on board an Ark for the 21st century. This tangible symbol of global steward-ship and global- citizen-ship brings together those already working for peace, ecology and human rights to focus on the creation of a greater collaborative force for positive change.

On October 11, 2001, in New York City, The Rainbow Ark Foundation hosted a Global Wisdom Council on board one of the great tall ships of the world, the Europa of the Netherlands. This event was co-produced with The Lifebridge Foundation.

On board, people actively working for peace met in council with media makers to spend an entire day together to begin to understand, and possibly heal, our us against them, victim vs. enemy, beliefs which dominate our way of being in the world. We discussed the recent example of our collective trauma that occurred in Manhattan, exactly a month earlier, with the intention going to a deeper level of healing within ourselves our piece of the collective broken heartedness.

One of the goals of the Rainbow Ark' Global Wisdom Council is to influence media makers to pick up the "torch of peacemaking" through making commitments to creating theater, film, television, journalism and personal statements that will reach and affect their audiences. A key objective of the New York council was to engage invited media makers in a deeper conversation regarding their role as story tellers, in creating peace.

This council was so powerfully impacting that attending council elder and filmmaker, Andre Gregory (My Dinner with Andre) imparted the following to all of us that evening, "I will try to heal the disease that's within me, the disease of indifferenceŠso that I take responsibility to doing something in this world that will relieve the pain of this world so we wouldn't continue to be where we are today."

Other attending council elders committed to join the next steps of the Rainbow Ark Foundation, the Global Wisdom Council trek. One was Paul MacIssac, award winning filmmaker, journalist and current artistic director of the Play Back Theatre,NYC (an international theater troupe that uses improvisational theater to make socio-political statements) will bring the power of his troupe to the council experience.

Others that have joined the Crew include renowned ecologist and representative for the environment at the recent opening of the 2002 Olympics at Salt Lake City, Jean­Michel Costeau. Seeing the importance of this kind of global synergy, Mr. Costeau made a commitment to help connect the Rainbow Ark internationally to sympathetic groups and individuals..

These Councils in New York and now, in Los Angeles on September 9th, mark the beginning of a series of Global Wisdom Councils that will draw together some of the world's greatest visionaries from the media and theater arts as well as peace, environmental, social justice and spiritual initiatives. These councils will create a visible, global chorus of Wisdom Elders (elderhood not necessarily meaning old age but a sacred way of being in the world, whether 18 or 80) to help heal our wounded world, as well as navigate our newly emerging compassionate world society, at its chaotic beginning of the twenty-first century. A contemplative chorus that will hold a higher divine directive, helping to create a culture based on spiritual values.

As, Peter Russell (author of The Global Brain) and council elder from England attested about the importance of these councils is - " This deep sharing from the heart is what our society needs so desperately. We need to develop kindness, caring and compassion for each other. This is how the "magic" happens and the needed changes in the world take place."

This visible chorus will carry the voice of another conversation, currently unrecognized by our corporate media, that speaks of a desire for a new moral code that's beyond the "eye for an eye." This chorus speaks of tolerance and compassion as Gandhi taught us: "a willingness to hear the other side."

The Rainbow Ark's Global Wisdom Council Trek's long-term vision is an on-going international television program oriented towards the interconnectedness and reverence for all life. This program will address issues holistically to help us remember and understand that our thoughts and actions always affect others as well as the Earth. Through a series of televised councils, this program will create a global echo that spreads like ripples on a pond from a pebble whose lasting effects will return the power to where it belongs; back to the global citizens of the EarthŠas well as the responsibility for ourselves and the Earth

These Global Wisdom Council treks are being established to fulfill the mission of the Rainbow Ark Foundation: To help create a culture of peace through the fundamental teaching of Sacred Ecology. Sacred Ecology is based on the axiom that all life is an expression of soul and spirit. It has a deep reverence for life and an understanding that there is no separation betw een humans and other living things including the Earth. It asserts that the recognition of this fact leads us to re-embrace and honor our inherent unity while celebrating our diversity. In practicing Sacred Ecology, we seek to reawaken our sense of the interconnectedness of life from which true respect and honoring of all life can exist for all beings of the Earth.

The Rainbow Ark's Foundation thanks you for your consideration in participating in this sacred work. We would love for you to be part of the crew.

With great love,
