American Rivers Statement on Judge Harden's Ruling Against Mountaintop Mining
   WASHINGTON - May 8 - The following is a statement on Judge Harden's ruling against mountaintop mining by Rebecca R. Wodder, president, American Rivers:

Thankfully, there are still checks and balances in American democracy.

Judge Harden's ruling today is a much needed rebuke to an Administration that is so eager to cater to the wishes of the energy industry that it would interpret the Clean Water Act to allow mining companies to dump tons of rubble and garbage into America's rivers, streams, and wetlands.

The federal court not only threw out the Administration's policy on dumping waste in streams -- released just last Friday -- it also ruled that all mountaintop mining permits which have been issued by the Corps Engineers are illegal. By putting an end to the practice of blasting off entire mountain tops and dumping the rubble in stream valleys, this ruling closes the book on one of the most notorious and abusive techniques for resource extraction used in America today.
