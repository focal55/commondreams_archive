Pro-Choice Advocates Send Message to GOP That Access to Safe Abortion Protects Lives, Health of U.S. Women
 

WASHINGTON - July 20 - News Advisory:

National Abortion Federation Public Service Ads Currently Appearing in Philadelphia Subway System Focus on the Fact that Almost Half of American Women Will Have an Abortion in Their Lifetime

WHO:
-- Vicki Saporta, executive director, National Abortion Federation
-- Dr. LeRoy Carhart, of recent Stenberg vs. Carhart Supreme Court case, medical director of Abortion Contraception Clinic of Nebraska
-- Joan S. Coombs, president and CEO of Planned Parenthood Southeastern Pennsylvania
-- Lynn Grefe and Susan Cullman, national director and Co-Chair of the Republican Pro-Choice Coalition
-- Cynthia Waters, board member of National Abortion Federation and Long-Time Philadelphia Advocate for a Woman's Right to Choose

WHAT:
On the eve of the Republican Convention and discussions around the abortion language in the Republican platform, representatives of the National Abortion Federation, Planned Parenthood Southeastern Pennsylvania, and the Republican Pro-Choice Coalition will emphasize the need for abortion to remain safe and legal for all American women. Dr. Leroy Carhart, who recently won his Supreme Court case against Nebraska's so-called "partial birth abortion" ban, will talk about the many obstacles he has faced to continue providing women with safe abortion care, including the latest efforts of anti-choice Nebraska legislators to evict him from his building.

WHEN:
Tuesday, July 25, at 10 a.m.

WHERE:
