Sanders Leads Coalition In Unveiling Global Sustainable Development Resolution
   WASHINGTON - April 13 - At 12:00 Thursday April 13th there will be a press briefing on the following topic:

Sanders Leads Coalition In Unveiling Global Sustainable Development Resolution

Congressman Bernard Sanders (I-VT) will lead a coalition of House Members and Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) in unveiling the "Global Sustainable Development Resolution." The resolution - co-sponsored by Reps. Brown, McKinney, Kucinich, Evans and DeFazio - is an overarching statement which calls for, among other things, the United States to renegotiate trade agreements to include worker rights and worker protections. Additionally, the resolution calls for reforming international financial institutions, including the IMF, World Bank and WTO. At the press conference, representatives from several NGOs will speak, including the Institute for Policy Studies (IPS), Results, 50 Years is Enough and Friends of the Earth.

What: Press Conference unveiling Global Sustainable Development Resolution

Participating House Members: Sanders, DeFazio, Kucinich, Lee
