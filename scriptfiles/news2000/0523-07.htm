Statement of Democracy 21 President Fred Wertheimer on May 24 Democratic Party Fundraiser
  WASHINGTON - May 23 - The following was released today by Democracy 21:

The $25 million Democratic fundraiser on May 24 to "honor" President Clinton is an obscene display of big money and greed in American politics by the self-described party of the people.

Terry McAuliffe, the chief money hustler for the Democratic event, has managed to come up with a new "populist" symbol for the Democratic Party: a top price ticket of $500,000 for a blue jeans-and-boots barbecue.

The effort by Democrats to portray their May 24 fundraiser as an event for the "rank and file" is a joke and a fraud. This is a classic Washington influence buying and selling fundraiser, with special privileges for the "fattest" of the "fat cat" givers.

The difference today between the nuclear arms race and the political money arms race is the fact that we have reversed the nuclear arms race, while the political money arms race goes on with no end in sight.

President Clinton, the key figure for the May 24 event, is an historic figure when it comes to chasing money. As far as presidential legacies go, President Clinton has spent more presidential time raising more political money than any other President in history - and no one's a close second.
