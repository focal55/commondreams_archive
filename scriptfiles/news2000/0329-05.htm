WHAT:
Press conference to kick-off Feminist EXPO 2000 -- the first coming-together of the feminist movement in the 21st century -- and to brief the media on the themes and new ideas for achieving women's equality and empowerment that will be featured during the convention.

Produced and sponsored by the Feminist Majority Foundation, Feminist EXPO 2000 will be the largest strategic gathering of feminists in the USA in the last two decades. More than 6,000 feminists from across the nation and around the world, in conjunction with 575 national and global co-sponsoring organizations from 65 countries and scores of campus delegations will come together at Feminist Expo 2000.

Feminist Expo 2000 will feature four general assemblies; nearly 600 speakers, celebrities and entertainers; 100 symposia, roundtables and training sessions; and an innovative Exhibit Hall in the theme of "Visualizing a Feminist Future."
