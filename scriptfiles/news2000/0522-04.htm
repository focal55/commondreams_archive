Press Conference to Release New Report, Poll on National Trend Toward Part-Time, Contract, Temp and Other Contingent Work
  WASHINGTON - May 22 - News Advisory:

A new national network will release a groundbreaking report that puts a human face on and tells the untold story of the nearly three-in-ten part-timers, temps and contract workers in America today. The report details a shift in staffing strategies that represents the "back side of the economic boom": the unfair treatment, lower wages, bad working conditions and other inequities suffered by so-called contingent workers over their counterparts in regular (or permanent) jobs. The survey profiles a growing movement that is fighting back.

In addition, a first-ever poll on public attitudes toward contingent work will be presented.

What:
Press conference to release "Contingent Workers Fight for Fairness," a new report and national poll on contingent work.
