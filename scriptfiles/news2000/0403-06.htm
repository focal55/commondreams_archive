New National Survey Of Women Voters To Be Released
  

WASHINGTON - April 3 - EMILY’s List, the nation’s largest funding resource for federal candidates, will release its second national poll of women voters of the 2000 election, the EMILY’s List Women’s Monitor, at a briefing Wednesday (4/5) at 10:00 am at the National Press Building.

The EMILY’s List Women’s Monitor is a series of election year surveys that allows observers to assess the role of women voters in federal elections. This Emily’s List Women’s Monitor updates the findings found in January’s Women’s Monitor and examines Democratic and Republican strength among women voters. In addition, it examines the major electoral dynamics on the key issue of education.

Pollsters Celinda Lake of Lake, Snell, Perry & Associates and Geoff Garin of Garin-Hart-Yang Strategic Research, who conducted this national survey, will present their findings at the news conference. EMILY’s List President Ellen R. Malcolm will also speak.
