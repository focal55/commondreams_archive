CLCV Annual Environmental Scorecard Out Tomorrow
  

SACRAMENTO - February 8 - As California voters turn toward their first early primary election on March 7, the California League of Conservation Voters will release its annual voter's tool, the 1999 California Environmental Scorecard, which grades the environmental voting records of the state Legislature and the governor.

The Scorecard will be released online at www.ecovote.org at 10:00 a.m. PST on Wednesday, February 9.

The Scorecard grades the voting record of legislators and the Governor on 16 key environmental bills in 1999, and looks into the issues of the 2000 legislature. Also included is the Scorecard tradition -- a "Best & Worst" list that pulls no punches and takes no prisoners, naming names in the following award categories:

Best Freshmen Legislators

Green Badge of Courage

Best and Worst Bills

A Special Salute

Budget Heroes

A full copy of the Scorecard and the news release will be available at Ecovote Online, http://www.ecovote.org, on Wednesday, at 10:00 a.m.
