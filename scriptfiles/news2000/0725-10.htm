Nader 2000: Ralph Nader Joins Maryland Petitioners To File For Ballot Access
 

WASHINGTON - July 25 -

WHO: Ralph Nader and Maryland Green Party Petitioners

WHAT: Press Conference

WHEN: 11:00-11:45 am Thursday, July 27, 2000

WHERE: 151 West Street Annapolis, MD Suite 200

Ralph Nader will join Maryland Green Party petitioners as they file the final installment of approximately 16,000 signatures from registered voters to put the party's presidential ticket on the ballot. Nader will discuss barriers to third party candidacies and review the status of the campaigns' lawsuits for fair ballot access laws.
