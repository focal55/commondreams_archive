Youth Speaks Out!
  

WASHINGTON - April 14 - A gathering of energetic, charismatic youth activists, leading the battle for human rights, environmental protection, and civil rights will come together on Friday April 14, 2000, at 12:00 PM. They will be addressing global justice, plans for action, and revealing our vision for the future.

St. Stephen's Episcopal Church, 1525 Newton St. NW will be the setting for the young voices of the movement to come together. This will be a great opportunity for photographs as well as providing rare access to activist youth for interview or comment.

For more information please contact Loren Finkelstein at (202) 285- 6760 or the Mobilization for Global Justice Media Desk at (202) 789- 5419
