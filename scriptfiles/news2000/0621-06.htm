Amnesty International USA Opposes Imminent Execution Of Gary Graham
  WASHINGTON - June 21 - News Advisory:

Amnesty International, the world's largest human rights organization, is calling on the Texas authorities to stop the imminent execution of Gary Graham in Huntsville, Texas on Thursday, June 22.

Ajamu Baraka, Amnesty International USA (AIUSA)'s Southern regional director, is in Huntsville with an AIUSA delegation and available for interviews on the issue.

Bianca Jagger, member of AIUSA's Executive Directors Leadership Council, is also on the delegation and available for interviews.

To arrange an interview with Ajamu Baraka, please call Wende Gozan on tel: 936-291-2151 (Room 113) or mobile tel: 404-931-6073.

To arrange an interview with Bianca Jagger, please call the above numbers, or mobile tel: 917-570-3700.
