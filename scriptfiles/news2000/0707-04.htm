Doctors Warn That Global Warming Will Have Severe Impact on Health in New Mexico
Albuquerque Press Briefing, July 11
   WASHINGTON - July 7 - News Advisory:

Event:
Press briefing and public release of "Death by Degrees: The Emerging Health Crisis of Climate Change in New Mexico," a new report that alerts New Mexico residents to the health effects of climate change.

When:
July 11, 12:30 p.m.

Where:
Civic Center Plaza 1 Civic Plaza (stage area, near corner of Tijeras and 3rd St.)

Sponsor:
Physicians for Social Responsibility

Speakers:
-- Dan Kerlinsky, MD -- is professor of Psychiatry at University of New Mexico School of Medicine and president of the New Mexico chapter of PSR

-- Robert Bernstein, MD -- is an endocrinologist in private practice in Santa Fe

-- Gregory Green -- is a staff member with the National Environmental Trust

Visuals:
A 15-foot "Death by Degrees" thermometer will illustrate the impact of global warming.

Interviews, embargoed press release, and report available on request.
