NOW President Patricia Ireland Unmasks George W. Bush at Halloween Protest
  WASHINGTON - October 26 - "The spookiest thing this Halloween is the prospect of another Bush in the White House," said National Organization for Women (NOW) President Patricia Ireland.  "When the rest of the country is putting on their costumes, NOW will unmask George W. Bush to reveal the true threat he would pose to the country if elected president."

Ireland and local activists will protest outside the Bush-Cheney 2000 campaign office in Washington, D.C., at noon on Halloween.

What:      Bush Whacker Halloween Protest

Where:    Bush-Cheney 2000 D.C. Campaign Office Republican
                National Committee Headquarters
                310 First Street, SE
                Washington, D.C.

When:     Tuesday, October 31
                12 Noon

"George W. Bush is like a vampire who will suck our rights away," Ireland said.  "We don't need a president who will appoint Supreme Court justices who will send us back to the times of back alley abortions and we surely don't need a nightmare on Pennsylvania Avenue."
