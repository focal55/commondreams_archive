National Survey Exposes High Cost Of Payday Loans
  

WASHINGTON - January 31 -

WHAT:
News conference to release investigative report comparing cost of payday loans in twenty states. Compares payday loan stores interest rates and other practices around nation.

WHEN:
Tuesday February 1, 2000 10 AM

WHERE:
2220 Rayburn House Office Building

WHO:
Ed Mierzwinski, U.S. PIRG Consumer Program Director
Jean Ann Fox, Consumer Federation of America
Rep. John J. LaFalce (NY-29)
Rep. Bobby Rush (IL-1) invited

WHY:
Payday lenders offer cash-strapped consumers small loans until payday at astronomical interest rates. This report reviews the status of their ongoing efforts to change state laws that prohibit or restrict their triple-digit loans.
