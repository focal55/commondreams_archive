Statement By Al Gore Regarding Gas Prices
  WASHINGTON - June 20 - The following was released today by Gore 2000:

Statement By Al Gore Regarding Gas Prices

I am extremely concerned about the conduct of the oil companies that may have led to these unreasonable price increases.

Three weeks ago I called upon the government to investigate the oil companies for price gouging. That investigation of big oil is now underway, and I would like to see the investigation broadened to look at allegations of price fixing and collusion among the oil companies.

We now know that -- according to the latest available statistics -- oil company profits have increased by as much as nearly five hundred percent in the first part of this year. These enormous and unreasonable profits suggest that big oil is gouging American consumers.

We need to get to the bottom of this -- and we must do so in a bipartisan way. This issue cuts across party lines. Republicans and Democrats alike have spoken out against the oil companies and appealed for relief from the problem of skyrocketing gasoline prices.

That is why today I asked Secretary Richardson of the Department of Energy and EPA Administrator Browner to contact the governors of the states most affected by these high gas prices and convene a meeting immediately. We want to find out what these governors have learned about this issue and how the federal government can work with them to help solve this problem.
