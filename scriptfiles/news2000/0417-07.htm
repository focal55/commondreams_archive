'Vote Environment' Advertising and Grassroots Effort to be Launched by League of Conservation Voters
  

WASHINGTON - April 17 -

News Advisory:

A multi-million dollar "Vote Environment" advertising and grassroots effort will be announced Wednesday by the nonprofit, nonpartisan League of Conservation Voters Education Fund.

The group will preview a series of "Vote Environment" television advertisements that begin airing nationally this week and in Washington, Austin, Nashville and Atlanta, and soon in several other cities. In addition, the group will announce an environmental polling project being conducted nationally and in 23 states and an aggressive grassroots strategy for mobilizing environmental voters.

The purpose of the public education campaign is to inform and motivate voters about environmental concerns and to encourage candidates for all levels of office to address environmental issues.
