Statement of NARAL President Kate Michelman in Response to Ralph Nader on a Woman's Right to Choose
  WASHINGTON - October 29 - The following is a statement by NARAL President Kate Michelman in response to Ralph Nader on a woman's right to choose:

In an interview today with Sam Donaldson on ABC, Ralph Nader once again dismissed as a "scare tactic" the real threat to women's lives and health should Roe v. Wade be reversed. He blatantly ignores the fact that one, maybe two appointments to the Supreme Court by anti-choice George W. Bush is all that it would take for women to lose their right to choose.

It is clear that Ralph Nader does not understand, nor is sympathetic to the real danger to women's lives and health with the potential overturn of Roe.

The fact is, today, sixteen states are poised to make almost all abortion illegal in every circumstance, at any time during pregnancy. Women's lives and health would be at grave risk.

NARAL's concern is for all American women regardless of their state of Residence. Women should not have to struggle for their fundamental rights state by state, legislature by legislature or year by year.

Ralph Nader's dismissal of the right to choose as a defining issue in this campaign is both irresponsible and dangerous.
