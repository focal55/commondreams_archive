Presidential Candidate and Consumer Advocate Ralph Nader to Speak at Press Club
   WASHINGTON - July 17 - News Advisory:

Ralph Nader, the consumer advocate and Green Party candidate for President, will speak at a National Press Club Newsmaker luncheon on Tuesday, July 18. The famed "public citizen" reinvigorated the consumer movement with his muckraking books and with numerous task forces that grew from the early "Nader's Raiders." Nader will speak on the reasons for and challenges to a third party presidential candidacy, the issues he believes deserve a higher profile in the upcoming election and the importance of third party access to presidential debates.

The luncheon will begin at 12:30 p.m. Remarks will begin just after 1 p.m., followed by a question and answer session. Advance reservations may be made by calling Patricia Nelson at 202-662-7501. The club is located at 14th and F streets, N.W., one block west of Metro Center, on the 13th floor. The National Press Club website is http://npc.press.org.
