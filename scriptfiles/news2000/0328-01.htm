Opponents of Social Security Privatization to Hold Press Conference on Benefits for Women
  

WASHINGTON - March 28 -

News Advisory:

The National Council of Women's Organizations' (NCWO) Task Force on Women and Social Security announces the release of a report titled "Strengthening Social Security for Women." The report, a joint project with the Institute for Women's Policy Research, outlines ideas on how to address the solvency of Social Security and provides options to strengthen benefits for women. The report is a coalition document that reflects discussions held at the Working Conference on Women and Social Security held at Airlie House, Va., in July 1999. The NCWO Task Force on Women and Social Security is the first organization within the broader anti-privatization coalition to offer a plan that addresses solvency and puts benefit increases back on the public agenda.
