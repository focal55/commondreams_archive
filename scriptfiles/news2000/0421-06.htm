Celebrities, Activists, Lawmakers to Salute Rosie O'Donnell
  WASHINGTON - April 21 - News Advisory:

On May 1, hundreds of celebrities, activists and lawmakers will gather at Cipriani on 42nd Street in New York to salute actress/talk show host Rosie O'Donnell for her tireless work to keep children safe from gun violence. The program will open with the legendary actress Lauren Bacall, who will kick-off the festivities. Also featured will be former White House press secretary Jim Brady, U.S. Rep. Carolyn McCarthy (D-N.Y.), U.S. Rep. Nita Lowey (D-N.Y.), Handgun Control President Mike Barnes, and singers Chantal Kreviazuk and Leslie Nuchow.

O'Donnell, a champion of many causes, will be given a special award for her outspoken support for common-sense gun laws. The even will benefit Handgun Control, the nation's largest citizens' organization working to reduce gun violence. On Mother's Day, May 14, O'Donnell will be hosting the Million Mom March in Washington, D.C., a nationwide event in support of common-sense gun laws.
