Super Bowl Coke Demo Saturday Cancelled
  

ATLANTA - January 28 - Super Bowl Demonstration to Launch Nationwide Tour of Giant Inflated Coke Bottle Postponed Due to Severe Weather

WHAT:
The demonstration by three environmental groups planned for Centennial Park near the site of Super Bowl XXXIV at 1:00 PM Saturday, January 29, 2000 has been cancelled due to severe weather conditions. The campaign targets the Coca-Cola Company's refusal take responsibility for growing plastic waste and plummeting recycling rates. The groups want Coke to publicly commit to using more than token amounts of recycled plastic in the 10 billion soda bottles they sell each year.
