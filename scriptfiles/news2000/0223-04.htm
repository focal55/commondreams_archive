Doctors Warn Global Warming Will Have Severe Impact on Health in Maine
  

WASHINGTON - February 23 -

News Advisory:

WHAT:
Press briefing and public release of "Death by Degrees: The Emerging Health Crisis of Climate Change in Maine," a new report that alerts Maine residents to the health effects of climate change.

WHEN:
Feb. 28, from 10-11 a.m.

WHERE:
Hall of Flags, State House, Augusta, Maine

SPONSOR:
Physicians for Social Responsibility

SPEAKERS:
Robert K. Musil, Ph.D., executive director and CEO of Physicians for Social Responsibility
Peter Wilk, MD, psychiatrist in East Sebago, Maine, and president of Physicians for Social Responsibility
Lani Graham, MD, MPH, former director of the Maine Bureau of Health

VISUALS:
A 15-foot "Death by Degrees" thermometer will illustrate the impact of global warming.

NOTE: To view an advance embargoed copy of "Death by Degrees," see the Physicians for Social Responsibility Web site at http://www.psr.org/maine. Interviews and embargoed press release available on request.
