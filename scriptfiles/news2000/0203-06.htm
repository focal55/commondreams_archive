Major Foreign Policy Issues:
Austrian Neo-Nazis, Sanctions on Iraq, NATO and War Crimes, Israeli Nukes
 

WASHINGTON - February 3 -

MARTIN A. LEE, (707) 431-7932, devlee@ap.net
The author of "The Beast Reawakens," a recent book about neofascism and right-wing extremism in Europe and the U.S., Lee said today: "The inclusion of Jorg Haider's Freedom Party, a movement with openly fascist roots, in the national governing coalition in Austria is one of the most dangerous developments in contemporary European politics. Holocaust-deniers and neofascist ideologues are influential figures within the Freedom Party, and its dramatic rise to power has coincided with a sharp increase in racist violence in Austria, where shocking levels of anti-Semitism persist. There is reason to be concerned about a spillover effect on German politics."

KATHY KELLY, (202) 258-4958, kkelly@igc.apc.org, http://www.nonviolence.org/vitw
Coordinator of Voices in the Wilderness, Kelly and her colleagues have been on a fast in front of the Capitol for nearly three weeks. They have been threatened with fines and imprisonment for unauthorized delivery of medicine and toys to Iraq. She said: "Yesterday, the Red Cross confirmed the findings of each of our 30 delegations to Iraq -- hundreds of thousands of Iraqi children are unjustly paying the price for the U.S./UN economic sanctions." Also this week, 70 members of Congress sent a letter to President Clinton (prepared by Reps. Tom Campbell and John Conyers) calling for an end to the economic sanctions.

WALTER ROCKLER, (202) 942-5789, walter_rockler@aporter.com
Rockler, a former prosecutor at the Nuremberg War Crimes Trials and the first head of the Nazi-hunting unit of the Justice Department, said today of attempts by Canadian attorneys and other lawyers to prosecute NATO officials: "NATO policymakers clearly are guilty of war crimes in Yugoslavia, but the prosecution staff of the [International Criminal] Tribunal [for the Former Yugoslavia] is not independent, so they are not going to charge those who effectively appointed them. It's like asking administration officials to jail their boss."

SAM DAY, (608) 257-4764, samday@chorus.net, http://www.nonviolence.org/vanunu
Former editor of the Bulletin of the Atomic Scientists and coordinator of the U.S. Campaign to Free Mordechai Vanunu, Day said: "Yesterday, due to the efforts of Issam Makhoul, a Palestinian member of the Knesset, the Israeli people were exposed to the truth of the massive Israeli nuclear arsenal by one of their own. But it was a dark day for the Knesset, which refused to listen as Makhoul was cut off by the assembly speaker halfway through his presentation. Makhoul noted that Israel is sitting on a time bomb, something that could trigger a nuclear holocaust in the Mideast and could cause an environmental disaster."
