Statement of Democracy 21 President on Senate Confirmation of Bradley Smith to Serve on FEC
   WASHINGTON - May 24 - Following is a statement by Democracy 21 President Fred Wertheimer on Senate confirmation of Bradley Smith to serve on the FEC:

"A majority of Senators today, joining with President Clinton, have shown utter disregard and disdain for the nation's campaign finance laws by confirming Bradley Smith to serve on the Federal Election Commission.

"Mr. Smith has made it clear that he firmly opposes the nation's campaign finance laws, believes they are unconstitutional and supports their repeal.

"Giving an individual with Mr. Smith's views' responsibility for administering and enforcing the federal campaign finance laws makes a mockery of the laws.

"Our nation's campaign finance laws are in total disrepair today, in large measure because the FEC has failed to properly administer and enforce the laws. By confirming Mr. Smith to serve on the FEC the Senate has added insult to injury.

"Mr. Smith's confirmation to the Federal Election Commission is the final nail in the coffin for the FEC, and establishes beyond a shadow of a doubt that it's time to establish a new system for enforcing the nation's campaign finance laws."
