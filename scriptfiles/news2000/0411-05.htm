As Tax Day Looms, New Report Details Billions Taxpayers Spend To Subsidize Sprawl
   WASHINGTON - April 11 - With tax day right around the corner, the Sierra Club will release a new, national report detailing the ways Americans' tax dollars are wasted to subsidize suburban sprawl. On Thursday, April 13, the Sierra Club will announce its findings at a press conference in front of the Internal Revenue Service headquarters.

"Sprawl Costs Us All: How Your Taxes Fuel Suburban Sprawl," reveals how sprawling development is not only destroying our environment, but is draining our pocketbooks and increasing our taxes. The report details how subsidies built into the development process-- and from numerous government programs-- encourage the sprawling development that is eating up the American countryside and draining the life from cities and small towns alike.

WHAT: Press Conference to release a new, national report on suburban sprawl's costs to taxpayers.
