Statement of Common Cause President Scott Harshbarger On Confirmation Of Bradley A. Smith To The FEC
 

WASHINGTON - May 24 - Imagine a criminal justice system where police refuse to arrest thieves or white collar crooks. That's not all that different from the system we currently have at the Federal Election Commission, where key campaign finance laws go largely unenforced. By confirming Brad Smith to a seat on the FEC the Senate has made a weak and ineffective agency even worse - at a time when a strong and independent FEC is needed now more than ever.

As a former state Attorney General, I know that enforcement is a key ingredient to having any law - even inadequate ones - have any meaning. It's hard to see how Brad Smith is going to effectively enforce laws he thinks should be repealed because he thinks they are unconstitutional.

The vote today on Smith in the Senate shows how out of touch some Senators are when it comes to understanding the American people's frustration with a campaign finance system spun out of control.
