Feminist Majority Foundation Hosts Expo 2000 for Women's Empowerment
  WASHINGTON - March 23 - News Advisory:

The Feminist Majority Foundation and over 400 co-sponsors from all over the United States and from every region of the world, including renowned feminist leaders, politicians, scholars, authors, activists, celebrities, entertainers, writers and musicians, will host Feminist Expo 2000 For Women's Empowerment to share strategies and fuel the women's movement, its ideas and vision for the future.

WHO:
Thousands of feminists from around the world, over 493 national and global women's organizations from 62 countries and scores of campus delegations will come together at Feminist Expo 2000. This historic event will bring together feminists from every sector of society -- media, law, medicine and health care, arts, politics, business, e-commerce, technology, education, public service, trade unions, non-profits' services, sports, entertainment, advocacy, religion and philanthropy. Featured speakers include Eleanor Smeal, Gloria Steinem, Patricia Ireland, Kate Michelman, Senators Barbara Boxer and Barbara Mikulski, Congresswomen Tammy Baldwin, Patsy Mink and Carolyn Maloney, Lieutenant Governor Kathleen Kennedy Townsend, Ambassador Carol Moseley Braun, Laura Dern, Tyne Daly, Dar Williams, Geraldine Laybourne, chairperson and CEO of Oxygen Media, Ellen Pack, founder and general manager of Women.com, Meredith Wagner of Lifetime, Dolores Huerta (UFW) and Linda Chavez Thompson (AFL-CIO) among others. See www.feminist.org for the complete list of speakers.

WHAT:
Feminist Expo 2000, the first coming-together of the feminist movement in the 21st century, will be the largest strategic gathering of feminists in the last two decades. Topics covered will include:
-- Elections/Politics
-- Abortion/Reproductive Rights
-- Breaking the Barriers to Equality
-- Global WTO, World Bank, IMF, Beijing Plus-5, Gender Apartheid in Afghanistan
-- Women and Violence
-- Health Trends for Women in the 21st Century
-- Future of Feminism
-- Women in the Media/Technology
-- Money, Business and Investing

Feminist Expo 2000 will feature four general assemblies; 350 speakers, celebrities and entertainers; 100 symposia, roundtables and training sessions; and an innovative Exhibit Hall in the theme of "Visualizing a Feminist Future."

WHERE:
Baltimore Convention Center on the Inner Harbor in Baltimore, Md.

WHEN:
March 31-April 2
Press check-in begins at 8 a.m. on Friday, March 31

Pre Expo interviews with Eleanor Smeal, president of the Feminist Majority Foundation, and other key speakers are available upon request.
