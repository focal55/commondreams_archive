American Rivers to Announce the Nation's Most Endangered River
 

WASHINGTON - February 28 -

News Advisory:

American Rivers will announce the most endangered river in the country at a press event in Washington, D.C. on March 9.

The event will begin with a press conference at the National Press Club and culminate with a walk and presentation at the White House.

DATE:
March 9

PLACE:
National Press Club, Washington, D.C.

TIME:
9:15 a.m.
