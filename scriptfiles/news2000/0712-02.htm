NARAL To Release Vice Presidential Report Cards
  WASHINGTON - July 12 - News Advisory:

NARAL, the nation's leading pro-choice organization, will release its report card on the 20 leading Republican and Democratic Vice Presidential hopefuls at a press conference at 10 a.m. on Thursday, July 13, at the NARAL headquarters in Washington, D.C. NARAL Executive Vice President Alice Germond will report NARAL's grades, which are based on each candidates' public record and statements on the issue of reproductive freedom and choice. Grades of A through F and "comments" will be given. The candidates' records will also be available.

WHO: Alice Germond, NARAL executive vice president

WHAT: NARAL grades potential vice presidential nominees and their records on choice

WHERE: NARAL, 1156 15th Street NW, Suite 700 Washington, DC

WHEN: Thursday, July 13, 10 a.m.

Note: Only journalists with valid press credentials will be admitted.
