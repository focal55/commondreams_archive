Democracy 21, Common Cause to Announce Major Campaign Finance Law Enforcement Action April 4
 

WASHINGTON - March 31 -

News Advisory:

Common Cause and Democracy 21 will announce a major campaign finance law enforcement action at 10 a.m. on Tuesday, April 4 at the J.W. Marriott Hotel, 1331 Pennsylvania Ave., N.W. in Washington, D.C., Treasury Room, Mezzanine Level.

The action to be announced by the two organizations will involve campaign finance activities in a high-profile federal election. Democracy 21 President Fred Wertheimer and Common Cause President Scott Harshbarger will make the announcement for the groups.
