Nader To Speak At U.S. Agricultural Communicators' Congress
  

WASHINGTON - July 24 -

WHAT: U.S. Agricultural Communicators' Congress

WHO: Ralph Nader, Nearly 500 Agricultural Communications Professionals

WHEN: 11:30am - 12:00pm, July 25, 2000

WHERE: J.W. Marriott Hotel, 14th and Penn. Main Ballroom

Ralph Nader will criticize corporate agribusiness and discuss the findings of the recent report, "A Food and Agriculture Policy for the 21st Century" from the Organization for Competitive Markets. Addressing nearly 500 agricultural communications professionals, Nader will outline present farm problems which can be traced to massive consolidation and vertical integration within the farm industry.
