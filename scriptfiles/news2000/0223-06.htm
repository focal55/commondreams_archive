Groups to Announce '25 Biggest Water Boondoggles' March 2
  

WASHINGTON - February 23 -

News Advisory:

Taxpayers for Common Sense and National Wildlife Federation will release the first ever report on the most wasteful water projects in the nation. The report, Troubled Waters: Congress, the Corps of Engineers, and Wasteful Water Projects will be released at a March 2 press conference to be held at the National Press Club in Washington, D.C. at 9:30 a.m.
