ZPG News Conference on Wal-Mart's Emergency Contraceptive Ban
   WASHINGTON - May 5 - News Advisory:

When:
Tuesday, May 9 at 12:30 p.m.

Where:
Wal-Mart, 600 Showers Drive, Mountain View, Calif.

Speakers:
Fmr. U.S. Congressman Peter H. Kostmayer of Zero Population Growth and health professionals

Background:
They may stock Viagra, but if you're a Bay Area woman who needs a prescription for emergency contraceptives filled at your local Wal-Mart, you can forget it!

Preven and Plan B -- the only FDA-approved emergency contraceptives -- are banned from Wal-Mart shelves.

Join veteran family planning campaigner Peter H. Kostmayer and health professionals in calling-on the drug-store giant to reverse their controversial anti-women's rights "business decision."

Note to Television News Assignment Editors
There will be a huge color backdrop at the news conference with the legend "Wal-Mart is rolling back Bay Area women's rights and we're NOT smiling"

Zero Population Growth (ZPG) is America's premier nonprofit environmental organization working to educate people about the impact of rapid population growth. ZPG believes that by tackling global population growth, we can improve and sustain a better quality of life for everyone. ZPG's Executive Director Peter H. Kostmayer is a former seven-term U.S. Congressman and senior Clinton-Gore Administration E.P.A. official.
