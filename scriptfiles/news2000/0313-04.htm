Union of Concerned Scientists to Release Report on Pollution Peformance Ranking of World's Automakers
  

WASHINGTON - March 13 -

News Advisory:

It's hard to turn on the television without seeing commercials of SUVs communing with nature, but which automakers truly walk their talk when it comes to the environment? Join the Union of Concerned Scientists in Detroit on Wednesday, March 15, for the unveiling of the first pollution performance ranking of the world's Automakers. UCS analysts will answer these questions and more:

How do Detroit's automakers fare on the environment compared to their claims and their competitors?

Will Ford's ranking live up to Bill Clay Ford's environmental pledges?

How can consumers use the report to make the most informed buying decisions?

What can automakers and politicians do to ensure the public is offered the cleanest cars and trucks possible?
