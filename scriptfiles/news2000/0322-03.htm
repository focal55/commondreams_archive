Common Cause Statement on McConnell-led Campaign Finance Hearings
   WASHINGTON - March 22 - Following is a statement by Scott Harshbarger, president of Common Cause, on McConnell-led campaign finance hearings:

There's one reason Sen. Mitch McConnell is holding these hearings on campaign finance -- to provide political cover to Senators who, in the heat of an election year, know that campaign finance reform is resonating as an issue with the American people.

These hearings are rehashing what has been testified to ad nauseum in Congress. The American people should not be fooled -- Mitch McConnell hasn't had a change of heart, just a change of strategy.

The purpose of these hearings is transparent -- to create the illusion that the Republican leadership in the Senate is working for reform. But McConnell and Majority Leader Lott will use these hearings to mark up a seriously flawed campaign finance bill which would preserve the soft money loophole and significantly hike contribution limits.

The Republican leadership isn't just wrong on the merits of this issue. They know they're on the wrong side of history and the wrong side of public opinion.
