Doctors, Farmers, Environmentalists, Natural Food Store Owners, and Chefs to Demonstrate Against Genetically Engineered Foods
  WASHINGTON - July 18 - News Advisory:

Genetically Engineered Food Alert, a coalition of consumer and health advocates, will launch its nationwide campaign against genetically engineered foods with press events in 22 locations including a Safeway grocery in Washington, D.C. Activists will demonstrate outside a local Safeway to inform customers about food corporations whose products contain genetically engineered (GE) ingredients and urge these companies to remove them immediately.
