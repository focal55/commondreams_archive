WWF's MPO to Release Root Causes of Biodiversity Loss Thursday, July 6
  WASHINGTON - June 29 - News Advisory:

WWF's Macroeconomics for Sustainable Development Program Office (MPO) is releasing a new book, Root Causes of Biodiversity Loss from Earthscan Publications. The MPO's Root Causes project offers an unprecedented understanding of the driving forces contributing to biodiversity loss.

Traditional conservation has approached biodiversity loss through separate analysis of and action on specific questions of economics, environment, demographics, and politics. Root Causes, by contrast, offers a single integrated approach to analysis and action on biodiversity loss.

ABOUT THE MPO: The WWF Network has a long-standing commitment to the principle that human welfare depends on sustainable management of our natural patrimony. One expression of this commitment was the creation of the Macroeconomics for Sustainable Development Program Office, the MPO.

The MPO's objective is to analyze the environmental impact of macroeconomic practics and to promote alternative approaches that integrate environmental and social concerns into macroeconomic planning and application.
