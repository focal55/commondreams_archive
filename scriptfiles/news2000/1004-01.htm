Northern California MTBE Use Increasing
  

SAN FRANCISCO - October 4 -

WHAT:

A new government report shows that MTBE use by California refineries is increasing. Use increased most dramatically in northern California despite the fact that over 80 percent of the gasoline sold in northern California is not required by law to use oxygenates.

Bluewater Network is calling on Governor Davis to take immediate action to halt MTBE use in northern California. In addition, Bluewater is calling for legislative action to promote the best alternative to MTBE--biomass ethanol.
